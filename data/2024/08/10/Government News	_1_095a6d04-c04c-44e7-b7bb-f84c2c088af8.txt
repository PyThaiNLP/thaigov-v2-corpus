​PM believes all sides respect Constitutional Court’s ruling
August 7, 2024, at 1615hrs, at Central World, Bangkok, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin stated that he has been reported about the Constitutional Court’s verdict on the dissolution of the Move Forward Party, and expressed confidence that all concerned parties respect the Court’s ruling. He also believed that the Move Forward Party has planned for its political future.
On the Government’s peace-keeping and safety measure, the Prime Minister believed that the security agencies have been prepared and stood ready for what would ever happen. Regardless, the Government will continue to work as usual. The Constitutional Court’s verdict would not make the Government’s task easier as people’s plights remain. The Prime Minister also affirmed his readiness to undergo the inspection process.
