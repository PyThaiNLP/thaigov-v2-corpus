PM congratulates “View Kunlavut” for grabbing silver at Paris Olympic 2024
August 5, 2024, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin extended his congratulations to “View” Kunlavut Vitidsarn, Thai badminton player who won a silver medal for men's singles badminton competition at the Paris Olympic 2024. Kunlavut, World No. 8, lost to World No. 2 Viktor Axelsen from Denmark. This is the first Olympic medal for the Thai national badminton team, and the first medal for Thailand at the Paris Olympics 2024.
The Prime Minister commended Kunlavut’s success as a result of his determination, dedication, and strict discipline. He has become an inspiration to others and has boosted morale of Thai athletes in others sports who will be competing after him at the Paris Olympic 2024. The Prime Minister stressed that Kunlavut still has a bright future ahead of him, and called on the Thai people to continue cheering him on. He also thanked concerned staffs who are behind the success.
