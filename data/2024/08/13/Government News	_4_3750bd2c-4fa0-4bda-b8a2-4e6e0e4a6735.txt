Prime Minister of the Lao People’s Democratic Republic to Pay an Official Visit to Thailand
August 13, 2024, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that His Excellency Mr. Sonexay Siphandone, Prime Minister of the Lao People’s Democratic Republic (Lao PDR) is scheduled to pay an Official Visit to Thailand on 15 August 2024 at the invitation of His Excellency Mr. Srettha Thavisin, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Thailand. This will be the first Official Visit to Thailand of the Prime Minister of the Lao PDR, and an opportunity for both countries to further strengthen the bilateral cooperation in all areas.
On this occasion,  the Prime Minister of the Lao PDR  will have an audience with Their Majesties the King and Queen of Thailand.
The Prime Ministers of the Lao PDR and Thailand will have a bilateral meeting, followed by an official luncheon to be hosted at the Government House by the Prime Minister of Thailand. Their discussion will cover economic cooperation, trade, investment, energy, connectivity, and tourism, as well as human security issues such as narcotics, online scams, human trafficking and PM 2.5 pollution.
According to the government spokesperson, as a close neighboring country, the Prime Minister believes that official visit to Thailand is a great opportunity to strengthen cooperation in order to enhance relations and jointly solve problems for the benefit of both countries.
