PM signs book of blessing on occasion of Her Majesty Queen Sirikit the Queen Mother’s birthday anniversary on August 12, 2024
Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that on August 12, 2024, at 0830hrs, at the Red Room, the Grand Palace, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin and spouse Mrs. Pakpilai Thavisin presented flower vases and signed a book of blessing for Her Majesty Queen Sirikit the Queen Mother on occasion of Her Majesty’s birthday anniversary on August 12, 2024. Participating in the ceremony were Deputy Prime Ministers and spouses, and Prime Minister’s Secretary General.
The Prime Minister and the Deputy Prime Ministers presented flower vases in front of Her Majesty’s portrait. The Prime Minister and spouse, then, signed the book of blessings. They, together with the Deputy Prime Ministers and spouses, paid respect in front of Her Majesty’s portrait to complete the ceremony.
