Lao PM to pay official visit to Thailand
August 13, 2024, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Mr. Sonexay Siphandone, Prime Minister of Lao PDR, is scheduled to pay an official visit to Thailand upon an invitation of Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin. This will be the first official visit of the Lao Prime Minister, and a good opportunity to discuss cooperation reinforcement in various areas for mutual interest. The Lao Prime Minister will also be granted an audience with His Majesty the King and Her Majesty the Queen during his official visit to Thailand.
Schedule of the Lao Prime Minister’s official visit to Thailand includes a bilateral meeting with the Thai Prime Minister at the Government House. The meeting will focus on economic cooperation, trade and investment, energy cooperation, connectivity, tourism, and human security issues, such as prevention and suppression of illicit drugs, human trafficking, online scams, and transboundary haze. It will be followed by a luncheon hosted by the Thai Prime Minister.
The Government Spokesperson stressed that Lao PDR is Thailand’s close and important friend. This official visit of the Lao Prime Minister will be an opportunity for both countries to further strengthen relations and to work together in addressing current issues for mutual interest.
