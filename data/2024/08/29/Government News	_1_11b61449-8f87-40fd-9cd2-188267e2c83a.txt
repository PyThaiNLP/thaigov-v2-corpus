Deputy PM Phumtham instructs provincial commerce offices to prevent profiteering of goods as flood level decreases
August 29, 2024, Deputy Prime Minister Phumtham Wechayachai, acting Prime Minister, disclosed that he has been constantly updated on the water situation. According to the report of Disaster Prevention and Mitigation Department on overall water situation (as of 1000hrs of August 28, 2024), flood level in several areas and provinces in the North and the Northeast, i.e., Chiang Rai, Phayao, Nakhon Sawan, Loei, and Nong Bua Lamphu, started to decrease. Concerned authorities have now begun their operation to pump out inundated water, restore and clean houses and schools affected by flood disaster in Muang district of Phrae province.
With flood level starting to decrease, the Deputy Prime Minister has instructed provincial commerce offices to closely monitor prices of consumption products to prevent profiteering until the situation is normalized. Ministry of Commerce has launched Hotline: 1569, and LINE id.: @MR.DIT, to receive complaints from the people who are taken advantage of by business operators. They may also contact provincial commerce office in their province to report their plights.
