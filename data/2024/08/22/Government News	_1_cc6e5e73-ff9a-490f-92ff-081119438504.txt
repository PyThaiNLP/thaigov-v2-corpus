PM expressed profound appreciation toward Crown Prince and Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia’s congratulatory message
Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra replied via Twitter (X) to His Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, Crown Prince and Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, for HRH’s congratulatory message. Detail is as follows:
His Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud has sent a cable of congratulations to Paetongtarn Shinawatra on her appointment as the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Thailand.
HRH the Crown Prince wished the Prime Minister success, and the Government and people of the Kingdom of Thailand steady progress and prosperity.
Here’s the Thai Prime Minister’s reply:
“I am highly honored and deeply appreciative of His Royal Highness's warm congratulatory message upon my appointment as Thai Prime Minister. I am determined to enhance Thailand's partnership with Saudi Arabia in all dimensions.”
