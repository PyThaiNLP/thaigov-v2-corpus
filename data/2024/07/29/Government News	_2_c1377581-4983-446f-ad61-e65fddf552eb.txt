PM and spouse chair chanting and alms-giving ceremony
on auspicious occasion of His Majesty King Rama X’s 6th cycle birthday anniversary
July 28, 2024, at 0700hrs, at Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, Bangkok, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin and spouse, Mrs. Pakpilai Thavisin, presided over the chanting and alms-giving ceremony on auspicious occasion of His Majesty the King’s 6th cycle birthday anniversary. Attending the ceremony were Privy Councilors and spouses, the House Speaker, President of the Supreme Court, Presidents of constitutional organizations, cabinet members and spouses, Secretary General to the Prime Minister, Army commanders and spouses, Commissioner of the Royal Thai Police and spouse, heads of a government agencies (permanent secretaries or equivalent), and private sector representatives.
Upon arrival, the Prime Minister and spouse lit candle and incense sticks to pay homage to the Triple Gems, and laid the golden offerings in front of His Majesty’s portrait. Following the chanting by 10 senior monks, the Prime Minister and participants presented the monks with the triple robes and offerings. He, then, paid homage to the Triple Gems, and His Majesty’s portrait, and led the participants to give alms to 173 monks to end the ceremony.
