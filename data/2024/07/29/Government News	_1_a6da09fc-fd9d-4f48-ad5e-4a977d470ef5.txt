PM leads cabinet members and government officials to take oath of allegiance to H.M. the King
The Prime Minister presided over an oath taking ceremony on the auspicious occasion of His Majesty the King’s 6th cycle birthday anniversary on July 28, 2024. Government officials took an oath to uphold decency and be the power of the nation. Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed gist as follows:
July 28, 2024, at 0800hrs, at Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, Bangkok, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin presided over an oath taking ceremony on the auspicious occasion of His Majesty the King’s 6th cycle birthday anniversary on July 28, 2024.  Participating in the ceremony were cabinet members, high-level government officials from all public sectors and state enterprises, military and police officers, and the public.
Upon arrival at the grand stage, the Prime Minister paid homage to His Majesty the King’s portrait, and offered traditional cone-shaped floral receptacle. He, then, led the cabinet members and government officials in taking an oath to uphold decency and be the power of the nation, and sang the Royal Anthem in unison to complete the ceremony.
The oath taking ceremony will also be held in other provinces to commemorate the auspicious occasion of His Majesty the King’s 6th cycle birthday anniversary.
