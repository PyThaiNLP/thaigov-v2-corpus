Public encouraged to donate blood in honor of H.M. the King’s 6th cycle birthday anniversary
July 7, 2024, Deputy Government Spokesperson Kenika Ounjit disclosed that Office of Permanent Secretary to Prime Minister’s Office has been assigned by the Government and Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin to collaborate with the Red Cross and various public and private agencies in implementing “10,000,000 cc of Blood to Save Lives” project in honor of His Majesty the King on the auspicious occasion of His Majesty's 6th Cycle Birthday Anniversary on July 28, 2024. The project is also aimed to supply blood for the Red Cross to address the problem of blood supply shortage.
The Government would, therefore, like to invite the public with volunteering mind to donate blood under the “10,000,000 cc of Blood to Save Lives” project on July 9, 2024 at 09:00 – 15:00 at the Inner Santi Maitri Building, Government House. For residents of other provinces, blood donation activity will be held by public and private agencies across the country throughout the year (January 1 – December 31, 2024).
