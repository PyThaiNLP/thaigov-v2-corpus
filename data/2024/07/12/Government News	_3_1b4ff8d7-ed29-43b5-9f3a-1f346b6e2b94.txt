Govt aims to increase farmers’ revenue by 3 folds in 4 years
uly 11, 2024, at 1140hrs, at the Parliament, Bangkok, during the 4th Session of the 26th House of Representatives, the 2nd Year (First Annual Ordinary Session), Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin emphasized the significance of agricultural sector to the country. Although Thailand has enjoyed high profit from the industrial sector, Thai people remain dependent to the agricultural sector. The Government, particularly Minister of Agriculture and Cooperatives, has been working hard to improve the country’s agricultural structure, with an aim to increase farmers’ revenue by 3 folds in 4 years, through implementation of half-half fertilizer project and precise agriculture, and seeking new international markets, among others.
