PM: Govt ready for Opposition’s no-confidence debate
July 2, 2024, at 1116hrs, at Nakhon Ratchasima Rajabhat University, Nakhon Ratchasima province, following the 4/2024 cabinet retreat, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin disclosed about the Opposition whip’s earlier interview that the Opposition would intensively scrutinize the Government’s budget administration, and would definitely seek a no-confidence debate against the Government during the upcoming annual ordinary session of the National Assembly. The Prime Minister stated that the Government has full authority to administer budget expenditure. He understood that it is the Opposition’s rights to hold a censure debate against the Government, and he is ready for it.
