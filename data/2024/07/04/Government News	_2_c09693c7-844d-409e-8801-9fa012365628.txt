PM and Malaysian PM to take field trip to Southern border provinces on August 3
July 3, 2024, at 1530hrs, at the Internal Security Operations Command (ISOC), Bangkok, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin presided over an ISOC special meeting with concerned agencies on the Southern border situation, following a car bomb incident at the Police Flats in Yala’s Bannang Sta district.
After the meeting, the Prime Minister stated that he had an intensive talk on the matter with Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Thai Army and Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior Anutin Charnvirakul. On August 3, 2024, he and the Prime Minister of Malaysia are scheduled to take a field visit together to the Southern border provinces. However, the Prime Minister may go first if necessity arises.
