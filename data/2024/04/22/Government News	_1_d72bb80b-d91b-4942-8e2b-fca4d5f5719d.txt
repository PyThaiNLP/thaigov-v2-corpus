Govt commits to keep on suppressing hybrids scammers
April 18, 2024, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed the Government’s strong commitment to eradicate cybercrimes, which have led to the success in arresting several hybrid scam networks, and confiscating over 80 million Baht in cash. As of now, confiscated assets totals over 250 million Baht.
Such accomplishment is an integrated effort between the Cyber Police and Anti- Money Laundering Office to launch the “Purge Operation” to track down and arrest hybrid scammers who have also been linked with previous scam cases happened during 2021-2022 in the areas of Bangkok’s Din Daeng and Sala Daeng, and in Chonburi. If the confiscated money is proved illegal, it will be averaged and later returned to the victims.
The Government Spokesperson emphasized the Government’s intent to ensure security of the people and their assets. Suppression of scammers will be done in parallel with constant publicity to prevent the public from being victimized by these scammers.
