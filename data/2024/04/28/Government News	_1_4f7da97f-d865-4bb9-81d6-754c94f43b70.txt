​PM pleased with Thailand topping CEOWORLD’s list of “Best Countries to Visit in Your Lifetime”
April 27, 2024, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin expressed appreciation towards all concerned agencies and sectors for promoting Thailand to become world’s popular tourist destination, and thanked all the Thai people for being a good host to foreign tourists. A testimony is with Thailand topping the list of the best countries to visit in your lifetime (2024), a survey conducted by CEOWORLD magazine.
The rankings of 67 countries/economies are based on feedback from CEOWORLD’s 295,000 readers. Thailand tops the list with the total score of 72.15 (https://ceoworld.biz/2024/04/15/worlds-best-countries-to-visit-in-your-lifetime-2024/). According to CEOWORLD, Thailand offers a diverse range of experiences such as wild nightlife, delicious food, vibrant art and culture, great shopping options, winding rivers and canals, Buddhist temples, and night markets. It also mentioned Bang Krachao Island, located in the province of Phra Pradaeng, which offers magnificent temples, a floating market, an exclusive park, and spirit houses.
The Government Spokesperson added that the Prime Minister was confident in Thailand’s potentials. The Government has also planned to develop tourism in a comprehensive manner to promote the country as global tourism hub. CEOWORLD’s survey has proved that the Government’s endeavor is on the right track.
