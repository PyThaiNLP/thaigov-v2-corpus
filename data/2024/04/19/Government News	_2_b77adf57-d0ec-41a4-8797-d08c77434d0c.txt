​PM meets with CEO of Comité Colbert
April 18, 2024, at 1430hrs, at the Purple Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House, Ms. Bénédicte Épinay, Déléguée Générale and CEO of Comité Colbert, paid a courtesy call on Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin on occasion of her visit to Thailand upon the Prime Minister’s invitation. Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed gist of the meeting as follows:
The Prime Minister welcomed Ms. Épinay, and expressed his readiness to exchange views with her and learn from her experiences, which he hoped to benefit Thailand’s endeavor in developing and upgrading its craftsmanship and soft power. During her visit to Thailand, the Déléguée Générale and CEO of Comité Colbert had the opportunity to experience Thai handicrafts and products. She commended the potential of Thai handicraft products and was very impressed with their quality.
Both parties exchanged views on the following issues:
The two parties agreed on the potential and quality of Thai craftsmanship, and were confident that proper marketing and branding would increase the products’ commercial value, especially in the global market. The Déléguée Générale and CEO of Comité Colbert was of the view that education is fundamental, and is crucial to capacity building and incubation of young designers and entrepreneurs. Comité Colbert is ready to work together with Thailand in developing relevant ecosystem to lay foundation for the development of new generation of designers and entrepreneurs in a sustainable manner.
The Prime Minister expressed hope that France’s Comité Colbert would be able to share their know-how and experiences with Thailand in order for the country to enhance potential and quality of its craftmanship and to gain global recognition of its handicraft products. As Thailand is France’s important market, Ms. Épinay added that the year 2025 would mark the 340th anniversary of relations between the two countries, and this would be a good opportunity to organize relevant activities to celebrate the occasion. Based on the joint statement between President Macron and the Prime Minister, issued during the latter’s visit to France, the Déléguée Générale and CEO of Comité Colbert was confident that the French President would be content with the outcome of the meeting today.
******************************
“Comité Colbert” is an independent association to promote and protect the benefits of France’s luxury products, covering both the dimensions of enhancing economic benefits of craftmanship products and marketing, and protecting business benefits of French products. The members are 81 French luxury brands and 6 other European luxury brands.
