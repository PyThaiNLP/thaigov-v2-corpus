Prime Minister of the People's Republic of Bangladesh, H.E. Sheikh Hasina pays Official Visit to the Kingdom of Thailand of between 24 - 29 April 2024
Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Her Excellency Sheikh Hasina, Prime Minister of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh will pay an official visit to Thailand, at the invitation of His Excellency Mr. Srettha Thavisin, Prime Minister, between 24 - 29 April 2024.
During the visit, the Thai Prime Minister and Prime Minister of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh will witness the signing of various Agreements and Memoranda of Understanding, and Joint Press Conference. The Prime Minister will also host a lunch in honour of the Prime Minister of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh and her delegation at the Government House.
This visit will be the first visit to Thailand of the Prime Minister of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh since 2002. It will be an opportunity for both sides to meet and discuss ways to strengthen bilateral relations and expand cooperation in multiple areas, especially the commencement of Free Trade Agreement negotiations which will help to expand trade and increase export opportunities. Thailand and Bangladesh will also explore possibilites to deepen collaboration in development cooperation, connectivity, tourism, and people-to-people ties.
