​Govt pleased with success of Maha Songkran World Water Festival 2024
April 16, 2024, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed about the immense success of Thailand’s Maha Songkran World Water Festival 2024, which was held during April 11-15, 2024 at the area around Ratchadamnoen Klang Avenue and Sanam Luang Royal Plaza. According to the Tourism Authority of Thailand (TAT), the number of both Thai and foreign tourists totaled 784,883 individuals, with the total sales of goods, food and beverages at the event at 9,334,335 Baht, generating 2,886.82 million Baht for the Thai economy. TAT also reported that both Thai and foreign tourists expressed greatest satisfaction over the event.
The Government commended the success of the Maha Songkran World Water Festival 2024, and would like to thank relevant agencies and sectors for organizing the event. The Government Spokesperson also disclosed the Government’s appreciation towards all the Thai people for being a good host, which he hoped to charm foreign tourists and draw them back to Thailand to celebrate Songkran in the years to come.
