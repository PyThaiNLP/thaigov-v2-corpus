Cabinet approves in principle constitutional referendum
April 23, 2024, at 1130hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet approved in principle a study on the constitutional referendum, according to which three referendums will be conducted to let voters express their opinion whether there is a need to draft a new Constitution. The Referendum Act, B.E. 2564 will also be amended to ensure that the law is an efficient democratic tool for the public to directly express their needs and opinions.
