Bangladeshi PM to pay official visit to Thailand during April 24-29
April 24, 2024, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that H.E. Sheikh Hasina, Prime Minister of People’s Republic of Bangladesh, will pay an official visit to Thailand, at the invitation of Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin during 24 - 29 April 2024. This visit, which reflects the confidence of the Bangladeshi Government towards Thailand and the Thai Government, will be the first visit to the country of the Prime Minister of Bangladesh since 2002.
On April 26, 2024, the Bangladeshi Prime Minister will attend an official welcoming ceremony at the Government House, followed by a four-eye meeting with the Thai Prime Minister and a plenary meeting. The Thai Prime Minister and Prime Minister of Bangladesh will witness the signing of 5 Agreements and Memorandum of Understanding, and co-chair the joint press conference. The Prime Minister will also host a luncheon in honor of the Prime Minister of Bangladesh and her delegation at Santi Maitri Building, Government House.
According to the Government Spokesperson, Bangladesh is Thailand’s important trade partner in the South Asian region. This visit of the Bangladeshi Prime Minister will be a good opportunity for both sides to meet and discuss ways to strengthen bilateral relations and expand cooperation in multiple areas, especially trade and investment promotion, and the commencement of Free Trade Agreement negotiations, as well as the cooperation in clean energy, customs, and sustainable tourism development.
