​PM happy with Q3/2024 GDP expansion
November 19, 2024, at 1210hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed about the report of Office of the National Economic and Social Development Council (NESDC) on “Thai Economic Performance in Q3 of 2024 and the Outlook for 2024 – 2025”, according to which the GDP has expanded by 3.0% (YoY), whereas in the first 9 months of 2024, the Thai economy grew by 2.3%. Economy in 2024 is projected to expand by 2.6%, for which key supporting factors include the increase in government consumption and investment, and tourism. Private investment, however, is lagged behind.
The Prime Minister has instructed Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Commerce, NESDC, and other concerned agencies to come up with measures to promote private investment in a bid for GDP to grow further.
