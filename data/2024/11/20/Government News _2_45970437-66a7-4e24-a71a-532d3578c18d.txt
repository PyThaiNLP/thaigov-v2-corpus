PM congratulates President-elect Donald Trump on election victory
November 19, 2024, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that at 0950hrs, Prime Miniter Paetongtarn Shinawatra had a phone conversation with President-elect Donald Trump to congratulate him on the election victory. The Prime Minister took the occasion to affirm Thailand’s commitment to continue working closely together with the U.S., and hoped for the exchange of high-level visits. Thailand is ready to welcome the President-elect to the country.
President-elect Donald Trump thanked the Prime Minister for her congratulations, and commended Thailand’s beauty. He also asked about the country’s current situation, and praised the Prime Minister’s work commitment, on which the President-elect also affirmed his support.
