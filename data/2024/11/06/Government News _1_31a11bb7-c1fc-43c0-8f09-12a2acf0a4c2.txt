PM’s schedule for 8th GMS Summit of Leaders and 10th ACMECS Summit
November 6, 2024, at 1100hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra will lead the delegation, which includes Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior Anutin Charnvirakul and Minister of Foreign Affairs Maris Sangiampongsa, to travel to Kunming, People’s Republic of China to attend the 8th Greater Mekong Subregion Economic Cooperation Program (GMS) Summit and the 10th Ayeyawady-Chao Phraya-Mekong Economic Cooperation Strategy (ACMECS) Summit during November 6-7, 2024. Her schedule is as follows:
November 6, 2024, at approx. 1100hrs, the Prime Minister and delegation depart from the Royal Thai Air Force Airport (Wing 6) for Kunming and, later, attend a welcome dinner.
In the morning of November 7, the Prime Minister is scheduled to attend the Leaders’ Retreat and a plenary of the 8th Greater Mekong Subregion Economic Cooperation Program (GMS) Summit. In the afternoon, she will attend the 10th Ayeyawady-Chao Phraya-Mekong Economic Cooperation Strategy (ACMECS) Summit before traveling back to Bangkok in the evening of the same day.
