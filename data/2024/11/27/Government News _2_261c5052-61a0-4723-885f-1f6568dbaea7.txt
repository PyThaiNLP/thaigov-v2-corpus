Singaporean PM to pay official visit to Thailand on Nov 28
Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that H.E Mr. Lawrence Wong, Prime Minister of the Republic of Singapore, is scheduled to pay an official visit to Thailand on November 28, 2024, as guest of the Thai Government. His official visit is upon an invitation of Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra. This is also his first official visit to Thailand as the Prime Minister of Singapore following his assumption of the position in May 2024.
Upon his arrival at the Government House, the Singaporean Prime Minister will attend the official welcoming ceremony, meet with the Thai Prime Minister, and witness the signing of MOUs and Agreements between various agencies of the two countries. The two Prime Ministers will, then, hold a joint press conference, before attending the luncheon hosted by Prime Minister Paetongtarn in honor of the Singaporean Prime Minister.
According to the Government Spokesperson, Thailand and Singapore will celebrate the 60th anniversary of diplomatic relations in 2025. Bilateral meeting between the two leaders is aimed to further strengthen relations and cooperation in various areas, e.g., exchange of visits, security and economic partnership, people-to-people relations, and strategic cooperation. The two leaders will also discuss cooperation promotion in new areas for mutual interests, i.e., cyber security, digital economy, green economy, and investment in future industries.
