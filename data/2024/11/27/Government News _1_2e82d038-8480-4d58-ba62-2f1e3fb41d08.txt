PM appreciates donation for flood-affected victims
November 26, 2024, at 1200hrs, at the Purple Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra received the donation, at the amount of 1 million Baht, from president of the Stock Exchange of Thailand (SET) and FETCO Director and Board Secretary Asadej Kongsiri for the relief and assistance to disaster-affected victims. Attending the presentation of donation were Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Phumtham Wechayachai, Minister Attached to the Prime Minister’s Office Chousak Sirinil, Minister of Commerce Pichai Naripthaphan, and Deputy Minister of Interior Theerarat Samrejvanich. Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed gist as follows:
The Prime Minister thanked the private sector and organizations for their good intention to help flood-affected victims, and added that she and members of the cabinet are scheduled to visit Chiang Rai at the end of this week. There, the Prime Minister will hand down related policy directives for flood prevention, and will use the donation money to help affected victims according to the donor’s intention. She also expressed appreciation to the donor on behalf of the victims.
