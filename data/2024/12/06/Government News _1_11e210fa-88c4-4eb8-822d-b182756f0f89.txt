PM and spouse present floral offerings on occasion of King Rama IX’s birthday anniversary,
Thai National Day, and Father’s Day on December 5, 2024
On December 5, 2024, at 08:30 hrs, at King Rama IX Memorial Park, Dusit District, Bangkok, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and her spouse, Mr. Pidok Sooksawas, presided over a ceremony to present floral offerings and pay homage to H.M. King Bhumibol Adulyadej The Great on the occasion of His Majesty’s birthday anniversary, Thai National Day, and Father’s Day. Cabinet members and their spouses also participated in the ceremony. Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub provided the following details:
Upon arriving at King Rama IX Memorial Park, the Prime Minister paid homage in front of H.M. King Bhumibol Adulyadej The Great’s statue and offered two traditional cone-shaped floral receptacles—one for herself and the other on behalf of the cabinet members. The Prime Minister’s spouse presented a floral receptacle on behalf of the cabinet members' spouses, completing the ceremony.
