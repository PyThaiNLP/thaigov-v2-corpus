PM chairs alms-giving ceremony on occasion of His Majesty late King Bhumibol’s birthday anniversary
December 5, 2024, at 0730hrs, at Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, Bangkok, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and Mr. Pidok Sooksawas, spouse presided over the Buddhist rites and alms-giving ceremony on occasion of the anniversary of His Majesty late King Bhumibol’s birthday anniversary. Participated in the ceremony were privy councilors and spouses, the House Speaker, President of the Supreme Court, President of the Senate, presidents of independent organizations under constitution, deputy prime ministers and spouses, representatives of royal offices, Prime Minister’s Secretary General, Governor of Bangkok, commanders of military forces and spouses, commissioner-general of the Royal Thai Police and spouse, permanent secretary-level officials, Permanent Secretary of the Bangkok Metropolitan Administration, and representatives of private sector networks. Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed gist as follows:
Upon arrival at the Outer Santi Maitri Building, the Prime Minister and spouse lit candle and incense sticks to pay homage to the Triple Gems, and laid the golden offerings in front of His Majesty’s portrait. Following the chanting by 10 senior monks, she and the participants presented the monks with the triple robes and offerings. She, then, paid homage to the Triple Gems, and His Majesty’s portrait, and led the participants to give alms to 189 monks at Sanam Luang Royal Plaza to end the ceremony.
