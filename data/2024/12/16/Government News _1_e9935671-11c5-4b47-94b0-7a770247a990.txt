PM arrives in Malaysia to commence official visit
December 15, 2024, at 1700hrs (local time), Deputy Government spokesperson Sasikarn Wattanachan disclosed that Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and the delegation arrived at the Kuala Lumpur International Airport to commence her official visit to Malaysia during December 15-16, 2024. Upon arrival, the Prime Minister reviewed the Static Guard of Honour. She was welcomed by Minister in the Prime Minister's Department (Federal Territories) of Malaysia Dr. Zaliha Mustafa, as the Minister-in-attendance, and was given a flower bouquet by a Malaysian girl.
