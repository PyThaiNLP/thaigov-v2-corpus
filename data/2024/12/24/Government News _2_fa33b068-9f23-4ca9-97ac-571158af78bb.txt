PM: GDP in 2025 expected to exceed 3%
PM: GDP in 2025 expected to exceed 3%
December 24, 2024, at 1240hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra gave an interview about the GDP and economic outlook in 2025 that the Government has worked on the economic forecast for each quarter, and would try its best to push for economic advancement of each quarter to achieve the target for steady growth of the Thai economy Overall, it is expected that the GDP grows more than 3% in 2025.
