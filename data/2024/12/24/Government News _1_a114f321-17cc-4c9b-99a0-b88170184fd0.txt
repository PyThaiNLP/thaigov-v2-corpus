​Dr. Nalinee Taveesin Appointed President of Thailand Trade Representatives
Dr. Nalinee Taveesin has been officially appointed as the President of Thailand Trade Representatives, reaffirming the Thai government’s commitment to fostering robust international trade partnerships and driving sustainable economic growth.
As President of Thailand Trade Representatives, Dr. Taveesin will play a pivotal role in promoting Thailand as a global trading hub and a preferred destination for foreign investment. Her leadership will focus on strengthening bilateral trade relations, facilitating investment opportunities, and ensuring that Thailand remains a competitive and attractive partner on the global stage.
Dr. Taveesin has pledged to follow through on Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra’s vision for 2025 as a year of “tangible, practicable possibilities and hope” for Thai citizens. She emphasized her dedication to translating the Prime Minister’s policy into actionable strategies that will create fruitful opportunities for both Thai businesses and international partners.
“I am deeply honored to serve as the President of Thailand Trade Representatives,” said Dr. Taveesin. “In this role, I will work tirelessly to align our trade initiatives with the Prime Minister’s vision, strengthening Thailand’s position in the global marketplace, enhancing trade relations, and fostering mutually beneficial opportunities for Thailand and our international partners.”
