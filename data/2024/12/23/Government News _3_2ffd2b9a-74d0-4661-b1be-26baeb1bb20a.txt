​PM conveys New Year wishes to all Thai people
December 23, 2024, at 1000hrs, at the Government House, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra gave a New Year's greetings to wish for all the Thai people to have a bright and cheerful mind and positive energy, and to be happy with small and simple things. Next year has been announced as the year of opportunity, and the Government would do its best to ensure that everyone’s opportunity is opened up. The Prime Minister also wished everyone to be healthy, travel safely, have fun during the New Year holidays, and return to work with physical and mental strength.
