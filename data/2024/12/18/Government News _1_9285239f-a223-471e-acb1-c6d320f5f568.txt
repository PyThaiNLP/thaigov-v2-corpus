​PM expects tangible progress of projects under Thailand-Malaysia cooperation by Feb 2025
December 17, 2024, at 1000hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed the Prime Minister’s briefing and instruction made at the weekly cabinet meeting.
The Prime Minister informed the cabinet of her successful official visit to Malaysia on December 15-16, 2024, during which border development, and the development at both national and ASEAN levels have been discussed and agreed upon.
Additionally, both Thailand and Malaysia came to terms to expedite the implementation of various projects that have been initiated between each other, i.e., infrastructure development and regional logistics networking and connectivity. This is especially international customs procedures from Malaysia, Thailand, and the Lao People's Democratic Republic to China and other countries in the region, which will benefit Thailand’s international trade and transport.
According to the Prime Minister, Kolok River dredging project, and construction of the 2nd Sungai Kolok Bridge in Narathiwat and CIQ in Yala’s Sadao district will also be expedited. The two parties also discussed an Agreement on Lao PDR-Thailand-Malaysia-Singapore Power Integration Project, which will benefit people of the 4 countries. The Prime Minister instructed concerned agencies to accelerate all related implementations, and update the cabinet with tangible progress of each project by February 2025 to ensure the best interest of the nation and people.
