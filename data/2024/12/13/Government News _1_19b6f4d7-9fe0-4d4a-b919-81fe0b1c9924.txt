PM to pay official visit to Malaysia during Dec 15-16
December 13, 2024, Deputy Government Spokesperson Sasikarn Watthanachan disclosed that Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra is scheduled to pay an official visit to Malaysia during December 15-16, 2024.
The Prime Minister and delegation, comprising Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Transport Suriya Jungrungreangkit, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior Anutin Charnvirakul, Minister of Foreign Affairs Maris Sangiampongsa, Minister of Tourism and Sports Sorawong Thienthong, Minister of Agriculture and Cooperatives Narumon Pinyosinwat, Minister of Culture Sudawan Wangsuphakijkosol, and Prime Minister’s Secretary General Prommin Lertsuridej, will attend the 7th Annual Consultation to affirm the shared goal on “Common Peace and Prosperity” for better quality of life of people of the two countries. The Prime Minister will also push forward cooperation in all areas toward tangible progress, especially, infrastructure development and promotion of trade and economic cooperation to achieve the target of US$30 billion in trade by 2027.
The Prime Minister and delegation will leave the Royal Thai Air Force Airport (Wing 6) on December 15, 2024 at approx. 1400hrs. On December 16, at 0900hrs, she is scheduled to attend the official welcoming ceremony at the Perdana Putra (Prime Minister’s Office) before participating in the 7th Annual Consultation at 1000hrs. After the meeting, the Prime Minister will witness the signing of Agreement/MoU between the two countries, and attend the joint press statement. The Prime Minister of Malaysia will later host a luncheon in honor of the Prime Minister at 1140hrs.
The Prime Minister is scheduled to return to Thailand on the same day (December 16) at approx. 1630hrs. Detail and photos/clips of her missions during the official visit to Malaysia will later be released to the public.
