Cabinet approves debt-relief program to alleviate financial plight of farmers
December 11, 2024, at 1240hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that the cabinet approved the proposal of Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives and Ministry of Finance on the implementation of debt-relief program for farmers, and members of the Farmer's Reconstruction and Development Fund who are debtors of 4 banks. So far, 16,794 members have expressed their intention to participate in the program. More can also participate, but only to the extent of the program’s budget framework of 15,481.66 million Baht as earlier approved by the cabinet.
Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Agriculture also reported about the cabinet’s resolution, made on December 3, 2024, on the promotion scheme for rice farming production management and quality enhancement during 2024/2025 harvesting year to alleviate the plights of farmers and reduce production cost, under which the target group of approx. 4.3 million rice farmers throughout the country will be provided with the subsidy of 1,000 Baht per rai (but not more than 10 rai per household). Budget framework approved for the scheme is 35 billion Baht. The Government will begin distributing the subsidized money on December 16. The distribution period is for 5 days per region.
