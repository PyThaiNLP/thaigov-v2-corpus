PM commends military personnel working to alleviate people’s plights at flood-affected areas
September 21, 2024, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra commended military personnel who have been dispatched to flood-affected areas to provide relief to the victims.
The Prime Minister was reported on the tasks accomplished by the military personnel in flood-affected Chiang Rai province, especially repair of electrical equipment in front of Mae Sai Bridge border checkpoint 1 (by Military Technical Training School); and rehabilitation operation at Ban Muang Daeng community (by the Armed Forces Development Command’s Regional Development Office 3 and Mobile Development Unit 34), Koh Sai Rim Nam Road (by Mobile Development Unit 35, in collaboration with the Department of Disaster Prevention and Mitigation), and at Ban Koh Sai community (by Mobile Development Unit 31, 32, and 33).
The Prime Minister thanked the military and offered her moral support to all the military personnel working in the front lines to help people and alleviate their plights in a bid to bring back normalcy. She also affirmed that the Government, as well as all concerned agencies, is always there for the people and stand ready to help them out at the best of its ability.
