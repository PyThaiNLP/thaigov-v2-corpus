PM: Amendment of Constitution on sections related to ethical standards is parliamentary matter
September 17, 2024, at 1150hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra stated to the press about an amendment of the Constitution on sections related to ethical standards of the politicians that law amendment is the matter discussed at the Parliament.
With regard to the annual military reshuffle, the Prime Minister has not been reported or signed any document. Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Phumtham Wechayachai is looking into this matter.
