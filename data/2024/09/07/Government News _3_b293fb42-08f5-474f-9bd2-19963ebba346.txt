PM and new cabinet take group photo before having special session of the cabinet meeting
September 7, 2023, at 0915hrs, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and members of the new cabinet take a group photo at the lawn in front of Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House before having a special session of the cabinet meeting.
The cabinet meeting (special session) is held to approved the Government's policy statement, and the dates when the policy statement will be delivered to the Parliament.
