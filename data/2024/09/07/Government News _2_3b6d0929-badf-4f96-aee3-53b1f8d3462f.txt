PM and cabinet members prepare to take oath of allegiance
September 6, 2023, at 1500hrs, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and members of the new cabinet arrived at Santi Maitri Building, Government House to take photo before traveling to Amphorn Sathan Residential Hall at 1815hrs to take the oath of allegiance prior to their assumption of position at 1400hrs.
On September 7, the Prime Minister and the new cabinet will, then, take a group photo at the lawn in front of Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House before having a special session of the cabinet meeting.
