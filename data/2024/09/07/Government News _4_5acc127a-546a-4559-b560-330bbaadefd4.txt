PM calls on cabinet members to heed
and follow His Majesty the King's sayings and teachings in their duty performance
September 7, 2024, at 1000hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra chaired a special session of the cabinet meeting. Gist is as follows:
The Prime Minister congratulated all the cabinet members for being royally appointed, and called on everyone to heed and follow His Majesty the King's sayings and teachings in their performance of duty. She also handed down the following policies:
1. Relevant ministers may proceed with an appointment of permanent secretary-level civil servants to replace those who are about to retire and those who have completed 4 year-term in respective position. Proposals can be submitted to the cabinet for consideration after the delivery of policy statement at the Parliament.
2. Members of the cabinet are urged to prepare their defense of relevant policy statement, and to work together in communicating with their subordinates and the public to ensure their understanding and awareness of the policies
3. Members of the cabinet are urged to continue the works of former Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin, especially economic stimulation measures, i.e., tourism promotion, assistance to the vulnerabilities and farmers, flood solution and relief, and acceleration of public investment expenditure, among others
