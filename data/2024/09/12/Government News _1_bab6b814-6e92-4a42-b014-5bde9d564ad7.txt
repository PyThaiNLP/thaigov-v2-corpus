PM affirms continuation of Digital Wallet scheme to alleviate people’s financial burden
September 12, 2024, at 0959hrs, at the delivery of policy statements of the Council of Ministers to the Parliament, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that the Government would continue to implement short-term economic stimulus measures, and affirmed that the Digital Wallet scheme would truly benefit the people, especially the vulnerability groups who will first receive the subsidy to alleviate their financial burdens and increase earning opportunities. The Digital Wallet scheme will also be a foundation to promoting digital economy.
According to the Prime Minister, such policies as the Digital Wallet scheme, development of government data center, and increase of access to funding sources for village and community development and occupation are parts of the Government’s plan on maintaining the nation’s economic stability. Fiscal policy will be administered through efficient and proper budget spending under strict fiscal discipline to ensure public’s utmost interest.
