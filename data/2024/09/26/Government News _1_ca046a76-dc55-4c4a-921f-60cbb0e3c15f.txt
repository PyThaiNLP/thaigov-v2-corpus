PM’s advisor: Concerned agencies instructed to create public understanding on rights and benefits of Marriage Equality bill
September 25, 2024, Prime Minister’s Advisor Jirayu Huangsap stated about the Royal Gazette announcement of the revised Civil and Commercial Code Amendment Act (No. 24) B.E. 2567, resulting in the Marriage Equality Act coming into effect 120 days later on January 22, 2568.
From now until the day the bill takes effect, concerned public agencies are instructed to conduct publicity on related criminal and civil laws to create public awareness and understanding on various rights and benefits, especially the rights to manage spouse’s properties, to adopt children, and to sign consent for spouse’s medical treatment.
Marriage Equality Act is one of the accomplishments of the Government to promote people equality and equity. The Prime Minister’s advisor affirmed that the Government would continue to push forward implementations that ensure equality in the society.
