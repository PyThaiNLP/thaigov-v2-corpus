PM: Ministry of Finance to discuss with BOT on Baht appreciation
September 24, 2024, at 1210hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra stated about the recent Baht appreciation, which has impacted goods export and caused concern of all other sectors, that the Government would leverage the advantageous aspects of Baht appreciation, while addressing the problem, on which the Ministry of Finance is assigned to talk with the Bank of Thailand (BOT).
Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Pichai Chunhavajira disclosed that he was scheduled to meet with BOT Governor Sethaput Suthiwartnarueput next week to discuss about recent appreciation of Thai Baht and the inflation target range.
