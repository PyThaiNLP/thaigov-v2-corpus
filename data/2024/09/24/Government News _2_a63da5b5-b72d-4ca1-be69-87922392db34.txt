PM: Government’s delivery of policy statement to Parliament was carried out in accordance with law
September 24, 2024, at 1300hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra affirmed that the Government’s delivery of policy statement to the Parliament was carried out strictly in accordance with the law, following an anonymous complaint filed with the Ombudsman on the incompletion of the Government's policy statement delivered earlier to the Parliament, risking the entire cabinet being removed. She stressed that who would not be afraid of the laws.
