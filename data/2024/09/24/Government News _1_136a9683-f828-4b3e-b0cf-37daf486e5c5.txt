PM thanks retired officials for their dedication and work for the country
September 23, 2024, at 1300hrs, at the Purple Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House, Prime Minister’s Secretary-General Prommin Lertsuridej led 17 officials from Office of the Prime Minister’s Secretary-General who will retire on September 30, 2024 to pay a courtesy call on Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra. Participating in the meeting were Deputy Secretary-Generals Natthriya Thaweevong and Chanida Kasemsook, and executives of the Office of Prime Minister’s Secretary-General.
The Prime Minister commended and thanked the retired officials for their dedication. With their work experiences of over 20 years (one of them has worked for 36 years), the retired officials and their family should take pride in having served the country and people with perseverance, diligence, and integrity which is not an easy job to do. She called on them to pass on knowledge and work experiences, or share good stories to the next generation of civil servants, and wished them a happy life and healthiness.
