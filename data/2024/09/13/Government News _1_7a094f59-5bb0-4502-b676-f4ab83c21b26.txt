​PM to take field visit to Chiang Rai today
September 13, 2024, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra is scheduled for a field visit to Chiang Rai to follow up on flood situation in the province. Her schedule is as follows:
At 0845hrs, the Prime Minister and delegation will leave from the Royal Thai Air Forces Airport (Wing 6) for Chiang Rai’s Mae Fah Luang Airport. She will, then, listen to the briefing and update of the situation.
At 1130hrs, the Prime Minister and delegation will travel to Mae Sai district office, where the province’s flood incident command center is situated, to closely monitor flood situation and damages, and follow up on assistance and relief provided to affected victims.
At approx. 1345hrs, the Prime Minister and delegation will travel to Chiang Rai Rajabhat University to give moral support and the relief bags to flood-affected victims.
The Prime Minister will also take this opportunity to hear from local authorities and residents on what they really need so that the Government would provide help and supplies accordingly. She will also instruct concerned agencies to closely monitor water situation in other areas, and will express her appreciation toward local government officials, local authorities, military, volunteers, and all the Thai people to join forces with the Government in helping out Chiang Rai this time.
