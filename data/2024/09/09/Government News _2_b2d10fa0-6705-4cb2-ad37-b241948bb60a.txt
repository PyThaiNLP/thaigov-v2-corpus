Prommin Lertsuridej appointed as PM’s Secretary General
September 7, 2024, at 1000hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, the cabinet, in
its special session chaired by Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra, approved an appointment
of Mr. Prommin Lertsuridej as Prime Minister’s Secretary General, which will be in effect from
September 7, 2024 onward.
