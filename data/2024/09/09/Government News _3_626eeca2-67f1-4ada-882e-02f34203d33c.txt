PM commends success of Thai athletes at “Paralympic Games Paris 2024”
September 9, 2024, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra posted on her Facebook to commend the success of Thai athletes who participated in the 17th Summer Paralympic Games, hosted by France during August 28 – September 8, 2024. The Prime Minister’s message is as follows:
“It’s time to welcome back Paralympic athletes, Thai heroes. This year, the Thai Paralympic team made history for Thailand with 6 gold medals, 11 silver medals, and 13 bronze medals, ranking 21st in the medal table. They have brought great happiness and pride for all of us Thais. I would like to thank all concerned individuals for working hard on behalf of the country, and would like to congratulate all the athletes for your hard-earned success. I believe that today's success will be a huge inspiration for the next generation of athletes."
