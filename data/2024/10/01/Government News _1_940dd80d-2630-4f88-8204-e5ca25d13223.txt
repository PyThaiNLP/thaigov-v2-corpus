PM: Real-time update and monitoring of water level in Ping River is important
September 28, 2024, at 1400hrs, at P1 water station at the Nawarat Bridge, Chiang Mai, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra followed up on flood situation in the province. She was welcomed by Chiang Mai governor and local residents. Prime Minister’s advisor Jirayu Huangsap disclosed gist as follows:
The Prime Minister listened to the briefing on real-time flood situation, and commended real-time update of concerned agencies which significantly helps alleviate people’s worriedness. She also instructed Ministry of Public Health to take care of mental health of flood-affected victims.
The Prime Minister added that she would later invite Chiang Mai Governor to meet and discuss about overall water management model, which includes relief and assistance. She, then, took photos with local officials and people.
