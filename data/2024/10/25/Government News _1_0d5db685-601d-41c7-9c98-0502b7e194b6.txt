PM leads cabinet members to lay wreath in honor of king Rama V
October 23, 2024, at 0830hrs, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra led members of the cabinet to take part in wreath laying ceremony and paid respect to King Chulalonglongkorn or Phra Piya Maharaj (King Rama V) at the King Rama V Monument to commemorate King Chulalongkorn Day. Also participating in the ceremony was Secretary General to the Prime Minister Prommin Lertsuridej.
The Prime Minister, together with members of the cabinet and secretary general to the Prime Minister, paid respect to King Chulalongkorn’s monument Monument, and let 2 wreaths for herself and on behalf of the cabinet.
King Chulalongkorn Day or Piya Maharaj Day is commemorated every year on October 23 in honor of King Chulalongkorn whose passing falls on this date.
