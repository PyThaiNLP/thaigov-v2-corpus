PM: ICON Group scam probing is underway
PM: ICON Group scam probing is underway
October 22, 2024, at 1200hrs, at the Command Building, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that the case of ICON Group scam, which involves public officials, is being investigated based on available evidences by the Royal Thai Police led by Commissioner-General Pol. Gen. Kitrat Panphet. She has already instructed concerned authorities to immediately notify the public of additional information, if any.
With regard to public official involvement in the case, the Prime Minister stated that the names are yet to be identified, but assured that there would not be fact distortion or concealment. Those who are proved to commit crime in the ICON Group scam case will all be prosecuted.
