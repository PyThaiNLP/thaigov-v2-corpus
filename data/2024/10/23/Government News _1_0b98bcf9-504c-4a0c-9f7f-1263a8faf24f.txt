PM: Flood, Storm, and Landslide Disaster Relief Operations Center to return flood disaster-hit area to Chiang Rai district office and municipality by Oct 27
October 22, 2024, at 1200hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that the cabinet has followed up on the operation of the Flood, Storm, and Landslide Disaster Relief Operations Center in Chiang Rai province, and was reported on the Center’s plan to hand back the authority over the disaster-hit area to Chiang Rai district office and municipality by October 27. Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Phumtham Wechayachai will supervise the handover of the disaster-hit area. Deputy Minister of Interior Theerarat Samrejvanich and Deputy Minister of Defense Gen. Nattapol Nakpanich, who remain in the field, also reported to the cabinet via Zoom on other related operation progress.
The Prime Minister added about the economic stimulus scheme through handing out 10,000 Baht to the disabled and disadvantaged people that the 2nd round of money distribution has been carried out for those who were missed out in the first round. There are still 2 more rounds of money handout to the rest on November 21, and December 21, 2024.
