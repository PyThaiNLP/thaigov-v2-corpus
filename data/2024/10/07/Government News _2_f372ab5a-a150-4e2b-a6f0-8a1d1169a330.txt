PM signs book of blessing for HRH Princess Chulabhorn’s speedy recovery
October 4, 2024, at 1545hrs, at Chulabhorn Hospital, Bangkok, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra, together with Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Phumtham Wechayachai, and Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Transport Suriya Jungrungreangkit, offered flower vases and signed a book of blessing, on behalf of the Prime Minister and members of the cabinet, for Her Royal Highness Princess Chulabhorn Krom Phra Srisavangavadhana’s speedy recovery.
