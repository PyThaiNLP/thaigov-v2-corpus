PM to attend 44th and 45th ASEAN Summit in Lao PDR
Prime Minister’s advisor Jirayu Houngsab disclosed that Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and the delegation is scheduled to attend the 44th and 45th ASEAN summits and related summits in Vientiane, Lao PDR on October 9-11, 2024, as invited by Prime Minister Sonexay Siphandone of the Lao People's Democratic Republic (Lao PDR). This will be the first official overseas visit of the Prime Minister as leader of ASEAN member state.
The Prime Minister will leave from Thailand to arrive in Lao PDR on October 8, and meet with Prime Minister Sonexay Siphandone and President of the National Assembly of the Lao People's Democratic Republic Saysomphone Phomvihane later in the day. She will also pay a courtesy call on Laos President Thongloun Sisoulith. Promotion of strategic partnership for growth and sustainable development between the two countries will be mainly discussed, especially water management in Mekong River, transport connectivity, tourism cooperation, and cooperation in solving narcotic problem, online fraud and scam, human trafficking, and cross-border haze.
During October 9-11, the Prime Minister will attend the 44th and 45th ASEAN summits and related summits, which are hosted by Lao PDR as chair of ASEAN in 2024, under the overarching theme of “ASEAN: Enhancing Connectivity and Resilience”.
According to the Prime Minister’s advisor, the Prime Minister will also attend meetings between ASEAN leaders and dialogue partners (i.e., China, Republic of Korea, Japan, Australia, India, U.S.A., and Canada), and ASEAN leaders with high-level representatives from international organizations (i.e., United Nations) to discuss issues of mutual interest, such as strengthening the ASEAN Community, promoting cooperation between ASEAN and external partners, and regional and international situations.
The Prime Minister will also meet with representatives of the ASEAN Inter-Parliamentary Assembly, ASEAN Youth, and the ASEAN Business Advisory Council, and attend the 2nd Asia Zero Emission Community Leaders' Meeting, as well as has bilateral meetings with leaders of participating nations.
