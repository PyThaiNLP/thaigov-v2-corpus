PM: Compensation for flood-affected victims in Ayutthaya will be disbursed by end of the year
October 29, 2024, at 1210hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that Flood, Storm, and Landslide Disaster Relief Operations Center has already been informed about the delay of compensation to be paid to flood-affected victims in Ayutthaya, and affirmed that the compensation will be disbursed very soon as concerned agencies are rushing in screening the eligibility. As for the matter of when, she also affirmed that disbursement would be done before the end of this year.
