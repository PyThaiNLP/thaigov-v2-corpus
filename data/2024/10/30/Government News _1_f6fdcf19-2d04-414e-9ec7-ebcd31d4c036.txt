PM orders preparation against PM 2.5 as Thailand enters cool season
October 29, 2024, at 1210hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that she has instructed Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Digital Economy and Society Prasert Jantararuangtong and Minister of Natural Resources and Environment Chalermchai Sri-on to work together with concerned agencies in preparing, preventing, and responding against the effect of PM 2.5 particulates, as Thailand is entering the cool season. Prevention should be comprehensively undertaken, from implementation of agricultural measures (e.g., banning import of maize and sugar cane that are grown by stubble burning) to reduction of PM emissions from vehicles and factories, which will need the cooperation of Ministry of Transport and Ministry of Industry, respectively.
