PM lays wreath on occasion of “Navamindra Maharaj Day”
October 13, 2024, at 0830hrs, at King Rama IX Memorial Park, Bangkok, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra chaired the wreath laying ceremony, and paid respect to the monument of His Majesty late King Bhumibol on occasion of the Navamindra Maharaj Day, the anniversary of His Majesty late King Bhumibol’s passing.
The Prime Minister laid 2 wreaths for herself and on behalf of the cabinet, and bowed to end the ceremony.
