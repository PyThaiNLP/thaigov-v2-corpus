Govt thanks people for their confidence
October 27, 2024, at 1530hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that the Government would like to thank the public for their response to NIDA (National Institute of Development Administration) Poll’s survey on "Will Prime Minister Ung Ing's Government survive?", which sought sample public opinion on whether the incumbent Government would last until the end of its four-year term. According to the survey result, most of the responders are confident that the Government would be able to end its term.
The Government Spokesperson, on behalf of the Government, thanked the public for their confidence on the Government’s stability, and affirmed its commitment to work hard in driving forward the country in a bid to solve people’s problems, and elevate their livelihood through various projects and measures, as stated in the policy statement delivered earlier at the Parliament.
