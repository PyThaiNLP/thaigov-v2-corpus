Govt thanks public and all sectors for successful organization of Royal Barge Procession
October 27, 2024, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra expressed appreciation toward all government agencies, military, and civilians for being part of the successful organization of the Royal Barge Procession 2024 on auspicious occasion of the 6th cycle birthday anniversary of His Majesty the King (July 28, 2024). The Royal Barge Procession, which was held in a royally-fitted and graceful manner, performed during the Royal Kathina (robe offering) ceremony when His Majesty the King delivered new robes to the monks at Wat Arun.
The Prime Minister also thanked volunteers for taking care and facilitating attendance of the public and tourists. The Royal Barge Procession 2024 demonstrated the unity of all sectors in organizing this event, and manifested loyalty, love, and profound reverence of the Thai people toward the monarchy. At the ceremony, both Thai and foreign spectators had a chance to admire the royal grace of His Majesty the King and Her Majesty the Queen, as well as members of the royal family, along the procession route on both sides of the Chao Phraya River. They also witnessed exquisite beauty of the Royal Barge Procession, which reflects the richness of Thai tradition and culture, as well as uniqueness of Thai arts, the pride of all Thais.
