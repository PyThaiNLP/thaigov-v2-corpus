PM and spouse signs book of blessing on auspicious occasion of Her Majesty the Queen’s birthday anniversary
June 3, 2024, at the Red Room, Grand Palace, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin and spouse, Mrs. Pakpilai Thavisin, signed a book of blessing for Her Majesty the Queen on auspicious occasion of Her Majesty’s birthday anniversary today.
The Prime Minister and Deputy Prime Ministers paid respect to, and offered a flower vase in front of Her Majesty’s portrait on behalf of members of the cabinet, and another flower vase from the Prime Minister and spouse. The Prime Minister’s spouse and spouses of the Deputy Prime Ministers, then, offered their flower vase.
To end the formality, the Prime Minister and spouse signed the book of blessings, and paid respect in front of Her Majesty’s portrait once again.
