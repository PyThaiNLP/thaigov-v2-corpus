​PM ready for defending FY2025 budget bill
June 19, 2024, at 0830hrs, at the Parliament House, Bangkok, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin affirmed his readiness and stated that he has prepared for two weeks for the defense of the draft FY2025 budget bill at the 1st reading of the House of Representative today.
On the Opposition’s plan to censure the Government on hoarding of budget for the Digital Wallet scheme, the Prime Minister affirmed that there would not be the case of budget hoarding, and that he would be able to clarify all the issues raised during the debate. Pheu Thai MPs and responsible ministers have also well prepared for the debate at the House’s 1st reading.
