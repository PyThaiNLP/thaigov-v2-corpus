PM: Cabinet acknowledges NWRB’s measures for 2024 rainy season
June 4, 2024, at 1120hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet acknowledged proposal of the national water resources board (NWRB) on measures for the rainy season of 2024, and the plan to increase water management efficiency during the rainy season and to store water for the upcoming dry season. This is as part of the preparation to prevent the impact of possible disasters against the business sector, factories, and foreign investments, and to ensure that the ecosystem remains intact.
