Cabinet acknowledges proposals of House of Representatives on Landbridge project
June 4, 2024, at 1120hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet acknowledged the proposal of an ad-hoc committee of the House of Representatives on transport infrastructure for the development of economic corridor linking the Gulf of Thailand and the Andaman Sea or the “Landbridge” project. The proposal focuses on issues related to environment and security, land expropriation, and use of technologies for capacity enhancement. Ministry of Transport was instructed to collaborate with other concerned agencies to take the proposal into consideration, and later resubmit it to the cabinet.
