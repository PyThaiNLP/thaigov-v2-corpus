PM: First episode of “Talk with Srettha” TV program is recorded
June 14, 2024, at 1300hrs, at the Government House, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin disclosed about the launch of “Prime Minister Meets the People” program to promote direct communication between the Prime Minister and the public. The first episode of the television program “Talk with Srettha” has already been recorded. With regard to the personnel conflicts within the Royal Thai Police, the Prime Minister revealed that the result of the investigation is expected to be released on Wednesday, June 19.
