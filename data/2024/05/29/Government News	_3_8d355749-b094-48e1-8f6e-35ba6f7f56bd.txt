Thailand – Hong Kong SAR to continue promoting closer trade and investment cooperation
May 29, 2024, at 1145hrs (Hong Kong SAR local time), at the Drawing Room, Office of the Chief Executive (CEO), Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, People's Republic of China, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin had a bilateral meeting with Hong Kong SAR Chief Executive the Honorable John Lee Ka-chiu. Gist of the meeting is as follows:
The Prime Minister and Hong Kong Chief Executive were pleased to meet again after a bilateral meeting during the Prime Minister’s visit to Hong Kong October last year. Thailand and Hong Kong have forged close economic cooperation with an increase in mutual trade value. Hong Kong Chief Executive commended the Prime Minister’s vision on investment promotion, whereas the Prime Minister praised Hong Kong’s excellent investment and MICE infrastructures.
The two parties were pleased with tourism cooperation. The Prime Minister thanked Hong Kong for allowing Thailand to organize Songkran Festival in Hong Kong which further strengthens people-to-people contact and tourism cooperation. Thailand and Hong Kong also committed to continue to work together in promoting closer trade and investment cooperation.
