​PM hosts “Exclusive Networking Lunch Microsoft Build: AI Day”
May 1, 2024, at 1250hrs, at the Inner Santi Maitri Building, Government House, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin hosted “the Exclusive Networking Lunch Microsoft Build: AI Day” to Mr. Satya Nadella, Executive Chairman and CEO of Microsoft Corporation, and the delegation. He also delivered special remarks at the event. Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed gist as follows:
In November last year, the Prime Minister had a fruitful discussion with Mr. Nadella in San Francisco. The signing of an MOU on promotion of cooperation between Thailand and Microsoft Corporation also took place. He was confident that the MOU would lead to progress in driving national digital development, sustainability efforts, and investment in digital infrastructure, with a focus on leveraging AI and cloud technology
This partnership reflects mutual commitment to establishing Thailand as a “Digital Economy Hub”. The Thai Government mode of doing business is “whatever needs to be done, will be done”. It will take all necessary actions to transform Thailand into a premier destination for high-tech industries. To achieve this goal, the Government is dedicated to working collaboratively and closely with both foreign investors and the Thai business community.
The Prime Minister expressed hope that this Exclusive Networking Luncheon would serve as a platform where all participants could share insightful expertise and constructive thoughts on how to accelerate progress of AI development and propel digitalization of the economy forward.
