​Minister Attached to Prime Minister’s Office (Pichit Chuenban) assigned to screen bills and law-related cases before forwarding to PM
​Minister Attached to Prime Minister’s Office (Pichit Chuenban) assigned to screen bills and law-related cases before forwarding to PM
May 7, 2024, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that the cabinet has assigned Minister Attached to the Prime Minister’s Office Pichit Chuenban with the following duties: 1) reviewing draft cabinet resolutions compiled by the Cabinet Secretariat; and 2) scrutinizing laws, bills, and legal-related agendas before proposing to the Prime Minister. This is as proposed by the Cabinet Secretariat.
According to the Government Spokesperson, all approved resolutions and orders made at each cabinet meeting will be compiled by the Cabinet Secretariat, and forwarded to responsible ministers for their final review and signing before officially notifying relevant agencies/individuals for their information, compliance, or action.
In general, laws, bills, and litigation cases must be first scrutinized to ensure legal compliances and facilitate the Prime Minister’s deliberation. The Prime Minister, therefore, assigned Minister Attached to the Prime Minister’s Office Pichit Chuenban with the aforementioned duties.
