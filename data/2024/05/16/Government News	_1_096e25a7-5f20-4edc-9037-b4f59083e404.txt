PM pleased with “SUBCON Thailand 2024” which promote business matching between world-class investors and Thai manufacturers
May 15, 2024, at 1100hrs, at Bangkok International Trade & Exhibition Centre (BITEC), Bangkok, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin disclosed that he was pleased with “SUBCON Thailand 2024” which the BOI has organized for the past 17 years as a regional business matching event. The most important thing is that these are world-class corporations. With their investment in Thailand, the Prime Minister emphasized to concerned agencies to particularly encourage these investors to use parts that are produced and assembled in the country.
