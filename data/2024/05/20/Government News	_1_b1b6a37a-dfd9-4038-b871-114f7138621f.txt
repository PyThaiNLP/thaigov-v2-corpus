PM meets with executive director of Italy’s Intesa Sanpaolo Bank
May 19, 2024, Deputy Government Spokesperson Radklao Inthawong Suwankiri disclosed that at 1000hrs (local time in Milan, Republic of Italy), at Park Hyatt Milan Hotel, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin has met with Mr. Nicola Doninelli, Executive Director, Corporate and Investment Banking of Intesa Sanpaolo Bank, a top Italian international banking group providing financial services in all business areas (retail, corporate, and wealth management) to 13.6 million customers through a network of over 3,300 branches throughout Italy and over 900 branches in 12 countries in Central Eastern Europe and Middle Eastern and North African areas.
The Prime Minister exchanged information and views with Mr. Doninelli on the issues of mutual interest, including the bank’s overseas financial services. He was pleased to learn that Intesa Sanpaolo is keen to initiate collaboration with Thailand, and called for the bank’s support on the Thai Government’s megaprojects, especially the Landbridge project which is aimed to connect trade and logistical route from the Pacific Ocean to the Indian Ocean. The Landbridge will facilitate and lower the cost of shipping cargos through cutting across southern Thailand and providing an alternative connecting point between the two oceans.
The bank also expressed readiness to support the Thai Government’s issuance of Sustainability-Linked Bonds through encouraging global green funds to consider joint investment.
