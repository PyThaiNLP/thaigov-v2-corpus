PM to meet with executives of 5 Italian corporations in Rome
May 19, 2024, at 1750hrs (local time in Milan, Republic of Italy), Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin gave a press briefing on his scheduled missions in Rome on May 20, 2024, which has been jointly agreed upon by both BOI and Ministry of Foreign Affairs for the best interest of Thailand in terms of investment cooperation. On May 20, the Prime Minister will meet with executives of Italy’s major corporations: 1) Leonardo S.p.A., an Italian multinational company specializing in defense and security and helicopter manufacturing. The company has been collaborating with Thai Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment; 2) Generali, one of the largest global insurance and asset management providers, who is interested in expanding its investment in Thailand; 3) Eni, an integrated energy company. Eni produces biofuel from rubber seeds; 4) Barilla, a world leader in the markets of pasta. The company is interested to match with Thai agro producers and ready-to-eats manufacturers; and 5) Ducati, an Italian motorcycle manufacturing company. Ducati has its showrooms in Thailand, and is keen to learn more about BOI investment incentives and privileges.
