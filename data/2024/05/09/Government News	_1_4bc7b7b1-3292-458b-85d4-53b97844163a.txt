Ministry of Social Development and Human Security instructed to expedite consideration on proposals of 2023 National Children and Youth Assembly
May 7, 2024, at 11.30 hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin disclosed that he has ordered Ministry of Social Development and Human Security to expedite their review and consideration over the proposed resolutions of the 2023 National Children and Youth Assembly.
He emphasized that the Government honors views and recommendations of the new generations, especially on the issues of child abuse, family violence, and school threats. The Prime Minister already instructed the Royal Thai Police to strictly enforce the law to prevent such crimes.
