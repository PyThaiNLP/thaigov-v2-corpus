PM to chair cabinet retreat in Phayao on March 19
PM to chair cabinet retreat in Phayao on March 19
March 12, 2024, Deputy Government Spokesperson Kharom Polpornklang disclosed that Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin is scheduled to take a field visit to the Upper Northern Provincial Cluster 2 (Chiang Rai, Nan, Phayao, and Phrae), as well as Chiang Mai and Lampang provinces during March 18-19, 2024. His schedule is as follows:
On March 18, 2024 at 1330hrs, the Prime Minister will meet with concerned agencies to discuss the plan for Phayao Airport development, which is to be located in Don Si Chum Subdistrict and Ban Tham Subdistrict, Dok Khamtai District. He will, then, travel to Ban Huak Border Checkpoint in Phu Sang district to follow up on the border situation and construction progress of the new Ban Huak / Phayao Border Checkpoint (CIQ) before paying homage to Pho Khun Ngam Muang Monument. The Prime Minister will later travel to TK Park Phayao to listen to the briefing on the province’s development roadmap and see exhibits and local products.
On March 19, 2024, at 1000hrs, the Prime Minister will chair the 2/2024 cabinet retreat at Phaya Ngam Muang Hall, Phayao University. He will, then, go to Chong Kham Temple in Ngao district, Lampang province, to pay homage, and meet with the local people at the provincial hall before returning to Bangkok.
