PM calls on cabinet members to prepare for upcoming general debates
March 19, 2024, at 1130hrs, at Phaya Ngam Muang Hall, Phayao University, Phayao province, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin gave a press briefing on the outcome of the 2/2024 cabinet retreat that he has instructed all the cabinet members to prepare for and attend the 2nd and 3rd readings of the Parliament regarding the 2024 Budget Act on March 20-21, 2024, and the general debate proposed by the Senate on March 25, 2024, as well as to get ready for the general debate by the Opposition parties.
On a question about an issue of former Prime Minister Thaksin Shinawatra which may be raised during the general debates, the Prime Minister affirmed his readiness, and stated that it was the duty of the Government to answer and clarify all the questions raised in the debates.
