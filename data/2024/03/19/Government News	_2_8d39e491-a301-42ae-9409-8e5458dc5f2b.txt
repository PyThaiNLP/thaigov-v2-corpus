PM to chair meeting of Digital Wallet Policy Committee
PM to chair meeting of Digital Wallet Policy Committee
March 19, 2024, at 1130hrs, at Phaya Ngam Muang Hall, Phayao University, Phayao province, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin gave a press briefing on the outcome of the 2/2024 cabinet retreat that he would chair a meeting of the Digital Wallet Policy Committee in a week or two to deliberate all recommendations and information compiled by related subcommittees. But before that, these recommendations and information would be first discussed among the Coalition parties. The Prime Minister affirmed that the people remain hopeful for subsidy under the Digital Wallet Scheme.
