PM wishes Thai people prosperity and affluence for the 2024 Chinese New Year
PM wishes Thai people prosperity and affluence for the 2024
Chinese New Year
February 9, 2022, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister
and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin gave a blessing to the Thai people on occasion of the
Chinese New Year. Here’s the Prime Minister’s blessings:
“ 你好 I, Srettha Thavisin, the Prime Minister of Thailand. On occasion of the 2024 Chinese New
Year, which is a very important event for the Chinese Thais and all the Chinese, I would like to
bless 新年快乐, 万事如意 to all of you. Wish all the best, and be this year be the year of
prosperity and affluence. I wish for flourish and cordial trade and investment cooperation
between Thailand and China, and hope that the visa exemption scheme would further facilitate
and promote people-to-people relations between the two countries. Thailand stands ready to
welcome and ensure safety of the Chinese people. May brotherly friendship between Thailand
and China last forever.”
