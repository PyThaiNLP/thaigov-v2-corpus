All sectors called on to maintain cleanliness of public spaces
during auspicious year of His Majesty the King's 6th Cycle
Birthday Anniversary
February 20, 2024, at 1200hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the
weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that
as part of the year-long celebration to commemorate His Majesty the King&#39;s 6 th Cycle Birthday
Anniversary, the cabinet has sought cooperation from Ministry of Justice’s Department of
Corrections, security agencies, armed forces, and provincial governors to maintain cleanliness of
public spaces, such as, footpaths, public parks, drainage system, water sources, etc.,
throughout this auspicious year. This is also to prevent flood disaster.
