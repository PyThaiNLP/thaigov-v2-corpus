PM: Next cabinet retreat to be held in Phayao province during March 18-19
February 20, 2024, at 1200hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the next cabinet retreat will be held in Phayao province during March 18-19, 2024.
