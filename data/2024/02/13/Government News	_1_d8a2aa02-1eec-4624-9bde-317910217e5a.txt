​Cabinet approves additional holiday on April 12 to stimulate tourism
February 13, 2024, at 11.00 hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet approved the proposal made by Office of the Cabinet Secretariat to announce April 12, 2024 as an additional public holiday for 2024. This will make 5-day holidays during April 12-16, 2024. The approval is made with an aim to promote traveling and tourism.
Minister of Tourism and Sports was also assigned to especially promote tourism in the secondary cities to ensure that these cities be most benefited from the additional holiday instead of such big cities as Phuket, Chiang Mai, or Pattaya which are already popular among the tourists.
