​Govt to set up National Halal Industry Commission and Thai Halal Industry Center
February 27, 2024, at 1020hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet has made approval to the establishment of National Halal Industry Commission and Thai Halal Industry Center in a bid to promote Thai Halal food/products and tourism.
The Prime Minister will also raise this matter during his field visit to the 3 Southern border provinces on February 27-29, 2024. He has earlier met with the ruler of Dubai, Sultan of Brunei, and the Malaysian Prime Minister, and agreed with them on Halal cooperation and the development of a center of Halal food. The cabinet’s approval on the establishment of National Halal Industry Commission and Thai Halal Industry Center will help uplift quality of life of the Thai people.
