​Govt to liaise with Cambodian counterpart in fighting against transborder fire
February 27, 2024, at 1020hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed about the establishment of a Thailand-Cambodia joint working group on wildfire prevention that during the past 3-4 weeks, many hotspots were detected in Cambodia. Ministry of Foreign Affairs has reported that the Cambodian Government would dispatch a team to liaise with the Thai counterpart on setting up of a wildfire Hotline. The Prime Minister also ordered Ministry of Natural Resource and Environment to establish the Team Thailand for liaising with the Cambodian Government. This will be an official cooperation following an ongoing discussion and immediate solution to wildfire incidents during the past 2-3 weeks.
