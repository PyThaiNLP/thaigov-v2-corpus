Title: Request for creating debt with binding effect on budget across fiscal year in respect of budget items involving amount of one billion Baht and over (Ministry of Justice)
The cabinet approved in principle for Ministry of Justice to create the debt with a binding effect on the budget across fiscal year (in respect of the Ministry’s 2 projects involving amount of one billion Baht and over) for the total amount of 3,390,406,000 Baht (678,081,200 Baht to be carried over to FY2025, and 2,712,324,800 Baht to be carried over to FY2026 and FY2027). This is in accordance with Section 26 of the Budgetary Procedures Act, B.E. 2561.
The Ministry’s two projects are:
Construction of Uttaradit Provincial Penitentiary Construction Project and accompanying buildings: Out of the total budget of 1,767,249,500 Baht, 353,449,900 Baht will be carried over to the fiscal year 2025 budget, the rest (1,413,799,600 Baht) will be carried forward to fiscal year 2026 and 2027 budgets
Construction of Yasothorn Provincial Penitentiary Construction Project and accompanying buildings: Out of the total budget of 1,623,156,500 Baht, 324,631,300 Baht will be carried over to the fiscal year 2025 budget, the rest (1,298,525,200 Baht) will be carried forward to fiscal year 2026 and 2027 budgets

Title: Request for creating debt with binding effect on budget across fiscal year in respect of budget items involving amount of one billion Baht and over (Ministry of Transport)

Title: Joint Statement of the 24th EU-ASEAN Ministerial Meeting
The cabinet approved the draft Joint Statement of the 24th EU-ASEAN Ministerial Meeting. Should there be an amendment in parts that are not gist nor against national interest, Ministry of Foreign Affairs may proceed without having to resubmit to the cabinet. Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs or a representative is authorized to adopt the Joint Statement. This is as proposed by Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
Gist
The 24th EU-ASEAN Ministerial Meeting will be held on February 2, 2024 in Brussels, Kingdom of Belgium. The draft Joint Statement of the 24th EU-ASEAN Ministerial Meeting will demonstrate the joint intent of ASEAN and EU member states to advance relations under strategic partnership, and promote cooperation in wide-ranging areas, e.g., connectivity, economic cooperation, trade and investment, FTA, climate change, and cyber security, among others.
Title: Outcome documents for the 4th ASEAN Digital Ministers Meeting (ADGMIN)
The cabinet approved 12 draft outcome documents for the 4th ASEAN Digital Ministers Meeting (ADGMIN). Should there be an amendment in parts that are not gist nor against national interest, Ministry of Digital Economy and Society may proceed without having to resubmit to the cabinet. Minister of Digital Economy and Society or a representative is authorized to sign the document in 1., and adopt the documents in 2. This is as proposed by Ministry of Digital Economy and Society.
Gist
The 4th ASEAN Digital Ministers’ Meeting (The 4th ADGMIN) and related meetings will be hosted by Singapore’s Ministry for Communications and Information during January 30 and February 2, 2024 under the theme: Building an Inclusive and Trusted Digital Ecosystem. Outcome documents to be signed and adopted at the meetings are:
To be signed: Memorandum of Understanding between the Association of Southeast Asian Nations and the People’s Republic of China on Co – operation in Communications and Digital Technology
To be adopted:
Singapore Declaration
The 4th ADGMIN Joint Media Statement
Part 2 of ASEAN Guide on Artificial Intelligence (AI) Governance and Ethics
Part 2 of EU – ASEAN Joint Guide on Model Contractual Clauses for Data Transfers - Implementation Guide
ASEAN Digital Masterplan 2025 Mid - Term Review
Financial Model of the ASEAN Regional Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT)
Regulatory Pilot Space (RPS) to Facilitate Cross - Border Digital Data Flows to Enabling Self - Driving Car in ASEAN
Draft ASEAN Framework Document on Digital Economy Supply Chain Logistics for Rural Areas
Assessing the Relationship between ICT Infrastructure and Digital Skills and the Inflow of Foreign Investment to the ASEAN ICT Sector: Identification of Policy Options to Improve Investment Attraction
Establishment of the Standard to Exchange Data and Information Related to Disaster in the ASEAN Region
Operational Framework of the Working Group on Anti - Online Scam (WG - AS)
Title: Draft Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between Ministry of Digital Economy and Society of the Kingdom of Thailand and Ministry of Communication and Information Technology of the Republic of Indonesia on Communication and Information Technology Cooperation and Digitalization
The cabinet approved the following proposals of Ministry of Digital Economy and Society:
Approved draft Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between Ministry of Digital Economy and Society of the Kingdom of Thailand and Ministry of Communication and Information Technology of the Republic of Indonesia on Communication and Information Technology Cooperation and Digitalization. Should there be an amendment in parts that are not gist nor against national interest, Ministry of Digital Economy and Society may proceed without having to resubmit to the cabinet
Approved Permanent Secretary to Ministry of Digital Economy and Society as Thai signatory of the aforementioned MOU
Gist
The draft Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between Ministry of Digital Economy and Society of the Kingdom of Thailand and Ministry of Communication and Information Technology of the Republic of Indonesia on Communication and Information Technology Cooperation and Digitalization prescribes promotion of bilateral relations and cooperation between the two parties under the scope of communication, information technology, and digitalization. The signing of the MOU will be carried out during the 4th ADGSOM held in Singapore on January 30-31, 2024.
