Cabinet approves in principle draft Act on Protection of Rights and
Promotion of the Way of Life of Ethnic Groups
February 6, 2024, at 1200hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, Prime Minister
and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet approved in principle the
draft Act on Protection of Rights and Promotion of the Way of Life of Ethnic Groups, and
subordinate legislations. It also ordered Office of Council of State and other concerned agencies
to scrutinize the draft bill to finetune and ensure that the bill does not contradict with other
laws before resubmitting to the cabinet and, later, to the Parliament, for final approval. The Act
on Protection of Rights and Promotion of the Way of Life of Ethnic Groups has been drafted on
the basis of cultural diversity to minimize disparity, prevent conflict, and protect the rights of the ethnic groups.
