Cabinet approves visa exemption for holders of diplomatic and official passports of Lithuania
February 6, 2024, at 1200hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet has made approval to the visa exemption for holders of diplomatic and official passports of the Republic of Lithuania in a bid to promote relations between the two countries.
