PM pleased with cabinet’s approval of Clean Air for People Act
January 9, 2024, at 1130hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet has made approval to the Clean Air for People Act, which was earlier opened for public hearing, and reviewed by concerned agencies and Office of Council of State.
According to the Prime Minister, the Government has declared the issue of PM2.5 particulates as a national agenda, and made clear that clean air is one of the people’s basic rights. The cabinet approved the clean air bill in accordance with the Government’s policy directives announced at the Parliament.
The Prime Minister also mentioned his field visit to Chiang Mai during January 10-12, 2024 to follow up on the solving of PM2.5 particulates, and affirmed the Government’s commitment to implement both short, medium, and long-term measures in a bid to promote clean air and pleasant environment for people’s health safety.
