PM: Next cabinet retreat is in Ranong
January 9, 2024, at 1130hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the next cabinet will soon be held in Ranong province with an aim to promote and enhance potentials of provinces along the Andaman Coast, and explore new tourist attractions to boost economy in these Andaman provinces. The Prime Minister added that he would look into the Landbridge project.
