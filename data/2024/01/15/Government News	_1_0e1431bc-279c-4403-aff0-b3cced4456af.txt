“Uncle Nid” the Prime Minister welcomed children to Govt House for 2024 National Children’s Day
January 13, 2024, at 0910hrs, at the Purple Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin greeted and welcomed 10 youth representatives, comprising those excelled in school and with good behavior, and the underprivileged children, who were brought in by Ministry of Social Development and Human Security on occasion of the 2024 National Children’s Day. He took photos with the 10 youths.
The Prime Minister, then, had a friendly conversation with the children, talking with them in English and Chinese to test their language ability, and giving his moral support to the underprivileged children, before taking the 10 youths to Santi Maitri Building to join the Government House’s activities for the 2024 National Children’s Day.
