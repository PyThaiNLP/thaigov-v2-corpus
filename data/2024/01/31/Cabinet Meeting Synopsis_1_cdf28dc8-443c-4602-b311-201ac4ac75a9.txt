Cabinet approves Pol.Gen. Roy Ingkhapairoj as new NSC Secretary-General
January 30, 2024, at 1130hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting today, approved an appointment of Police General Roy Ingkhapairoj as Secretary-General of the National Security Council (NSC).
