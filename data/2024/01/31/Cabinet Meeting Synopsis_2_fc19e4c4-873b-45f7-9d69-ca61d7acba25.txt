PM: Cabinet approves amendment of Fishery Act
January 30, 2024, at 1130hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet approved in principle a draft bill to amend the Fishery Act, which is in accordance with the Illegal, Unreported and Unregulated (IUU) principles as scrutinized by Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives and Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The bill will be forwarded to Office of Council of State and, later, to the Parliament for their deliberation, of which the process is expected to take approx. 2 months, before being resubmitted to the cabinet for approval.
