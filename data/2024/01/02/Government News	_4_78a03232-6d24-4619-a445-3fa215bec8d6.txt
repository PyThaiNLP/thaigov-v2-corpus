PM calls on cabinet members to heed His Majesty the King's New Year statement
PM calls on cabinet members to heed His Majesty the King's New
Year statement
January 2, 2024, at 1100hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that at the first cabinet meeting of the year, he has instructed members of the cabinet to heed and follow His Majesty the King's 2024 New Year statement on work ethics and conservation of Thai cultural heritages and traditions. The year 2024 will be an auspicious year as it marks His Majesty the King's 6th Cycle Birthday Anniversary (July 28, 2024). The Government has planned for a year-long celebration, and the Prime Minister called on concerned committees and authorities to strictly follow the plan in implementing related celebrations and events.
