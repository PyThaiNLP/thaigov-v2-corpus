​PM ready to showcase Thailand’s investment potentials at WEF Annual Meeting 2024
January 17, 2024 at 0800hrs (Davos local time), at the Congress Centre, Davos, the Swiss Confederation, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed about the message he would convey to investors from all over the world at the Country Strategy Dialogue for Thailand, held during the World Economic Forum (WEF) Annual Meeting 2024 (Davos 2024).
That key message is “Thailand is back on track”. The country is ready to join hands with all partners; old or new; big or small, and to welcome inward foreign investment. A range of attractive tax and non-tax incentives are being offered, and the country is also investing heavily in renewable energy. The Prime Minister will take the opportunity to present his vision and the country’s economic, trade and investment policies in a bid to boost confidence of business leaders from around the world.
Minister of Transport Suriya Juangroongruangkit added that the Landbridge project will also be presented. This is a project that the Prime Minister and the Government attach great importance to, and hope to draw interest from world’s top investors.
