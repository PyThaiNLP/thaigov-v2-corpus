PM pleased with success of his attendance at Davos 2024
January 18, 2024, at 0900hrs (Davos local time), at the Congress Centre, Davos, the Swiss Confederation, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that he was pleased with his attendance at the World Economic Forum (WEF) Annual Meeting 2024 (Davos 2024), where he managed to send a message to the world that Thailand has been back on track. The country has opened for and in all dimensions, including trade negotiation and foreign investment.
During his attendance at Davos 2024, the Prime Minister engaged in 19 activities altogether, be it, seminars, bilateral meetings with important figures, and meetings with the private sector. He has met with the Swiss President, Prime Minister of Belgium, Prime Minister of Cambodia, and Prime Minister of Vietnam. He also met with top executives from world’s leading firms, e.g., COCA COLA, Dubai World, and Adani Group.
The Prime Minister believed great benefits had been reaped as a result of his engagement in seminars and meetings during Davos 2024. Although the schedule was a bit tight, he did not feel tired. The Prime Minister met with WEF Founder and Executive Chairman, and affirmed his commitment to be back at the WEF annual meeting next year.
