PM meets with EC president
January 18, 2024, at 1000hrs (Davos local time), at the Congress Centre, Davos, the Swiss Confederation, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin met with president of the European Commission Ursula Von der Leyen.
The Prime Minister and EC president exchanged views on world’s current conflicts and unrests, and the need for countries across the world to further strengthen relations and connectivity at both the economic and people-to-people levels. They also agreed to finalize negotiation on Thailand-EU FTA soon.
