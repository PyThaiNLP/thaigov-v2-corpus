​Airport bottlenecks to be urgently addressed
​Airport bottlenecks to be urgently addressed
March 22, 2023, Deputy Government Spokesperson Traisulee Taisaranakul disclosed about the overview of Thailand’s tourism situation which saw continuous increase in the number of incoming tourists and domestic travel of the Thais.
According to the report of Ministry of Tourism and Sports, the country was visited by 5,578,721 foreign tourists during January 1 and March 18, 2023, which generated the revenue of over 215,052 million Baht.
However, with the notable increase of tourists entering Thailand, airport bottleneck has become a problem, with long lines of passengers at the airline counters and the immigration checkpoints. Airfares are also reported to hike up considerably. According to the Deputy Government Spokesperson, concerned agencies are now working together to immediately address these problems.
