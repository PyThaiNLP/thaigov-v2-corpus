Prime Minister’s Statement on Holy Month of Ramadan (AH 1444)
March 22, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed the statement of Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, delivered occasion of the Holy Month of Ramadan for AH 1444.
The Prime Minister conveyed well wishes and love to the Muslims throughout the country and the world, and congratulated them for the chance to perform their religious ritual with strong will and forbearance in fasting, refraining from bad deeds of all kinds, and treating others with kindness and non-violence, all of which are key fundamentals for pureness to the mind. Studying the Noble Qur’an is truly a means to express their respect and faith in Allāh, and enhance mental strength and purity with strong faith, which would bring about sustainable peace in the community, society, and the nation.
On the auspicious occasion of the Holy Month of Ramadan AH1444, the Prime Minister asked for the blessing of Allāh for the happiness of all Muslims in Thailand. May they progress in lives and careers. He also wished for prosperity, and success in all endeavors.
