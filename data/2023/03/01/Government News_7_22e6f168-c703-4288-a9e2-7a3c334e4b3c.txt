PM: Thai economy is positively advancing with continued recovery of local and grassroots economy
February 24, 2023, at 1225hes, at the Government House, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha stated about positive trend of the Thai economic situation. An increase of tourism activities has contributed to the recovery of local and grassroots economy, and the increase in GDP ratio of tourism sites, accommodations and food. Although Thailand saw a decrease in agricultural product prices, the decrease is not as serious as concerned, and relevant agencies are closely monitoring and addressing the situation. SMEs empowerment has also been undertaken to ensure their access to funding and continued development in line with the Government’s policy on economic restructuring.
The Prime Minister also emphasized the importance of revenue generation, and management and optimization of budget in line with the people’s need. People of all sectors, including low-income earners, must have equal access to public services and welfares. Nevertheless, implementation of any new measure needs to take into account possible impact and budget availability for genuine benefit of the country and people. The Prime Minister also stressed that national administration has been carried out based on related principles, regulations, and laws, which are all transparent, accountable, and just.
