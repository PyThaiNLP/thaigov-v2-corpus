PM pleased with the AOT's increased net profit report.
On February 24, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha was reported on the Airports of Thailand Public Company Limited (AOT) ’s financial statements for the three-month period from October to December 2022, which generated a net profit of 342.77 million baht, compared with the prior year’s similar period with a net loss of 4,271.66 million baht.
According to the AOT’s financial statements, the revenue from sales and services also accelerated by 6,496.69 million baht, or 279.15%, as a consequence of an increase in air traffic at the six airports under the AOT’s responsibility, which increased by 150,378 flights, or 105.04%, compared to that of the same period of the prior year. Moreover, the growing number of profits came from the increase of 10.98 million international passengers and 12.03 million domestic passengers.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister was pleased to learn that the net profit of the Airports of Thailand Public Company Limited (AOT) increased to 342.77 million baht and believes that profits will continue to rise, demonstrating the Government's commitment to managing all concerned agencies to work together to improve the country's economy and tourism sectors this year.
