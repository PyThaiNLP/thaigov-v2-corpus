Govt Spokesperson: 24/7 Hotlines set up by 12 commercial banks to receives public reports on call center gangs and money scams
March 9, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed information about the 24/7 hotlines set up by 12 commercial banks to directly receive reports and complaints from the public on cases related to call center gangs and money scams. These hotlines are:
KBANK: 0-2888-8888 press 001
Krung Thai Bank 0-2111-1111 press 108
Krungsri Bank 1572 press 5
Bangkok Bank 1333 or 0-2645-5555 press *3
Siam Commercial Bank: 0-2777-7575
TMBThanachart Bank 1428 press 03
Government’s Savings Bank: 1115 press 6
CIMB Thai Bank: 0-2626-7777 press 00
Thai Credit Retail Bank: 0-2697-5454
UOB Bank Thailand: 0-2344-9555
Land and House Bank: 0-2359-0000 press 8
Government Housing Bank: 0-2645-9000 press 33
According to the Government Spokesperson, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha has closely followed up on the progress of his instruction made to concerned agencies to accelerate their action against online frauds and scams. The Government also pushes forward the Royal Decree on Cyber Crime Suppression and Prevention, B.E. …, to enhance authority’s power in curbing scammers in an efficient manner.
