PM: Concerned ministries instructed to boost tourism through various measures
December 19, 2023, at 1120hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that he has instructed Minister of Tourism and Sports to host a meeting with concerned ministers of Cambodia, Lao PDR, and Vietnam to discuss promotion of regional tourism. He would also inquire with the Malaysian Prime Minister himself whether Malaysia would like to join the pact.
The Prime Minister also ordered Ministry of Tourism and Sports to work together with the Royal Thai Police in maintaining orderliness at the immigration checkpoints and ensuring tourist safety. The Ministry will also collaborate with Office of Insurance Commission (OIC) to provide insurance to all incoming tourists, with the coverage of up to 1,000,000 Baht in the case of death, and compensation based on actual amount or up to 500,000 Baht in the case of accident. Ministry of Public Health is also assigned to arrange healthcare services and health insurance for tourists, and proceed according to the budgetary procedure if there is a need to utilize central budget. The Prime Minister also instructed Ministry of Foreign Affairs to collaborate with Ministry of Tourism and Sports to hand down policy to staffs at 30 local tourism offices across the country on proactive operation, and train them on how to promote the best side of Thai tourism.
