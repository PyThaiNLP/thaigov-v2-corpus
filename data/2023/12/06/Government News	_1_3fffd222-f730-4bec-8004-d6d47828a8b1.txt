PM and spouse present floral offerings on occasion of King Rama IX’s birthday anniversary, Thai National Day, and Father’s Day on December 5, 2023
December 5, 2023, at 08.30 hrs, at King Rama IX Memorial Park,Dusit district, Bangkok, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin and Dr. Pakpilai Thavisin, spouse, presided over the ceremony to present floral offerings and pay homage to H.M. King Bhumibol Adulyadej The Great on occasion of His Majesty’s birthday anniversary, Thai National Day, and Father’s Day. Participating in the ceremony were cabinet members and spouses.
Upon arrival at King Rama IX Memorial Park, the Prime Minister paid homage in front of H.M. King Bhumibol Adulyadej The Great’s statue, and offered two traditional cone-shaped floral receptacles, one for himself and the other, on behalf of members of the cabinet, while the Prime Minister’s spouse presented floral receptacle on behalf of spouses of cabinet members to complete the ceremony.
