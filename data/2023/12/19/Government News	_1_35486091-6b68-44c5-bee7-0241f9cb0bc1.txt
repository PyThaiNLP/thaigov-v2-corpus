​PM: Draft Marriage Equality Act expected to be brought to Parliament on Dec 21
December 19, 2023, at 1120hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that the cabinet approved the Civil and Commercial Code Amendment Act (No. …) B.E. …. The amendment was made to accommodate the draft Marriage Equality Act which will soon be submitted to the Parliament.
The draft Marriage Equality Act was opened for public hearing, and was reviewed by concerned agencies, such as, Ministry of Social Development and Human Security and the Court of Justice. Office of Council of State has also scrutinized the bill once again to ensure it is intact. Once taken into force, the bill will allow two individuals to legally marry regardless of their sexual orientation or gender identity, and be entitled to all the rights and privileges connected to the marriage.
The Prime Minister added that the draft Marriage Equality Act is expected to be proposed to the Parliament on December 21, 2023.
