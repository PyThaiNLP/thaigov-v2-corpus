​PM: Govt continues to assist Thai hostages in Israel
​PM: Govt continues to assist Thai hostages in Israel
December 5, 2023, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed about a few matters, including his conversation with chief of the Royal Thai Armed Forces Gen. Songwit Noonpakdee about the border security situation in all dimensions, particularly Thailand-Myanmar border situation, and the Royal Thai Armed Forces’ scope of assistance in tackling illicit drugs and PM2.5 particulates.
The Prime Minister emphasized the need to address the issue of PM2.5 particulates during his recent visit to Chiang Mai. Collaboration between Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment and security agencies is necessary to tackle PM2.5 main sources, such as open burning, wildfire, among others.
With regard to the Government’s continued assistance of Thai nationals who have been held hostage in Israel and are yet be released, the Prime Minister stated that the assistance must be waited until the next truce to ensure safety of the Thai hostages. On the issue of illegal pork import, he has instructed Ministry of Justice to track down mastermind and financiers behind illegal pork imports.
