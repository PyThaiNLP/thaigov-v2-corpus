PM meets with Special Envoy of the Government of Japan for National Reconciliation in Myanmar
January 13, 2023, at 1300hrs, at the Purple Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House, Mr. SASAKAWA Yohei, Special Envoy of the Government of Japan for National Reconciliation in Myanmar and chairman of The Nippon Foundation, paid a courtesy call on Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha on occasion of his visit to Thailand. Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed gist of the meeting as follows:
The Prime Minister and Mr. Sasakawa constructively exchanged view on the Myanmar situation, and agreed on the non-violence. Both parties also came to terms to support peaceful resolution for peace and reconciliation.
