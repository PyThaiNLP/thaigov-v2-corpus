​PM pleased with USOPC’s recognition of Muaythai
January 24, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha was pleased that Muaythai is officially a recognized sport organization, granted by The United States Olympic & Paralympic Committee (USOPC) Affiliate Organizations Council on January 10, 2023. The recognition is in line with the Thai Government’s policy to promote Muaythai as tourism soft power.
The Government Spokesperson added that concerned agencies have continued to promote Muaythai at the international stage. Thai boxers were sent to represent Thailand at “The World Games 2022” in Birmingham, Alabama, United States during July 7-17, 2022, and brought back 2 golds, 1 silver, and 1 bronze medals. At the World Games 2022, Thai athletes were also sent to compete in 9 sports, namely, air sports, jujitsu, sumo, pétanque, billiards, Muaythai, extreme, floorball, and wushu, among which Muaythai was the most popular with the greatest number of spectators.
The Prime Minister was pleased that Muaythai has been recognized globally, and instructed concerned agencies to take forward the success in mobilizing national sports development plan to further promote Thai sports at the international level.
