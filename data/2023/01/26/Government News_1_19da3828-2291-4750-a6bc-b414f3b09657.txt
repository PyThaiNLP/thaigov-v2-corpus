Memorandum of Understanding on Thailand - Turkiye Higher Education Cooperation approved by cabinet
January 25, 2023, Deputy Government Spokesperson Tipanan Sirichana disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting on January 23, 2023, has made approval to the Memorandum of Understanding on Thailand - Turkiye Higher Education Cooperation to be signed between Thai Ministry of Higher Education, Science, Research and Innovation and Turkiye's Council of Higher Education. The MOU prescribes exchange of academic and scientific cooperation, i.e., student and personnel training, short-term research project, exchange of educational media, workshop, seminar, and conference, between higher education institutions of the two countries. The MOU will take effect for 4 years, as proposed by Ministry of Higher Education, Science, Research and Innovation.
According to the Deputy Government Spokesperson, aside from exchange of educational and technical cooperation, Thailand and Turkiye agreed upon educational accreditation in accordance with each other's related law, and exchange of scholarship recipients under the mutually agreed procedure. The two countries also came to terms to set up, implement, and develop joint curriculum for both undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral studies.
Deliberation of the draft MOU will be part of the agenda for the 4th meeting of joint committee on Thailand - Turkiye Academic and Economic Cooperation to be held during January 25-26, 2023 in Ankara, Republic of Turkiye. Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Don Pramudwinai will lead the Thai delegation to attend the meeting.
