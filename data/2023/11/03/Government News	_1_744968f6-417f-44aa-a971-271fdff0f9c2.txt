PM: Appointment of NSC Secretary-General postponed due to legal complications
November 2, 2023, at 0930hrs, at Office of the National Security Council (ONSC), which is located inside the Government House compound, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin gave a press briefing, following his chairing of the NSC meeting, that the meeting discussed about a prospective NSC Secretary-General, but the appointment has been postponed due to legal complications, which is expected to be settled in 10 days. He affirmed that the meeting was carried out in a cordial mood, and in spite of the postponement, NSC could continue to perform duty in an efficient manner.
After the meeting, the Prime Minister walked back to Thai Khu Fah Building, and greeted canteen vendors, asking them about prices of raw materials, such as cooking oil, eggs, and sugar. The vendors informed the Prime Minister that the cost of living remains high, especially raw material cost. The Prime Minister also visited 60+ Plus Bakery and Chocolate Café in the Government House, and bought some drink. The 60+ Plus Bakery and Chocolate Café is established to support employment of people with disabilities.
