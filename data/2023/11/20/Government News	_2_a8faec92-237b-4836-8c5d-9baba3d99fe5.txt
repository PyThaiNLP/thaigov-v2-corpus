PM congratulates Anntonia Porsild for winning 2023 Miss Universe
Pageant 1 st runner-up
November 19, 2023, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister
and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin congratulated Ms. Anntonia Porsild, the Thai beauty
queen who represented Thailand at the Miss Universe Pageant 2023, for winning the 1 st runner-
up. The beauty pageant was held in San Salvador, El Salvador.
The Prime Minister commended Ms. Porsild for her best representation of Thailand, and her
showcase of Thai culture and soft power to the world through the national costume ‘Goddess of
the Ayutthaya Kingdom’ which is inspired by the sculpture “Earth Goddess” from the Ayutthaya
period of the Siamese Kingdom (14 th – 18 th century). Although she was not crowned Miss
Universe title, she is a pride of Thailand and the people.
