PM: Thailand eagerly welcomes prospective investors to be part of Landbridge project
November 12, 2023, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed that he would take the opportunity of his attendance at the APEC Economic Leaders’ Meeting and related meetings to invite prospective investors to consider investment cooperation with Thailand on the Landbridge project.
According to the Prime Minister, in every official visit abroad, he would find a chance to update foreign investors on Thailand’s readiness and eagerness to welcome new investments. This would not only boost confidence of the investors, but also be a key factor for the new generations, especially who are aspiring to relocate overseas, to map their future. Even though the Landbridge project may not be able to implemented soon, he hoped that the new generations would foresee great opportunities in and be inspired by the project, as well as the Government’s other mega projects, and change their mind on the relocation. Thailand must become better off with the solidarity of its people, who will work together to move the nation forward and make our society a better place to live.
In terms of the investment incentives, the Landbridge project will offer tax privileges, clean and renewable energy promotion, water management in industrial sector, aviation hub, and high-speed railroad, as well as Laem Chabang Port to accommodate future logistics activities and the Landbridge project. As for social dimension, which is also a factor the foreign investors would take into account in their decision for investment, Thailand boasts peaceful coexistence of the people, and world-class medical and educational institutions.
