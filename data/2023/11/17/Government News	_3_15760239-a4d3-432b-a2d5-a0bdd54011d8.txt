PM has bilateral meeting with Japanese PM
November 15, 2023, at 1630hrs (San Francisco, U.S.A. local time), Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin had a bilateral meeting with Prime Minister Kishida Fumio of Japan during the 30th APEC Economic Leaders' Week (AELW) to discuss bilateral relations and cooperation that have spanned for over 50 years.
The Thai Prime Ministers affirmed that despite an increasing demand of EVs, Thailand would continue to support Japan’s investment and manufacturing of combustion engine vehicles, as Japan has been top investor in Thailand for the past 30 years. The Japanese Prime Minister also affirmed that Japanese investors would continue to invest in automobile industry in Thailand, and added that he would attend the ASEAN Summit during December 16-18, 2023.
The two Prime Ministers also discussed and agreed upon business visa exemption to facilitate networking and connectivity between the business sector of the two countries. Ministry of Foreign Affairs would work together with the Japanese counterpart on related detail including the feasibility of the matter.
