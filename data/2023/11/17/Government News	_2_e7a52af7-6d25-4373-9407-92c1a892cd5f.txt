PM meets with executives of Apple Inc.
November 15, 2023, at 1400hrs (San Francisco, U.S.A. local time), at the Moscone Convention Center, San Francisco, U.S.A., executives of the Apple Inc. paid a courtesy call on Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin.
According to the Apple executives, Thailand ranks 2nd in terms of the number of iOS application developers in the Southeast Asian region. They commended the infrastructure and quality of products manufactured in the country, as Apple has its manufacturers in Thailand as well.
Apple executives also inquired about Thailand’s policy on digital economy, on which the Prime Minister affirmed the Government’s plan to implement digital economy policies in a comprehensive manner, be it, ease of doing business, one stop services, digital government, and green energy, among others. Apple has set the goal to reduce carbon emission by 2030, and expects its suppliers and manufacturers to follow suit. The Prime Minister stated that Thailand leads the region in terms of renewable energy, as the country boasts its wind power, solar power, and hydropower, and has planned for an expansion of hydro-floating solar hybrid project in dams in the country.
Both parties agreed to establish Apple Developer Academy, the Prime Minister extended an invitation for the company to set up one in Thailand. The Thai Government stands ready to provide support, and promote exchange of knowhow and expertise with Apple. Thailand’s concerned agency will collaborate and talk with Apple Inc. in more detail regarding the matter.
