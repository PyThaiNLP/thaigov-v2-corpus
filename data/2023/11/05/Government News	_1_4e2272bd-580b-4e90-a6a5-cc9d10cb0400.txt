PM expresses condolences over earthquake disaster in Western Nepal
November 4, 2023, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin expressed condolences on his Twitter (X) account over the earthquake disaster in Nepal’s Jajarkot and Rukum districts on November 3, 2023, which have costed over 120 lives and injured over 100 people. Here’s the Prime Minister’s post:
“My deepest condolences to the families of the victims and those injured by the earthquake in Jajarkot and Rukum, Nepal on Friday (November 3). We stand in solidarity with the people of Nepal and wish a swift recovery of the victims and speedy restoration of the affected areas.” (https://twitter.com/thavisin/status/1720726455179776420?s=46…)
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister conveyed his moral support to the Nepalese Government and people for fast recovery of the incident.
