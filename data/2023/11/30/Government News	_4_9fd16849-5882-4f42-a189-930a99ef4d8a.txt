PM pleased with extension of truce between Israel and Hamas
November 30, 2023, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister
and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin was pleased with the mediation between Israel and
Hamas to extend the ceasefire in their war from 4 to 6 days, and according to the latest
agreement today, for another more day (the 7 th day). He hopes that all parties to the conflict
end all acts of violences soon in order to reach the complete ceasefire and to allow
humanitarian assistance and access to those in need.
The Prime Minister is of the view that an extension of truce between Israel and Hamas is a
good sign, and hopes for the immediate release of all civilian hostages, including Thai nationals.
He affirmed that Thailand would continue its effort to assist and negotiate for the safe release
of all the Thai nationals.
