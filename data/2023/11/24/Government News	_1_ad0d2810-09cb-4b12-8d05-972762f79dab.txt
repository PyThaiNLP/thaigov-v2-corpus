PM emphasizes “Can Do Attitude” and commends Team Thailand’s teamwork
November 23, 2023, at 1030hrs, at Wites Smosorn, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin attended the presentation on “Targeted Countries for Proactive Economic Diplomacy” held at the 2023 Meeting of Thai Ambassadors and Consuls-General, held during November 19-24, 2023. Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed gist as follows:
Upon arrival, the Prime Minister took photos with Thai Ambassadors, Consul-Generals, Commercial Attaché, and Investment Attaché to countries around the world who attended the meeting, and commended comprehensiveness of the presentation. He mentioned the Ministry of Commerce’s summary on 7Cs, which are Common Goal / Customer-centric / Co-creation / Cooperation / Connectivity / Care / Can do, and emphasized that obstacles in work could be resolved with the “Can Do Attitude”. He expressed confidence that Thailand’s potential could shine at the global stage through this “Can Do Attitude”.
The Prime Minister would like Ministry of Foreign Affairs to be able to perform its duty at the global level to perfection and to see the new generations be teamed up to enhance the Ministry’s work efficiency. He also commended Team Thailand’s teamwork and entrusted the country’s future in their hands.
