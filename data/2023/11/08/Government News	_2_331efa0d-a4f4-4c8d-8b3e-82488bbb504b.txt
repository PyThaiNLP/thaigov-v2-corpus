PM to reveal the government performance during the past two months in "Chance of Possibility" on November 9, 2023. 1900 hrs
November 8, 2023, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister and Minister of Finance H.E. Mr. Srettha Thavisin is preparing to present the performance of the government during the past two months in the special program "Chance of Possibility, From Policy to Actual Action Under the Government Administration of Prime Minister Settha Thavisin" on November 9, 2023, at 1900 hrs. via TV channels and social media: NBT2HD, NBT11, Facebook, Youtube, Facebook เศรษฐา ทวีสิน - Srettha Thavisin, and Facebook ไทยคู่ฟ้า (ThaigovSpokesman)
The Prime Minister will reveal the 60-day government performance and the results impact to improve people’s life. These also contain the progress and challenges of urgent issues for people with short-term, medium-term, and long-term policies. The Prime Minister will also emphasize "Three Quick Wins": reduce expenses, increase income, and expand opportunities, which are the core of the government's efforts to stimulate the economy and reduce inequality to enhance the quality of life of the Thai people.
