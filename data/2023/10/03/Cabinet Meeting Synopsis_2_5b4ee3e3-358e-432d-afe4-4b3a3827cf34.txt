รัฐบาลไทย-ข่าวทำเนียบรัฐบาล-The cabinet met on September 26, 2023.


วันอังคารที่ 3 ตุลาคม 2566
The cabinet met on September 26, 2023.
Some of the resolutions are as follows:
Title: Overview of Economic and Trade Indices for August 2023
The cabinet acknowledged the Overview of Economic and Trade Indices for August 2023, as proposed by Ministry of Commerce.
Gist
According to the report,
Consumer Price Index (CPI) in August 2023: 108.41 (+0.88%YoY)
Headline inflation rate: +0.88%MoM
Core inflation rate: +0.79%YoY
Average CPI over the last 8 months (January – August 2023): +2.01%AoA
Consumer Confidence Index (CCI): 53.4
Title: Approval of Government’s contingency fund for emergency to support promotional scheme for electric vehicle (cars and motorcycles) use
The cabinet approved the budget under Government’s contingency fund for emergency, for the total amount of 1,024,414,000 Baht, to support promotional scheme for electric vehicle (cars and motorcycles) use, as proposed by Ministry of Finance. This is to ensure measure implementation efficiency on investment in EVs manufacturing.
Title: Approval of Government’s contingency fund for emergency (Ministry of Interior)
The cabinet approved the budget under Government’s contingency fund for emergency, for the total amount of 541,096,800 Baht, to be allocated to Bangkok Metropolitan Administration for the following:
Elderly pension: 518,718,200 Baht
Support grant for people with disabilities: 22,378,600 Baht.
This is as proposed by Ministry of Interior.
