​PM expresses condolences over passing of 18th Sheikhul Islam of the Kingdom of Thailand
Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin expressed profound condolences over the passing of H.E. Mr. Aziz Phitakkumpon (انا لله وانا اليه راجعون), the 18th Sheikhul Islam of the Kingdom of Thailand.
The website of Sheikhul Islam Office has announced that following his medical treatment under Royal Patronage at Chulalongkorn Hospital, the 18th Sheikhul Islam returned to the mercy of Allah at 1032hrs on October 22, 2023. The Janazah Prayer in absentia (Solat Al Ghaib) for the 18th Sheikhul Islam of Thailand was held at 1000hrs, on October 23, 2023, at the central mosque of Songkhla province.
