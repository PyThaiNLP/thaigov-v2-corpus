PM chairs alms-giving ceremony on occasion of “Navamindra Maharaj Day”
October 13, 2023, at 0730hrs, at Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, Bangkok, Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin and spouse Dr. Pakpilai Thavisin presided over the alms-giving ceremony on occasion of the “Navamindra Maharaj Day”, the anniversary of His Majesty late King Bhumibol’s passing. Participated in the ceremony were privy councilors and spouses, President of the Supreme Court and spouse, President of the Senate, presidents of constitutional organizations and spouses, cabinet members and spouses, Prime Minister’s Secretary General, officials under the Royal Household Offices, Bangkok Governor, military and police chiefs, high-level government officials, and BMA Permanent Secretary.
Upon arrival at Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, the Prime Minister and spouse lit candle and incense sticks to pay homage to the Triple Gems, and laid the golden offerings in front of His Majesty’s portrait. Following the chanting by 10 senior monks, he and the participants presented the monks with the triple robes and offerings. He, then, paid homage to the Triple Gems, and His Majesty’s portrait, and led the participants to give alms to 189 monks to end the ceremony.
