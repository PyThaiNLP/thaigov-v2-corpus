​Rice export during first two months of 2023 increases by 38.2%
April 19, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed an overview of Thai rice export situation during the first two months of 2023, as Thailand remains world’s 2nd largest rice exporter with export volume of over 1.4 million tons, an increase of 38.2%yoy.
According to the report of Ministry of Commerce, in collaboration with the Customs Department, during January and February 2023, Thailand exported rice, worth 25,404.15 million Baht (USD 753.9 million), an increase of 38.2% comparing to the same period of 2022 (18,376.8 million Baht). Major markets include the United States, Indonesia, Iraq, Bangladesh and Senegal, among others. Thailand remains world’s 2nd largest rice exporter behind India.
Thai Rice Exporters Association forecasted that rice export volume in March 2023 be approx. 700,000-730,000 tons due to an increasing demand in the global market. The most exported Thai rice are white rice and parboiled rice.
The Government Spokesperson affirmed the Government’s commitment to continue to promote Thai rice export, through showcasing Thai rice at international food fairs and expos, seeking new trade opportunities, and maintaining existing markets. Not only rice, concerned agencies also strive to promote other agricultural products and innovations.
