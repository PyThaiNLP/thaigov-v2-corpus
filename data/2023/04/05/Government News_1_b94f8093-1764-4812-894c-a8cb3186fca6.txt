Cabinet approves waiving of toll fee during Songkran Holiday
April 4, 2023, Deputy Government Spokesperson Traisuree Taisaranakul disclosed that the cabinet has made approval to an exemption of toll fee for Burapha Withi Expressway and Motorway No. 9 (Kanchanaphisek Rd.: Bang Phli – Suk Sawat) during Songkran Holiday of 2023. Toll fee will be waived from 0001hrs of April 12, 2023 to 2400hrs of April 18, 2023, a total of 7 days.
Waiving of toll fee is aimed to facilitate people’s traveling during the long holiday. Ministry of Transport estimates that a total of 2,578,394 vehicles (approx. 368,342 vehicles/day) will be using Burapha Withi Expressway and Motorway No. 9 during the 7 days of fee waiving, which will cost Expressway Authority of Thailand (EXAT) a total revenue of 105.22 million Baht.
However, benefits will be gained from the decrease of vehicle-usage expenses and time-saving cost, which is estimated at 123.98 million Baht (Burapha Withi Expressway: 51.19 million Baht, and Motorway No. 9: 72.79 million Baht).
