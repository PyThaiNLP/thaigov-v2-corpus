New MOU on cooperation between EECO and Korea’s KOTRA approved by cabinet
April 4, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government
Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that the cabinet approved a new MOU on the
cooperation between the Eastern Economic Corridor Office of Thailand (EECO) and Korea
Trade-Investment Promotion Agency (KOTRA), and authorized EECO Secretary-General or a
representative as signatory of the MOU.
The new MOU on the cooperation between the Eastern Economic Corridor Office of Thailand
(EECO) and Korea Trade-Investment Promotion Agency (KOTRA) will be a framework for
promotion of business and industrial cooperation between Thailand and South Korea in the
Eastern Economic Corridor (EEC), especially in hi-tech industries, i.e., automation and robotics,
advanced agriculture, bio-technology, digital, development, and smart cities. Scope of
cooperation includes encouraging private sectors of the two countries to seek business
opportunities in the EEC for mutual interest, sharing and exchanging useful and updated
information, and providing support to concerned projects and companies. Upon signing, the
MOU will take effect for 3 years.
According to the Government Spokesperson, EECO and KOTRA have forged close cooperation
on hi-tech industy development and investment in the EEC since 2019 under the first MOU,
which expired on November 25, 2022.
