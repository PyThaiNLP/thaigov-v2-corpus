“Ministry of Commerce’s Price Reduction for the People” campaign helps save over 16,498 million Baht in its 3-year implementation
April 15, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed about “Ministry of Commerce’s Price Reduction for the People” campaign which was implemented for 3 years since 2020 to alleviate people’s plight on cost of living as a result of the COVID-19 situation. Under the campaign, manufacturers, retailers/wholesalers, department stores, local malls, online shopping platforms, and convenient stores offered products on sale, both offline and online. The campaign was implemented during March 2020 and January 2023, under which the price of 33,485,560 pieces of product and 1,674 items of services were reduced by 32-87%. Most products were consumer products, e.g., food, raw materials, vegetables, fruits, rice, agro products, meat, school supplies, etc. The campaign helped save over 16,498 million Baht.
According to the Government Spokesperson, “Ministry of Commerce’s Price Reduction for the People” campaign helped alleviate people’s plight, especially the vulnerability groups, and stimulate domestic spending during the difficult time.
