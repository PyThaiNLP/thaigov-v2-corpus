PM delivers statement on AH 1444 Idul Fitri
April 20, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed the statement of Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, delivered on occasion of AH 1444 Idul Fitri to convey well wishes and love to the Muslim Thais in the country and abroad.
Idul Fitri marks the end of the Holy Month of Ramadan and accomplishment of the Muslims in performing their religious duty with pure mind and faith in Allāh. The Prime Minister respected their strong will and forbearance in fasting to refrain from bad deeds of all kinds, and treat others with kindness and love, a truly beautiful and loving gesture.
On occasion of the AH 1444 Idul Fitri, the Prime Minister asked for the blessing of Allāh for the happiness and healthiness of all Muslims. May they have strong spirit to adhere to faith and virtue for the benefit of oneself, society, and the nation.
