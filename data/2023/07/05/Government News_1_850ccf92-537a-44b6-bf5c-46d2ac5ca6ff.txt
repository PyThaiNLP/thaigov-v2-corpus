PM congratulates first sitting of 26th House of Representatives and election of House Speaker and Deputy Speakers
July 4, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha congratulated the first sitting of the 26th House of Representatives (first annual ordinary session) at the Parliament House, which took place in an orderly manner according to the democratic process.
At the first sitting of the 26th House of Representatives, president and vice-presidents of the House of Representatives were elected. Prachachat party leader (member of the House of Representatives by party list basis) Wan Muhamad Noor Matha was elected as the president of the House of Representatives, whereas Move Forward MP Padipat Santithada and Pheu Thai MP Pichet Chuamuangphan were elected first and second vice-presidents of the House of Representatives respectively.
The Prime Minister wanted the new Government to be formed soon for the benefit of the country, and affirmed his commitment to perform his duty as incumbent Prime Minister and Government’s leader to the best of his ability until the new Government is ready to take the office. He also called on all sides to join forces in promoting love and solidarity for national development, stability, prosperity, and sustainability, and to uphold the nation’s three pillars in advancing the nation for the future of all the Thai people.
