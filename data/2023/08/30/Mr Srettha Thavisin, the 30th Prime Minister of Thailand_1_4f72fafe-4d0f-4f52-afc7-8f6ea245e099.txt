PM Srettha Thavisin meets with Chinese Ambassador to Thailand
August 29, 2023, H.E. Mr. Han Zhiqiang, Ambassador of People’s Republic of China to Thailand, together with Mr. Wu Zhiwu, Minister at the Embassy of the People's Republic of China to Thailand and the delegation, paid a courtesy call on Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin to congratulate him on being appointed as 30th Prime Minister of Thailand. Participating in the meeting were Dr. Prommin Lertsuridej, M.D., Mr. Parnpree Bahiddha-Nukara, Mr. Chakrapong Sangmanee, and Ms. Sarassanun Annopporn.
The Prime Minister affirmed his policy on reinforcing relations between Thailand and China in both economic, social, and cultural dimensions. The two parties also discussed relaxation of visa measures and regulations for Chinese citizens traveling to Thailand to promote investment and tourism, especially during the high season.
The Chinese Ambassador stated that Thailand is China’s important strategic partner, and China stands ready to cooperate and support the Thai Government under the leadership of Pheu Thai Party. He also extended an invitation for the Prime Minister to pay an official visit to China, and meet with President Xi Jinping to discuss economic development and cooperation.
