​PM thanks cabinet members and government officials at his last cabinet meeting
​PM thanks cabinet members and government officials at his last cabinet meeting
August 29, 2023, Deputy Government Spokesperson Rachada Dhanadirek disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha expressed sincere appreciation toward members of the cabinet, MPs, and all government officials for their dedication and hard work throughout the past 4 years, which has been the driving force for changes. He would forever cherish the memory when he had met with all these good peers and friends.
The government has laid out development foundations in many areas for the country to achieve security, prosperity, and sustainability. These foundations are hoped to be taken forward by the new Government. He would extend his moral support to everyone who works for the benefit of Thailand and its people. The Prime Minister took the opportunity to invite the power of the Triple Gems and the prestige of His Majesty King Rama X and all the past kings to protect everyone, before taking photos with members of the cabinet, and government executives and officials.
