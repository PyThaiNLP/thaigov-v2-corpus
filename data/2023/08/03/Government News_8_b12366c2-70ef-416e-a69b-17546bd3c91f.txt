First Thailand – Cambodia cargo train, route Map Ta Phut – Khlong Leuk – Poipet – Phnom Penh, officially launched
August 2, 2023, Deputy Government Spokesperson Ratchada Thanadirek disclosed that concerned public and private sectors from Thailand and Cambodia have recently attended the launching ceremony of the first Thailand-Cambodia cargo train at Khlong Leuk railway station in Aranya Prathet district, Sakaeo province. The cargo train development project (Map Ta Phut – Khlong Leuk – Poipet – Phnom Penh route) has been implemented by the State Railway of Thailand as part of Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha’s key policy to promote rail transport development and connectivity with ASEAN’s rail transport networks.
According to the Deputy Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister has placed importance on the development of seamless and multimodal transport networks and logistics to enhance the country’s trade competitiveness and increase transport options. He believes that the cargo rail route would increase logistics efficiency and benefit both Thailand and Cambodia.
