​PM to visit firework explosion site in Narathiwat
August 3, 2023, Deputy Government Spokesperson Ratchada Thanadirek disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha will fly to Narathiwat on July 4, 2023 (Friday) to visit firework explosion site in Munoh subdistrict, Sungai Kolok district, Narathiwat province, and give moral support to affected victims of the incident which took place on July 29, 2023. His schedule is as follows:
The Prime Minister and delegation will leave Bangkok at 0715hrs to arrive in Narathiwat at 0915hrs. He will, then, travel to the incident command center, situated right at the explosion site, to observe the rescue operation, and give moral support to the staffs and affected victims. The Prime Minister will later go to Sungai Kolok Hospital to pay a visit to the injured before flying back to Bangkok.
According to the Deputy Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister has had great concern over the wellbeing of local people, and has closely followed up on the situation. He also ordered concerned agencies to rush in rescuing and providing relief to the affected victims.
