​World leaders congratulate 30th Thai Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin
In pursuant to the royal announcement on appointment of Thailand’s 30th Prime Minister, world leaders have sent congratulatory messages to the new Thai Prime Minister Srettha Thavisin, and expressed their hope to work closely with the Prime Minister in advancing relations with Thailand under his premiership. These leaders are President
Joe Biden of the United States of America;
H.E. Mr. Sonexay Siphandone, Prime Minister of Lao PDR;
Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong of the Republic of Singapore;
H.E. Dr. Lotay Tshering, Prime Minister of Bhutan;
and Prime Minister Hun Manet of the Kingdom of Cambodia, among others.
The Prime Minister will send back a message to thank the world leaders for their congratulations at the next opportunity.
