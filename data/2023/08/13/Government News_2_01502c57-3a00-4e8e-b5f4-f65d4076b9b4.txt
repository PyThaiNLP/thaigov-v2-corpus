​PM chairs alms-giving ceremony to commemorate Her Majesty Queen Sirikit the Queen Mother’s birthday anniversary on August 12, 2023
Deputy Government Spokesperson Rachada Dhanadirek disclosed that on August 12, 2023, at 0730hrs, at the Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha and spouse Assoc. Prof. Naraporn Chan-o-cha presided over the alms-giving ceremony to commemorate Her Majesty Queen Sirikit the Queen Mother’s birthday anniversary on August 12, 2023. Participating in the ceremony were president and members of the Privy Council and spouses, president of the Supreme Court and spouse, president of the Senate, chiefs of organizations under constitution and spouses, members of the cabinet and spouses, staffs of the Royal Office, Prime Minister’s Secretary General, governor of Bangkok Metropolitan Administration and spouse, Commanders-in-chief and spouses, chief of the Royal Thai Police and spouse, and Permanent Secretary to the Prime Minister’s Office.
Upon arrival, the Prime Minister and spouse lit candle and incense sticks to pay homage to the Triple Gems, and laid the golden offerings in front of Her Majesty’s portrait. Following the chanting by 10 senior monks, he and the participants presented the monks with the triple robes and offerings. He, then, paid homage to the Triple Gems, and Her Majesty’s portrait, and led the participants to give alms to 192 monks to end the ceremony.
