PM pleased with increasing number of new business registration
Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha
Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha was
reported on an increase of new business registration in April 2023 by the Department of
Business Development, and commended concerned agencies for the success.
According to the report, 6,041 applications have been recorded for new partnership and
company limited registration in April 2023, with total capital worth 20,897.87 million Baht, an
increase of 12.37%YoY. Top 3 businesses requested for registration are real estate business
(434) which accounts for 7.19%, general building construction business (405), accounting for
6.70%, and restaurant business (262), accounting for 4.34%. Number of new business
registration in the first 4 months of 2023 is recorded at 32,223, the highest in 10 years.
The Prime Minister is confident in Thailand’s economic potential and opportunity for further
growth. An increasing number of business registration reflects the country’s success in
promoting ease of doing business and conducive business environment in a bid to boost
confidence of all concerned sectors and advance national economic development in a stable and
sustainable manner.
