PM presents flower offerings in front of statue of Her Royal Highness Princess Galyani Vadhana Krom Luang Naradhiwas Rajanagarindra
May 6, 2023, at 0815hrs, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha and Associate Professor Naraporn Chan-o-cha, spouse, presented flower offerings in front of the statue of Her Royal Highness Princess Galyani Vadhana Krom Luang Naradhiwas Rajanagarindra at Princess Galyani Vadhana Institute of Music, Bangkok. Attending the flower-offering ceremony were president of the Senate, president of the Supreme Court and spouse, and representatives of concerned agencies.
Upon arrival, the Prime Minister and spouse, president of the Senate, and president of the Supreme Court and spouse bowed to pay respect to Her Royal Highness Princess Galyani Vadhana Krom Luang Naradhiwas Rajanagarindra, then, presented flower offerings in front of Her Royal Highness’ statue, and bowed again.
The Prime Minister and participants, then, visited an exhibition in honor of Her Royal Highness at Princess Galyani Vadhana Institute of Music to end the ceremony.
