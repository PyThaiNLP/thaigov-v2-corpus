​PM’s statement on 2023 Labor Day
May 1, 2023, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha delivered a statement via the Radio Thailand on occasion of the 2023 Labor Day. Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed gist of his statement as follows:
The Prime Minister conveyed his regards to all the workers who have been an integral part of national development effort. Their quality of life must be elevated in order to enhance resilience. They also need to be upskilled/reskilled in response to the market demand. The Prime Minister, on behalf of the Thai Government, appreciated the Thai workers and all concerned sectors for their commitment to enhance capacity and skills to become national economic driving forces, and the country’s quality and skilled workforces in both the industrial, agricultural, business, and service sectors. Their dedication and commitment greatly contribute to the national advancement and resilience amidst all the global crises.
The Prime Minister invited the virtue of all sacredness, and the royal prestige of His Majesty the King and Her Majesty the Queen to bless all the Thai workers happiness and prosperity with strong physical and mental health in order to remain an integral part in advancing the nation toward security and sustainability.
