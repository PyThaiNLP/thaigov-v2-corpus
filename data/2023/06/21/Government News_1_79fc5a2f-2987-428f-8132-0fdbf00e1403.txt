Budget approved for subsidy to UN’s “Regular Budget” for 2023
June 20, 2023, Deputy Government Spokesperson Traisuree Taisaranakul disclosed that the
cabinet acknowledged a resolution of the Election Commission (EC), in its meeting dated May
30, 2023, on an approval of budget from the Government’s contingency fund for emergency
(FY2023), for the total amount of 222.95 million Baht, to subsidize United Nations (UN)’s
“Regular Budget” for 2023.
Thailand is a UN member state. Earlier on April 25, 2023, upon approving the budget from the
Government’s contingency fund for emergency for the total amount of 222.95 million Baht to
Ministry of Foreign Affairs to be used as a subsidy to UN’s “Regular Budget” for 2023, the
cabinet instructed that the matter be proceeded according to Section 169(3) of the Constitution
of the Kingdom of Thailand, which prescribes that the outgoing cabinet may give approval to
the expenditure of budget reserved for an emergency only through prior approval of the
Election Commission.
