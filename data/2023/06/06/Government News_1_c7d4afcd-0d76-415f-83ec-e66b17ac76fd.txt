“Amazing Thai Taste Festival” to be held in Rayong, Chiang Mai, and Udon Thani
June 2, 2023, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Tourism Authority of Thailand (TAT), in collaboration with related private sector and agencies, will organize the gastronomic event “Amazing Thai Taste Festival” in 3 provinces, namely, Rayong, Chiang Mai, and Udon Thani, to attract both domestic and foreign tourists, and to promote the country’s gastronomy tourism. The event, which will be held during June and August 2023, is believed to boost local tourism and attract not less than 40,000 tourists per event area, generating over 50 million Baht. Attendance is free of charge.
“Amazing Thai Taste Festival” has been held consecutively since 2015, and is a favorite event among Thai and international food lovers. The event theme for 2023 is “Food Lover Paradise”. Schedule of the “2023 Amazing Thai Taste Festival” is as follows:
• Rayong: During June 3-5, 2023, at 1600-2300hrs at Rayong Central Department Store
• Chiang Mai: During July 28-30, 2023, at 1600-2300hrs at Chiang Mai Central Festival Department Store
• Udon Thani: During August 25-27, 2023, at 1600-2300hrs at Udon Thani Central Department Store
The event features famous restaurants and food across the country, cooking demonstration, discussion forum on gastronomy tourism and local tourism routes in search for raw food ingredients, and other recreational activities. Those who are interested may find further information at: Facebook Fanpage: Amazing Thai Taste Festival or TAT Contact Center call 1672.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister thanked all concerned sectors and agencies for continuing to highlight Thai food as one of the country’s soft powers and identities. Thailand’s food culture, with its abundance and diversity, could easily be built upon to promote gastronomy tourism at an international level.
