PM and spouse chairs floral offering ceremony to bless Her Majesty the Queen
PM and spouse chairs floral offering ceremony to bless Her
Majesty the Queen
June 3, 2023, at 2009hrs, at Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, Bangkok, Prime Minister and Defense
Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, together with spouse Assoc. Prof. Naraporn Chan-o-cha,
presided over the ceremony of homage offering and glory praising in celebration of Her Majesty
the Queen’s birthday anniversary on June 3, 2023. Attending the ceremony were President of
the Senate, President of the Supreme Court, presidents of constitutional organizations and
spouses, cabinet members and spouses, high-level government officials, military, police,
civilians, private and people sectors.
The Prime Minister and all the participants took their position, paid homage to Her Majesty’s
portrait, and presented traditional cone-shaped floral and homage offerings before delivering a
commemorative speech to praise Her Majesty the Queen. He, then, led all the participants to
bow and pay homage to Her Majesty’s portrait again, followed by singing the Royal Anthems
and chanting “Long Live the Queen” 3 times to complete the ceremony.
