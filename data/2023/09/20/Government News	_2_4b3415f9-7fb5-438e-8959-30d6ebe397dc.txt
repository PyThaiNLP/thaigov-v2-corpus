​PM meets with CEO of BlackRock
September 19, 2023, at 1130hrs (New York, U.S.A. local time), Government Spokesperson Chai Wacharonke disclosed that Mr. Larry Fink, CEO of BlackRock, Inc., World’s leading asset management and investment company, based in New York City, paid a courtesy call on Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin to discuss business expansion in Thailand, especially in promoting clean energy, and investing in the Thai Government’s Sustainability Linked Bond.
BlackRock is world’s largest asset management company, with the total asset of US$9.43 trillion under its management (as of the end of 2nd quarter of 2023). A number of Thailand’s large-scale funds, i.e., Social Security Fund, and Government Pension Fund, are also investing through BlackRock's Exchange Traded Fund (ETF). The company places priority on sustainability investing, and is interest in investing in Sustainability Linked Bond to be issued by the Thai Government next year. The CEO of BlackRock also commended the Southeast Asian region, including Thailand, as high-potential region BlackRock plans to continue investing in.
The Prime Minister is satisfied with the meeting outcome which would lead to BlackRock’s tangible and sustainable investment in Thailand, especially in the Government’s Sustainability Linked Bond.
