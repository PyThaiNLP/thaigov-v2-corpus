PM: Delegation of responsibilities among deputy prime ministers determined based on people’s interest
September 14, 2023, at 1030hrs, at Bhakdi Bodin Building, Government House, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Srettha Thavisin disclosed about the delegation of responsibilities among deputy prime ministers, according to which Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Commerce Phumtham Wechayachai, and not Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Natural Resources and Environment Pol. Gen. Phatcharavat Wongsuwan, is delegated to oversee Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives. The Prime Minister insisted that the Government is the non-partisan Government of the Thai people even though it is composed of a total of 11 coalition parties. It works for the best interest of the people. Delegation of responsibilities was done based on appropriateness and relevancy as Deputy Prime Minister Phumtham Wechayachai would be able to oversee agriculture and commerce in parallel.
