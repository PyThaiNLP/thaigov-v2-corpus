​PM to chair Loy Kratong celebration at Khlong Padung Krung Kasem Canal
November 7, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha will chair Loy Kratong celebration under the theme: “New Normal Loy Kratong: Carrying on Thai Tradition Toward Sustainability” to be held on November 8, 2022 at 1600hrs, at Khlong Padung Krung Kasem Canal adjacent to the Government House. Participating in the celebration were members of the cabinet. The event is organized with an aim to take forward Thai culture and tradition, and pass on cultural wisdom to the new generations. Loy Kratong celebration at Khlong Padung Krung Kasem Canal will feature Kratong-making demonstration and display, cultural performances, and floating market, among others.
The Government Spokesperson added that the Prime Minister instructed all concerned agencies and authorities in each locality to prioritize safety in organizing and monitoring the Loy Kratong event. Measures must be prepared to accommodate a large number of people and ensure safety and orderliness, while piers and wharfs must be safety checked. Loy-Kratong goers are strongly advised to continue observing COVID-19 preventive measures, i.e., social distancing, washing hands, and wearing masks. They are also encouraged to dress in Thai traditional outfits and use floating kratong that is made from natural and biodegradable materials.
