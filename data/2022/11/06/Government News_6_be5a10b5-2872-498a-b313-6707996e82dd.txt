​Thai women and women associations invited to take part in hosting WEF and HLPDWE under APEC framework
September 2, 2022, Deputy Government Spokesperson Tipanan Sirichana disclosed about the Government’s policies on women empowerment, which are handed down to Department of Women's Affairs and Family Development, Ministry of Social Development and Human Security under the leadership of Minister Juti Krairiksh, that a number of policies have been implemented to promote women’s role in various dimensions, including international women’s affairs.
As host of APEC 2022, Thailand is scheduled to host APEC Women and the Economy Forum (WEF) and High-Level Policy Dialogue on Women and the Economy (HLPDWE) on September 7, 2022 at the Icon Siam, Bangkok. The forum is aimed to promote post-COVID-19 women’s economic empowerment in the digital era. The concept of Bio-Circular- Green- Economy Model (BCG) will be adopted to ensure balanced and inclusive growth. Focus will be made on the implementations and initiatives under “La Serena Roadmap for Women and Inclusive Growth”, and networking of Thai women BCG entrepreneur in APEC to promote their opportunities at the regional level.
According to the Deputy Government Spokesperson, all Thai women and women associations are encouraged to take part in hosting the event in order to promote women’s economic empowerment that is environmentally friendly, reduce disparity and poverty, and upgrade their quality of life.
