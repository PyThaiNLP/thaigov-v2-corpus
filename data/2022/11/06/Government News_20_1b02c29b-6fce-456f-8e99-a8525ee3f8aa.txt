​Govt Spokesperson: Gen. Prayut will respect Constitutional Court’s verdict no matter what it will be
September 14, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed about the decision of the Constitutional Court today to convene on September 30, 2022 for the verdict on Minister of Defense Gen. Prayut Chan-o-Cha’s 8-year term limit as prime minister that, Gen. Prayut has reaffirmed his faith on the judicial system, and would respect the Court’s ruling no matter what it will be.
He also thanked the people for their moral support. As Minister of Defense, Gen. Prayut has sedulously performed duty to support the Government. Especially in the current situation where the people are badly impacted by flood and inundation, he has ordered the military and defense agencies to promptly deploy personnel to alleviate people’s plight in Bangkok and other areas throughout the country.
