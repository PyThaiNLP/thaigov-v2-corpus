Cabinet approves cooking gas subsidy for low-income earners
October 18, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting today, approved 302.5 million Baht in budget from the Government’s central fund for contingency and emergency to the Department of Energy Business.
The budget will be used to subsidize cooking gas expenses for approx. 5.5 million state welfare card holders. The 100 baht per month subsidy will be given for 3 months through the state welfare card to alleviate the burden of low-income earners during October 25 and December 31, 2022.
