Govt continues to promote gender equality and women’s empowerment
September 28, 2022, Deputy Government Spokesperson Ratchada Thanadirek disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting on September 27, has made approval in principle to the Joint Statement of the ASEAN - U.S. Ministerial Dialogue on Gender Equality and Women's Empowerment, to be held on September 29, 2022 via a teleconference. This is as proposed by Ministry of Social Development and Human Security.
The draft Joint Statement of the ASEAN - U.S. Ministerial Dialogue on Gender Equality and Women's Empowerment prescribes: 1) the importance of gender equality, women’s empowerment, related international laws, and eradication of violence against women and children; 2) promotion of women’s economic security and entrepreneurship, and their participation in the ASEAN workforce and market; 3) implementation according to the ASEAN Gender Mainstreaming Strategic Framework (2021-2025), ASEAN Committee on Women’s roadmap, and other related ASEAN frameworks; 4) building of conducive and safe environment for women’s economic empowerment and investment; and 5) the importance to eliminate gender violence.
