​Deputy PM to chair policy committee for national image enhancement through soft power
August 29, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed about the 206/2565 Order of the Prime Minister’s Office, dated August 19, 2022, on appointment of a policy committee for national image enhancement through soft power, with Deputy Prime Minister Supattanapong Punmeechaow as chair, and Minister of Culture as vice chair. Members of the committee include concerned permanent secretaries, i.e., permanent secretaries to Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Tourism and Sports, Ministry of Commerce, Ministry of Culture, Ministry of Digital Economy and Society, Ministry of Higher Education, Science, Research and Innovation, and secretary general of National Economic and Social Development Council, as well as representatives from the private sector, such as director-general of Thailand Development Research Institute (TDRI), president of Federation of Thai Industries, president of Chamber of Commerce and Board of Trade of Thailand, and president of Digital Council of Thailand. The policy committee for national image enhancement through soft power is authorized and tasked to determine goals and strategic plans for the development of the country’s soft power, and national image enhancement.
According to the acting Government Spokesperson, the Government strives to promote Thailand’s soft power as a new driving force for economic growth and international trade promotion. The policy committee for national image enhancement through soft power will mobilize an integrative effort of the public and private sectors to promote and generate genuine economic value of the Thai soft power.
