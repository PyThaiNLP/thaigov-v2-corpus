Title: Draft Royal Decree on land expropriation in parts of Sa Kaeo province (Muang district’s Sa Kaeo and THa Kasemsubdistricts), B.E. …
The cabinet approved the draft Royal Decree on l
and expropriation in parts of Sa Kaeo province (Muang district’s Sa Kaeo and THa Kasemsubdistricts), B.E. …, as proposed by Ministry of Transport. Ministry of Transport shall take comments of Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives, Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment, and Official of Council of State into consideration.
Gist
The Royal Decree prescribes areas in Sa Kaeoprovince (Muang district’s Sa Kaeo and THaKasem subdistricts) to be expropriated for construction and expansion of a local road (route Kor.) Land survey and appropriation shall commence within 180 days after the Royal Decree is in effect.

Title: Report on the Industrial Economics Status in June 2022
The cabinet acknowledged Industrial Economic Report for June 2022, as proposed by Ministry of Industry.
Gist
Based on the report, manufacturing production index (MPI) contracted by 0.1% YOY due to the impact from the contracting global supply chain which has resulted in shortage of raw materials or parts.
Key industries that contributed to the contractionwere hard disk drive (-16.72%), plastic beads (-15.35%), and iron and steel (-16.72%)
Key industries that have expanded well in June 2022 were petroleum refinery (+18.53%), and air conditioner (+21.26%).

Title: Request for Government’s contingency fund (fund for emergency) FY2022 for the operation of the Royal Thai Police’s Security Emergency Operation Center during February 1- March 31, 2022 (59 days)
The cabinet approved the budget framework of146.94 million Baht under the Government’s contingency fund (fund for emergency) FY2022for the expenses related to mitigation of the COVID-19 situation, incurred during February 1- March 31, 2022, under the operation of the Royal Thai Police’s Security Emergency Operation Center. This is as proposed by the Royal Thai Police.

Title: Approval of documents for the 54th ASEAN Economic Ministers' meeting (AEM) and related meetings
The cabinet approved 12 documents for the 54th ASEAN Economic Ministers' meeting (AEM) and related meetings, to be held in Cambodia during September 11-18, 2022.
The 12 documents are:
1.​Protocol to Amend the ASEAN Agreement on the Movement of Natural Persons
2.​The Twenty-First AEM-MOFCOM Consultation Joint Statement on Cooperation for Post-COVID Economic Recovery
3.​The Transposed ATIGA Product Specific Rules (PSRs) and its Attachment of Textile Single List in AHTN 2022
4.​The Transposed ATIGA List of Information Technology Agreement (ITA) Products in AHTN 2022
5.​Implementation Plan for the Framework for Circular Economy for the ASEAN Economic Community 2023 – 2030
6.​Work Programme on Further Deepening ASEAN -China Trade Economic Cooperation 2022 – 2026
7.​Joint Feasibility Study Report to Further Enhance the ASEAN-China Free Trade Area
8.​ASEAN-EU Trade and Investment Work Programme 2022 – 2023
9.​ASEAN Plus Three Economic Cooperation Work Programme 2023 – 2024
10.​ASEAN-UK Work Plan to Implement the ASEAN-UK Joint Ministerial Declaration on Future Economic Cooperation
11.​2022 - 2023 ASEAN - U.S. Trade and Investment Framework Arrangement (TIFA) and Expanded Economic Engagement (E3) Workplan
12.​Assessment Report of the ASEAN Economic Community (AEC) Fact-Finding Mission (FFM) to Timor-Leste
At the 54th AEM, outcome of the ASEAN-Australia-New Zealand Free Trade Agreement (AANZFTA) negotiation will be disclosed, and the joint committee under ASEAN-India Trade Agreement will be established.
