​Cabinet approves MRTA’s land rent by SAT for construction of youth sports center
Deputy Government Spokesperson Traisuree Taisaranakul disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting on September 6, 2022, has made approval for the Mass Rapid Transit Authority of Thailand (MRTA) to rent out part of its Huay Khwang Depot to the Sports Authority of Thailand for the construction of sports center for youths.
According to the Deputy Government Spokesperson, the Sports Authority of Thailand (SAT) has been allocated the FY2022 budget for the total amount of 33.5 million Baht to construct a youth sports center, and made an agreement with MRTA to rent out the area of 1,867.29 square meters or 466.82 square wah of MRTA’s Huay Khwang Depot. The rental fee is agreed at 124,290.83 per month (VAT excluded). SAT will subsidize land and building taxes, while MRTA will prepare a monitoring plan to ensure safety of its electrical infrastructure. Under the agreement, the rental period is not specified, but there is a conditional clause prescribing that SAT return the land to MRTA by a specific period if the latter wants to use the land to expand its service system and operation.
