Govt expresses profound condolence over Queen Elizabeth II’s passing
September 9, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri conveyed the Thai Government’s condolences over the passing of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II, following a statement from Buckingham Palace on September 8, 2022 (local time) confirming Her Majesty’s passing at the age of 96 at her residence in Balmoral Castle, Scotland.
“The Thai Government would like to express profound condolences over the passing of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II who was widely admired and revered by people around the world.”
According to the Government Spokesperson, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Prime Minister Gen. Prawit Wongsuwon has ordered public agencies and state enterprises to fly flag at half staff for 3 days on September 9,12,13 2022 and will make a visit to the UK Embassy in Thailand to sign a book of condolences.
