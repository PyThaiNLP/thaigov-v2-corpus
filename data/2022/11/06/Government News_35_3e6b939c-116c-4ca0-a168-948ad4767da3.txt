​Deputy PM Prawit Wongsuwon thanks retired officials for their dedication and work for the country
Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that today (September 29, 2022), at 1000hrs, at the Purple Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House, Deputy Secretary-General Nicha Hiranburana Thuvatham led 15 officials from Office of the Prime Minister’s Secretary-General who will retire on September 30, 2022 to pay a courtesy call on Deputy Prime Minister Gen. Prawit Wongsuwon. Participating in the meeting were executives and political officials of Office of the Prime Minister’s Secretary-General.
The Deputy Prime Minister handed memorabilia to the retired officials, and sincerely thanked them for their dedication and work for the country with perseverance, diligence, and integrity. He also wished them all the best.
