PM pleased with success of 29th APEC Finance Ministers’ Meeting
October 23, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha was pleased with the success of the 29th APEC Finance Ministers’ Meeting (APEC FMM) which was chaired by Minister of Finance Arkhom Termpittayapaisith and held under the theme “Advancing Digitalization, Achieving Sustainability” on October 19-20, 2022 at Queen Sirikit National Convention Center, Bangkok.
At the 29th APEC FMM, APEC economies pledged to address surging inflation and promote free, open, fair, inclusive, non-discriminatory, transparent, and predictable international trade and investment environment, which is conducive to global and regional economic growth. New measures have been explored as revolutionary forces for economic growth, e.g., digitalization, economic-growth sustainability, among others.
According to the Chair’s Statement of the 29th APEC FMM, APEC Finance Ministers have given consensus to promoting sustainable finance, digitalization for digital economy, and implementing the Cebu Action Plan, and emphasized their commitment to implement flexible fiscal policies to support inclusive economic growth and job creation, as well as to use all available policy tools – monetary, fiscal and structural - individually and collectively, to the extent possible, to manage inflationary pressures and steer the global economy back on its growth path
The 29th APEC FMM highlighted the joint effort of APEC member economies to advancing progress towards sustainable development. It was also a good opportunity to discuss the economic and financial outlook faced by their economies, secure, efficient, and affordable access to sustainable finance, and application of digital technology for digital economy. They also shared views on appropriate policy actions to achieve tangible economic and environmental goals.
APEC Finance Ministers and participants at the 29th APEC FMM also welcomed the completion of the APEC Policy Considerations for Developing Cross-border Payments and Remittances, which covers key considerations and implementation options for, inter alia, risk management, and consumer protection for developing cross-border payment and remittance linkages.
