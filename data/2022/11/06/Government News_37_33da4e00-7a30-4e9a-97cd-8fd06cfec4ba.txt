Govt Spokesperson discloses construction progress of Automotive and Tyre Testing, Research and Innovation Center
October 3, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed about the construction of Automotive and Tyre Testing, Research and Innovation Center (ATTRIC), which is now over 50% in progress, and is expected to open for full service in 2026, following the cabinet’s resolution in 2016 to approve ATTRIC establishment by Ministry of Industry’s Thai Industrial Standards Institute.
ATTRIC is located in the Eastern Economic Corridor (EEC), and is established with an aim to enhance the country’s automotive industry into Super Cluster for modern vehicle industry, and to upgrade Thailand’s automotive, parts, and tire industries. It will be a learning center that will pass on technology to relevant sectors.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the establishment of ATTRIC will also contribute the Government’s plan to promote Thailand as world’s major EV manufacturing base, employment creation, and technology transfer.
