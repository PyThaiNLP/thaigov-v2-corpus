Govt Spokesperson: Gen. Prayut respects Constitutional Court’s verdict and thanks Thai people for moral support
September 30, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha’s reaction over the ruling of the Constitutional Court on the issue of his 8-year tenure that the Prime Minister respects the court’s verdict, and thanks the Thai people for constant moral support.
Throughout the past month, the Prime Minister took the opportunity to contemplate the need to maximize the rest of the Government’s tenure in pushing forward and completing a number of projects and initiatives, especially mega infrastructure projects, in a bid to transform Thailand toward prosperity and advancement for the future of our next generations.
