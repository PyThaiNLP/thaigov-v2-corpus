​Cabinet approves 5th SME Promotion Master Plan for 2023-2027
September 21, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting on September 20, 2022, made an approval to the 5th SME Promotion Master Plan (2023-2027) as a framework for Thailand’s SMEs development and promotion, and a guideline for the development of SME action and budgeting plans for an integrative operation between all concerned agencies.
The draft 5th SME Promotion Master Plan (2023-2027) identifies 3 areas of development and 15 strategies:
1) Promoting inclusive growth: Strategies under this area of development include maintaining business operation stability during the start of business, enhancing business capacity and competitiveness at the global standard, supporting elderly business operators, promoting business survival, and promoting farmers’ business professionalism, among others.
2) Promoting targeted growth: Strategies under this area of development include increasing domestic market share, and internationalizing businesses, etc.
3) Developing environments conducive to business growth: Strategies under this area of development include promoting access to funding, enhancing personnel and labor skills, revising related laws and regulations, and developing data system, among others.
According to the Government Spokesperson, key strategies added in the 5th SME Promotion Master Plan, comparing to the preceding 4th SME Promotion Plan, are supporting elderly business operators, and promoting farmers’ business professionalism.
