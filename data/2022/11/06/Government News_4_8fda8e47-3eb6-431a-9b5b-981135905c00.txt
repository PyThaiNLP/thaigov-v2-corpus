​Deputy PM Prawit Wongsuwon meets with Malaysian Defense Minister
August 30, 2022, at 15.00 hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, YB Dato’Seri Hishammuddin bin Tun Hussein, Minister of Defense of Malaysia, paid a courtesy call on Deputy Prime Minister Gen. Prawit Wongsuwon, on occasion of his visit to Thailand to attend the expo “Defense & Security 2022”.
The Deputy Prime Minister, Acting Prime Minister welcomed Malaysian Minister of Defense to Thailand, and emphasized that this would be a good opportunity to advance cooperation between the two countries on defense industry. Thailand is pleased with the exchange of visit at all commander levels, which has strengthened cooperation in all dimensions, especially in border cooperation, Southern border province problem solving, and improvement of border people’s wellbeing.
Malaysian Defense Minister congratulated the success of “Defense & Security 2022” which has contributed to the development of national security, and expressed confidence on Thailand’s potential to forge cooperation with Malaysia in other areas, such as people-to-people connectivity, education, and the Eastern Economic Corridor (EEC), for mutual interest.
Both parties also came to terms to further reinforce cooperation on border security under the 3 meeting frameworks, namely, General Border Committee (GBC), High Level Committee (HLC), and Regional Border Committee (RBC).
The Deputy Prime Minister, Acting Prime Minister has recently attended the 55th GBC in May 2022, where border connectivity promotion projects have been agreed upon. Transportation routes will be concretely developed, while other pending projects, such as construction of road linking customs checkpoints of the two countries, and construction of new bridges crossing Golok River, will be pushed forward. Both parties also agreed on the peace talk process in solving the Southern border problem, and in bringing peace and security to the border people of the two countries.
