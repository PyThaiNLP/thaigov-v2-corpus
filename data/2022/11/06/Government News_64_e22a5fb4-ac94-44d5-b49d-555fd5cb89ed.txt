PM meets with Lao PDR PM on occasion of groundbreaking ceremony of 5th Thai–Lao Friendship Bridge
October 28, 2022, at 1000hrs, at the construction site of the 5th Thai–Lao Friendship Bridge (Bueng Kan – Bolikhamxai) in Bolikhamxai, Lao PDR, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha had a bilateral meeting with Prime Minister of Lao PDR Phankham Viphavanh. Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed gist of the meeting as follows:
The Prime Minister was pleased to cochair the groundbreaking ceremony of the 5th Thai–Lao Friendship Bridge (Bueng Kan – Bolikhamxai), which has been implemented according to the earlier agreement made between the two Prime Ministers, and expressed confidence that the bridge would further facilitate people-to-people relations, as the Thai people stands ready to support Laotian friends, and hoped for the same from Lao people.
The Lao PDR Prime Minister was pleased with the connection between Thailand and Lao PDR through the bridge construction. However, even without the bridge, Thai and Laotian people have been heartfelt close and connected, so true to the saying “Having Rice From the Same Field, Eating Fish From the Same Pond”.
According to both the Prime Ministers, the bridge not only symbolizes close friendship, but also contributes to people-to-people relations and goods transport between the two countries, and will promote subregional connectivity between Thailand, Lao PDR, and Vietnam on R8 route. The two Prime Ministers also discussed rail transport connectivity between Thailand’s rail system and the Boten–Vientiane railway, and agreed on close collaboration between concerned agencies of the two countries to minimize obstacles and optimize the rail infrastructure.
The Lao PDR Prime Minister emphasized commitment of both Thailand and Lao PDR to promote and facilitate people-to-people connectivity, trade, investment, and tourism, as well as socio-economic development, for prosperity and the better wellbeing of people of the two countries.
