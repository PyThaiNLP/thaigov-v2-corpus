PM briefed on preparation of 29th APEC Economic Leaders' Meeting and Related Meeting
November 14, 2022, at 10.30 hrs, at the Green Room, Thai KhuFah Building, Government House, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha listened to the briefing from concerned agencies on the preparation of the 29th APEC Economic Leaders' Meeting and Related Meetings. Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed gist as follows:
The Prime Minister thanked relevant agencies for their participation in the ASEAN Summit in Cambodia, where Thailand has proudly played its role at the ASEAN stage. He also expressed confidence on all concerned agencies and staffs who have performed their respective duty to the best of their ability throughout the year in preparing for the 29th APEC Economic Leaders' Meeting and Related Meetings.
With regards to the participants of the 29th APEC Economic Leaders' Meeting and Related Meetings, heads of state of 14 economies, and the representatives of 7 economies (South Korea, Malaysia, Mexico, Peru, Russia, Chinese Taipei [in accordance with the APEC practice], and the U.S.) have confirmed their attendance. Special guests for the 29th APEC Economic Leaders' Meeting and Related Meetings include the Prime Minister of Cambodia, President of France, and Crown Prince and Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia.
The Prime Minister was also briefed on other aspects of the preparation, e.g., key topics and issues of discussion, and expected tangible outcomes, among others. He was confident that the meetings would be a success and that Thailand and the Thai people would benefit from the hosting of the 29th APEC Economic Leaders' Meeting and Related Meetings, especially through tangible economic stimulation.
