​PM to attend 40th and 41st ASEAN Summits and related summits in Phnom Penh during November 10-13
November 7, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha is scheduled to attend the 40th and 41st ASEAN summits and related summits in Phnom Penh, Kingdom of Cambodia on November 10-13, 2022.
The overarching theme of the 40th and 41st ASEAN summits and related summits is “ASEAN A.C.T.: Addressing Challenges Together”, to reflect ASEAN’s collective effort in addressing various challenges, both the COVID-19 pandemic, geopolitical volatility, and international and regional unrest.
The Prime Minister will attend 16 meetings, and will meet with representatives of the ASEAN Inter-Parliamentary Assembly, ASEAN Youth, and the ASEAN Business Advisory Council. He will also attend the 2nd ASEAN Global Dialogue under the topic: “Post-COVID-19 Inclusive Recovery” which will be attended by the ASEAN leaders, dialogue partners, and high-level representatives from international organizations. At the event, the Prime Minister will put forward 3 areas of cooperation, namely, public health security, economic recovery, and digital development in the region.
