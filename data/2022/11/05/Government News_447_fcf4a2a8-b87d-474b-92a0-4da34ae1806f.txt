Cabinet approves 18,447.9800 million Baht for expenditures related to COVID-19, made during April 1- May 15, 2022
August 23, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting on August 23, 2022, has made approval to the budget of 18,447.9800 million Baht, under the Royal Decree authorizing Ministry of Finance to seek additional loan for economic and social problem solving, remedy, and rehabilitation as a result of spread of COVID-19, B.E. 2564, for the 4th public healthcare service scheme of National Health Security Office.
The budget will be allocated for expenditures related to COVID-19 public health services, made during April 1- May 15, 2022, at both public and private medical facilities across the country, i.e., provision medical equipment, medicines, vaccines, R&D, and domestic development of vaccines.
