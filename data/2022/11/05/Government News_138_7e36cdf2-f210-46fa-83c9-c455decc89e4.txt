PM congratulates success of 38th and 39th ASEAN Summit and related meetings
October 28, 2021, at 1530hrs, at Bhakdi Bodin Building, Government House, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha attended Closing and Handing Over Ceremony of ASEAN Chairmanship via a videoconference. Attending the meeting were leaders of the ASEAN member states. Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed gist as follows:
His Majesty Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah of Brunei Darussalam, as chair of the 38th and 39th ASEAN Summit and related meetings, delivered a closing speech at the Closing and Handing Over Ceremony of ASEAN Chairmanship. Brunei Darussalam has handed over ASEAN chairmanship to Cambodia for the year 2022.
The Prime Minister adopted the following 25 documents at the 38th and 39th ASEAN Summit and related meetings:
1. Bandar Seri Begawan Declaration on the Strategic and Holistic Initiative to Link ASEAN Responses to Emergencies and Disasters (ASEAN SHIELD)
2. ASEAN Leaders' Declaration on Upholding Multilateralism
3. ASEAN Leaders' Declaration on the Blue Economy
4. Terms of Reference for the High-Level Task Force on the ASEAN Community's Post-2025 Vision and the Roadmap
5. Consolidated Strategy on the Fourth Industrial Revolution for ASEAN
6. ASEAN Leaders’ Statement on Advancing Digital Transformation in ASEAN
7. ASEAN Comprehensive Framework on Care Economy
8. ASEAN Joint Statement on Climate Change to the 26th Conference of the Parties to the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate
9. ASEAN Strategic Policy Framework on Promoting an Adaptive ASEAN Community of Greater Understanding, Tolerance and a Sense of Regional Agendas among the Peoples of ASEAN
10. Bandar Seri Begawan Declaration on the Importance of the Family for Community Development and Nation-Building
11. ASEAN Declaration on Promoting Competitiveness, Resilience and Agility of Workers for the Future of Work
12. ASEAN Leaders' Declaration on the Reformulation and Production of Healthier Food and Beverage Options
13. Declaration on the Elimination of Bullying of Children in ASEAN
14. Joint Statement of the 22nd ASEAN-Republic of Korea Summit on Advancing ASEAN-Republic of Korea Cooperation
15. ASEAN-China Joint Statement on Cooperation in Support of the ASEAN Comprehensive Recovery Framework
16. ASEAN-China Joint Statement on Strengthening Green and Sustainable Development
17. ASEAN-U.S. Leaders' Statement on Digital Development
18. ASEAN Plus Three Leaders' Statement on Cooperation on Mental Health Amongst Adolescents and Young Children
19. East Asia Summit Leaders' Statement on Sustainable and Green Recovery
20. East Asia Summit Leaders' Statement on Economic Growth through Tourism Recovery
21. East Asia Summit Leaders' Statement on Mental Health Cooperation
22. ASEAN-India Joint Statement on Cooperation on the ASEAN Outlook on the Indo-Pacific (AOIP)
23. Comprehensive Plan of Action to Implement the Association of Southeast Asian Nations and the Russian Federation Strategic Partnership (2021-2025)
24. ASEAN-Russia Statement: Building United, Secure and Sustainable Region
25. Statement of ASEAN and the Russian Federation on Cooperation in the Field of Addressing and Countering the World Drug Problem
