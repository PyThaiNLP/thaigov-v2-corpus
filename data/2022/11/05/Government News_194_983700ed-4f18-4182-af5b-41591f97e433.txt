​PM: All variants of COVID-19 can be prevented if everyone cooperates and takes responsibility for themselves and society
​PM: All variants of COVID-19 can be prevented if everyone cooperates and takes responsibility for themselves and society
December 26, 2021, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha has made an order for all public agencies to closely monitor the COVID-19 situation. Local administrative offices, in particular, are urged to tighten their COVID-19 preventive measures in accordance with the policy set by Center for COVID-19 Situation Administration (CCSA) in providing public services. Technologies should be applied to minimize personal contact that may increase infection risk.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister warned that the war against COVID-19 is not over yet, and everyone must take the matter as a national agenda, and strictly follow related public health measures. All variants of the COVID-19 can be prevented if everyone cooperates and take responsibility for themselves and the society.
