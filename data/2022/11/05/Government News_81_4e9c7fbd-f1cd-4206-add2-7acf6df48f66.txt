Govt Spokesperson: PM denies rumors on House dissolution and cabinet reshuffle
September 1, 2021, at the Sappaya-Sapasathan (Parliament House), Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha’s affirmation that he had not been pressured by factors under the Palang Pracharath Party to undertake cabinet reshuffle after the censure debate, and that there is yet any plan for cabinet reshuffle nor House dissolution. Any recently-spread news and information related to the matters are totally groundless.
The Government Spokesperson called on the public to have confidence in the Government and the Prime Minister, as he has fully committed to lead the nation and help out the people out of the COVID-19 crisis, in a bid for Thailand to move safely and securely toward the future, and for the economy to quickly recovered.
