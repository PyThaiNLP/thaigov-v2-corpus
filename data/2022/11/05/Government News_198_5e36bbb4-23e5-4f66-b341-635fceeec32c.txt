PM conveys the media New Year wishes for 2022
December 28, 2021, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha conveyed the 2022 New Year wishes to the press and media. He invited the virtue of all sacredness and the royal prestige of His Majesty the King and Her Majesty the Queen to bless members of the press and media happiness and good health, as well as all the successes they wish for. He also hopes that they be a part to lead the country and the Thai people forward in a stable and sustainable manner as the quality media people who uphold ethics and morality for the love and unity of the Thai people.
