Cabinet approves revision of “Ying Chai Ying Dai” e-voucher-giveaway scheme
July 20, 2021, Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting today, approved a revision to the "Ying Chai Ying Dai" (the more you spend, the more you get) scheme, a government’s measure to alleviate the plight of people from the impact of COVID-19 pandemicthrough the giveaway of e-vouchers. Detail of the revision is as follows:
1. Extending the “Ying Chai Ying Dai” scheme, originally effective from now until September 30, 2021, to November 30, 2021
2. Increasing the maximum spending from       5,000 Baht to 10,000 Baht/person/day
3. Reducing the target number from  4,000,000 to 1.4 millionpeople, which leads to the downsize of the scheme’s budgetframework from 28 billion Baht to 9.8 billion Baht, a cut of approximate 18.2 billion Baht
According to the Government Spokesperson, the revision has been made in accordance with an order of the Center for COVID-19 Situation Administration (CCSA) to close department stores, shopping centers, and community malls in themaximum and strict controlled areas for 14 days (except for supermarkets, pharmacies, and venues for vaccination or other state medical and health services), which may also reduce the number of e-voucher users.
