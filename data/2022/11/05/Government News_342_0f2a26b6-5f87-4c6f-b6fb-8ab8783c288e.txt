​PM discloses key outcomes of cabinet meeting
​PM discloses key outcomes of cabinet meetin
May 30, 2022, at 13.22 hrs, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha disclosed about some of the outcomes the cabinet meeting.
The cabinet approved interest subsidy scheme for sugarcane farmers. The scheme will support farmers under the Bank for Agriculture and Agricultural Cooperatives (BAAC)’s 3-year soft loan scheme (2022-2024), worth 2 billion Baht/year (the total of 6 billion Baht), for solving drought problem and purchasing agricultural machinery. The Government will subsidize 3% of the interest rate. The Prime Minister also disclosed that Agri – Map will be used to enhance sugarcane farming efficiency. The cabinet also approved amendment of the regulation of Cane and Sugar Board on registration of sugarcane institutions to streamline operation and enhance competitiveness.
The cabinet agreed with Ministry of Culture’s proposal to declare August 12 of each year as “National Thai Textile Day” on occasion of the 90th birthday anniversary of Her Majesty Queen Sirikit, the Queen Mother on August 12, 2022. The declaration of “National Thai Textile Day” is aimed to remember Her Majesty’s great contributions and endless benevolence on promotion of Thai textile and local textile production and design in every province, which has created employment and generated revenues of the local people, leading to their better livelihood. The use of Thai traditional textiles has also contributed to the conservation of national culture and local wisdom in textiles and helped promote Thai textiles in the international markets.
