PM chairs alms-giving ceremony in remembrance of His Majesty late King Bhumibol
October 13, 2021, at 1000hrs, at Makut Kasatriyaram Ratchaworawihan Temple, Bangkok, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha and spouse Assoc. Prof. Naraporn Chan-o-cha presided over the alms-giving ceremony on occasion of the anniversary of His Majesty late King Bhumibol’s passing. Participated in the ceremony were members of the cabinet, Prime Minister’s Secretary General, Permanent Secretary to the Prime Minister’s Office, Prime Minister’s advisor on royal and special affairs, and government officials.
Upon arrival, the Prime Minister and spouse lit candle and incense sticks to pay homage to the Triple Gems, and laid the golden offerings in front of His Majesty’s portrait. Following the chanting by 10 senior monks, he and the participants presented the monks with the triple robes and offerings. He, then, paid homage to the Triple Gems, and His Majesty late King Bhumibol’s portrait to end the ceremony.
