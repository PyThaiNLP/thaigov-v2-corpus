The cabinet met on January 4, 2022
The cabinet met on January 4, 2022. Some of the resolutions are as follows:
Title: Integrity and Transparency Assessment(ITA) Report for FY2021
The cabinet acknowledged the report on Integrity and Transparency Assessment (ITA) for FY2021, as proposed by the National Anti-Corruption Commission (NACC).
Gist
National Anti-Corruption Commission (NACC)reported that 1,331,588 government officials/civil servants from 8,300 public agencies have participated in the ITA process, which was conducted via the website: http://itas.nacc.go.th(ITAS system) during February 1- July 31, 2021.
According to the National Anti-Corruption Commission (NACC) report on Integrity and Transparency Assessment (ITA) for FY2021, the nation’s average score is 81.25 (B level), an increase of 13.35 from last year. There are 4,146 agencies (49.95%) that have passed the target score for anti-corruption and malfeasance in accordance with the National Strategy, an increase of 36.76% from last year.
Title: Thailand’s nomination as host of World Heritage Committee’s 46th session
The cabinet approved in principle Thailand’s nomination as host of World Heritage Committee’s 46th session, and assigned Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment (Office of Natural Resources and Environmental Policy and Planning) to work together with other concerned agencies to draft the TOR and other related detail, as well as budget estimation, to be submitted to the cabinet for approval before submitting the nomination to UNESCO World Heritage Centre. This is as proposed by Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment.
The World Heritage Committee normally meets every June-July each year. Decision on the hosting of the World Heritage Committee’s 46th session will be made during the 45th session to be held on June 19-30, 2022 in Kazan, the Russian Federation.
Title: Request for budget for Thailand’s nomination to host Specialised Expo in Phuket under name: “EXPO 2028 – Phuket, Thailand”
The cabinet approved the budget framework at the amount of 4,180,000,000 Baht for Ministry of Public Health’s nomination to host the Specialised Expo in Phuket under name: “EXPO 2028 – Phuket, Thailand”.
Gist
The implementation phase for Thailand’s nomination of the Specialised Expo in Phuket is as follows:
2021:​​Candidature phase
2022-2023:​Project Examination Phase by the Bureau International des Expositions (BIE)
2024:​​Recognition Phase
2025 – 2029:​Implementation Phase​
Thailand’s nomination of the Specialised Expo in Phuket is aimed to uplift Phuket as world-class healthcare tourism. The theme is set as “Future of Life - Living in Harmony, Sharing Prosperity”, with the sub-themes on Life and Well-Being,Human-Nature, and Mutual Prosperity.
