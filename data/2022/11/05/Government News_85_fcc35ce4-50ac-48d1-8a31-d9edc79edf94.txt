PM commends success of Thai athletes at “Tokyo 2020 Paralympics”
September 5, 2021, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha commended the success of Thai athletes who participated in the 16th Summer Paralympic Games, hosted by Japan during August 24- September 5, 2021.
He thanked the athletes for their determination and dedication, despite the physical risk and stresses brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic, to bring the country pride and fame and Thai people’s smile of happiness. He also thanked concerned staffs, public and private sectors who are behind the success, and all the Thai people for cheering them on throughout the competition.
At the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games, Thailand ranks 25th on the medal table with 5 golds, 5 silvers, and 8 bronzes.
