PM affirms Govt’s commitment to curb gasoline price hike
February 7, 2022, at 1015hrs, at the Government House, following the awarding ceremony for organizations with excellence in financial management, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha stated about gasoline price hike that international gasoline price situation and budget must be taken into account in the decision for price control. Nevertheless, the Government strives to solve the problem to ensure that gasoline prices would not be too high to cause plight to the people and all concerned sectors.
