PM to visit Sakon Nakhon province on June 15
June 14, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha is scheduled for a field visit to Sakon Nakhon province on June 15, 2022.
In the morning, the Prime Minister will visit Phra Ajarn Fan Arjaro Hospital in Panna Nikhom district to follow up on the implementation of “Thai People Map and Analytics Platform (TPMAP)”, a data analytics tool aiming at precision poverty alleviation and improving quality of life for Thai citizens, and hand over renovated houses to representatives of target households under TPMAP. He will also follow up on the implementation progress to promote Thailand as “herbal hub”, before traveling to Puparn Royal Development Study Centre in Muang district to meet with representatives of farmers’ network.
In the afternoon, the Prime Minister will observe the implementation of high-value agriculture by Khokhun Ponyangkham community enterprise, and meet with Khokhun Phu Thai and Khokhun Ponyangkham cattle farmers at Ban Tha Yiam village, Muang district. He will, then, visit indigo dyed fabric shops and meet with local weavers and sellers at KRAM Walking Street before returning to Bangkok.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the visit of the Prime Minister is aimed to follow up on various implementations under the Government’s, especially on poverty eradication and development of people of all ages. In 2022, approx. population of Sakon Nakhon is 1.14 million, out of whom target population under TPMAP accounts for 12,261 people, a decline in number comparing to 2018 (22,922 people). This demonstrates successful mobilization of the policy to reduce the number of TPMAP-targeted population.
