PM: Budget expenditure needs to be transparent and accountable.
PM: Budget expenditure needs to be transparent and accountable.
May 31, 2022, at 11.57 hrs, at the 2023 budget bill debate of the House of Representatives, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha has made a clarification regarding the allegations that the government's spending on investments in the fiscal year 2022 was lower than proposed. The 2022 proposed budget has been set at 624,399.8520 million Baht, however, the budget was revised by the legislature and drop 12,466.4041 million Baht; as a result, the investment budget was reduced to 611,933.4479 million Baht, which meant the budget reduced for 19.74% of the total budget. Therefore, the government had to manage the budget for the investment projects, which also had to be proposed to the cabinet for consideration. The budget has to be adapted to projects that are not budgeted.  Moreover, the Prime Minister also emphasized that the budget and the proposed projects must be in accordance with the need of the people in the area and is transparent.
