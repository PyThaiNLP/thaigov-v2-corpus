​PM led cabinet members and MPs to Sukhothai to visit flood-affected victims
September 26, 2021, at 10.15 hrs, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, concerned ministers and MPs took a field trip to Sukhothai province to give moral support and relief bags to flood-affected victims in Si Samrong district’s Wangyai and Ban Rai sub-districts.
The Prime Minister visited Wangyai sub-district’s Ban Khlong Chad and Ban Rai sub-district’s Wat Donchan Temple to talk to the local people who have been affected by flash flood, and gave them moral support. He also ordered concerned agencies to immediately explore the damages, and provide relief and remedies to the affected people.
The Prime Minister added that he and the delegation came today on behalf of the cabinet with great concern over the wellbeing of everyone regardless of which political factors they side. He also warned them to be careful of poisonous animals that come with flood.
The Prime Minister, then, headed to Wat Ban San Temple to pay respect to sacred Buddha images before being reported on the flood disaster situation in Sukhothai province from representatives of the local agencies.
