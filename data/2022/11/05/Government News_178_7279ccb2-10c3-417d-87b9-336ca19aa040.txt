​Govt Spokesperson: Businesses urged to hire migrant workers under MoU to prevent spread of COVID-19
December 11, 2021, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that following the Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha’s instruction to law enforcement officials, military, police, and local administrative offices in all the border provinces to intercept smuggling and illegal entry of migrant labors in order to prevent illegal labor, crimes, and minimize the risk of unscreened COVID-19, a large number of illegal migrants have been arrested. He also ordered for thorough investigation of the entire smuggling process. If any involvement of public officials found in any case, they will be subject to the highest legal penalties.
The Prime Minister also called for the employers/entrepreneurs to strictly abide by the law, and hire migrant workers under a memorandum of understanding (MoU), which requires the COVID-19 screening process to ensure safety of the businesses and individuals.
