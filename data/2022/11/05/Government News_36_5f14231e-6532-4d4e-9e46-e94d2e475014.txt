Cabinet approves additional budget to remedy employers and insured employees under Section 33 in maximum and strict controlled areas
July 20, 2021, Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting today, approved the budget framework of 10,985.316 million Baht for the remedy scheme for employers and insured employees under Section 33in the businesses located in the maximum and strict controlled areas and the Southernmost provinces (10 provinces) who have been affected by the Government’s COVID-19 measures, as proposed by Ministry of Labor. The approved budget frameworkwill add to the earlier-approved 2,519.38 million Baht to sum up to the total of 13,504.696 million Baht for the implementation ofthe scheme.
As for additional 3 provinces recently included in the maximum and strict controlled areas, namely, Chachoengsao, Chonburi, and Ayutthaya, the cabinet has approved expansion of the remedy scheme to also cover the 3 provinces. Additional budget framework will later be proposed to the cabinet by Ministry of Labor.
