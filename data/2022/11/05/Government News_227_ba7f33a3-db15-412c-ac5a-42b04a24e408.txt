PM wishes Thai people happiness and good health for the 2022 Chinese New Year
February 1, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha gave a blessing to the Thai people on occasion of the Chinese New Year. He wishes for happiness, prosperity, and healthiness.
As we are still in the middle of COVID-19 situation, it is important that everyone keep their guards up and strictly observe COVID-19 preventive etiquette, i.e., wearing mask, washing hands, social distancing. They are also strongly advised to undertake ATK test both before and after the gatherings. The Prime Minister hopes that everyone would responsibly celebrate with their loved ones during the Chinese New Year.
As for the updated COVID-19 situation, today (February 1, 2022), a total of 7,422 new confirmed cases is reported, out of which 7,161 were found domestically, and 261 from overseas traveling. The total of 12 deaths and 8,715 recoveries were reported with 83,014 active cases. From January 1, 2022, cumulative number of cases are 224,529, and cumulative number of recoveries, 174,271. Cumulative number of vaccinations across the country from February 28, 2021 to January 30, 2022 is 116,002,932 doses (1st shot: 52,290,140 persons, 2nd shot: 48,582,903 persons, 3rd shot 14,141,210 persons, and 4th shot: 988,679 persons).
