​ASEAN-Korea Industrial Innovation Center to be established by 2023
March 25, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Defense Minister and Prime Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha ordered concerned agencies to accelerate the development of science, technology, and innovation in order to push forward Thailand as the region's future industry hub.
Following the cabinet's approval on March 1, Ministry of Commerce, as Thai representative,  is set to sign an MOU on the establishment of the ASEAN-Korea Industrial Innovation Center (AKIIC) by mid 2022, and AKIIC will be set up in South Korea within 2023 with an aim to promote cooperation between ASEAN and South Korea on sciences, technology, and innovation for various hi-tech industries through joint researches, exchange of knowledge, technology transfer, and commercial use of technologies. NSTDA will represent Thailand as AKIIC executive member. Ministry of Commerce will closely collaborate with NSTDA to propel the operation of AKIIC toward tangible results, especially the S-Curve industry- related implementations that are relevant with Thailand's 20-year strategy on the development of Industry 4.0 (2017-2036), such as the development of innovation for diversification and value-added of products and services, and promotion of smart farming through adoption of technology to enhance efficient, reduce cost and time, and generate more revenues.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Government always supports mechanisms conducive to Thailand's competitiveness enhancement. The establishment of AKIIC not only promotes Thailand's cooperation with South Korea, a country notable for advanced science, technology, and innovation, but also provides an opportunity to build upon regional cooperation (ASEAN) for the development of wide-ranging innovations, which works help increase industrial capacity and potentially, as well as commercial values, for such industries as smart farming, next-generation automotive, high-efficiency battery, medical equipment, and entertainment industry, among others.
