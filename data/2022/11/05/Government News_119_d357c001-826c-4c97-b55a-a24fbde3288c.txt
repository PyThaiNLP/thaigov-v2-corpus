PM thanks all concerned agencies for facilitating vaccination of foreign residents
Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed about the performance of the website: https://expatvac.consular.go.th, set up since August 1, 2021 by Ministry of Foreign Affairs, in collaboration with Ministry of Public Health, as the COVID-19 vaccination registration site for foreign residents in Thailand.
According to the Government Spokesperson, over 70,000 foreign residents in Thailand who registered have been vaccinated, and the overall number of foreigners who have been vaccinated so far is over a million. Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha has also been reported on the number of vaccinated foreign residents, and thanked all concerned sectors for facilitating foreign residents in the country and ensuring their access to COVID-19 vaccines in a bid to promote good health for everyone who resides in Thailand. He also expressed concern over the wellbeing of Thai citizens living abroad.
The Government Spokesperson also affirmed the Thai Government’s commitment to provide equitable care to foreign residents, and to give COVID-19 shots to at least 70% of both Thai and foreign residents combined by the end of 2021. Thailand aims to reopen the country on the basis of universal prevention by implementing the “Smart Control and Living with COVID-19” plan.
