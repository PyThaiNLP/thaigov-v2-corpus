​PM pleased with positive sign of economic recovery through increase of tourism activities during Songkran
April 18, 2022, at 10.55 hrs, at the Government House, following the meeting of the organizing committee on the celebration of the 100th birthday anniversary of Her Royal Highness Princess Galyani Vadhana, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha met with the press and expressed pleasure that the Thai people got a chance to rest and return to their loved ones during the Songkran Holiday. He also disclosed that concerned agencies have been ordered to closely monitor post-Songkran COVID-19 situation, of which the consequence should be manifest in the next 7 days, and emphasized that those who attended Songkran activities or just returned from traveling were urged to do own monitoring and properly undergo the ATK test.
The Prime Minister was also pleased with constant economic recovery through an increase of tourism activities thanks to the long holidays, and added that this would make positive impact to other supply chains. However, everyone is strongly advised to continue to exercise caution and comply with the COVID-19 preventive measures.
The Prime Minister also stated that Thailand has various numbers of soft power potentials in its food, nature, and health, among others, on which the Government strives to promote among the international tourists and community.
