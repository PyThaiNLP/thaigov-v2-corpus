PM pleased with Ayutthaya being listed among top 50 best places to visit post-pandemic by “Forbes Advisor”
May 4, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha acknowledged and was pleased that Ayutthaya province has been listed by the website, “Forbes Advisor”, as one of the top 50 best places to visit post-pandemic. He thanked the global community for appreciatingthis beautiful and historic place. With the diversity and uniqueness of Thai tourist attractions, coupling with properpublic health management and disease control measures, the Prime Minister is confident that more tourists would soon flock to Thailand.
According to “Forbes Advisor”, Ayutthaya is 1 of the 8 Asian countries/cities listed, and was the largest city in the world in 1700s. The city is only 90 minutes away from Bangkok, but staying the night would allow tourists to see the temples in early morning and late afternoon, when they are at their quietest. Other Asian cities on the list include Harbin (China PRC), Bhutan, Assam (India), Lombok (Indonesia), Taipei (Taiwan), Uzbekistan, and Doha (Qatar).
The Government Spokesperson added that the Prime Minister sent moral support to officials from all concerned agencies for efficiently administering the entry of foreign tourists in accordance with the current control measures, taking into account the safety of both Thais and tourists. Under the ongoing implementation of public health measures, tourism business and activities continue to be promoted and propelled forward to create revenues and employments.
