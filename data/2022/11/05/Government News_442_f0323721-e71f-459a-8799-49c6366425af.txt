​Thailand placed on top as world’s best country for wellness retreats
August 21, 2022, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha was pleased with a report from the website: https://www.traveldailynews.asia/ which referred to the Slingo’s research of TripAdvisor (both are popular tourism websites) on the countries with the best resort hotels, wellness and spa activities. According to the report, Thailand is the best country in the world for wellness retreats, and comes in second, after Maldives, for highest-rated wellness activities.
The country is known as the spa capital of Asia, and is home to some of the most therapeutic and luxurious retreats in the world, with a total of 2,673 wellness and spa activities available to choose from. When those are filtered to four-star ratings and higher, 1,952 activities remain, accounting for  73.03%. The top-rated activities are professional yoga and pilates classes.
The Prime Minister commended cooperation of the public, private, and people sectors in elevating Thai medical tourism standard, and stimulating economy through tourism activities. He added that the Government strives to implement appropriate measures to promote tourism in line with what are trending. Medical tourism is one of Thailand’s strengths, and the ranking proves the country’s capacity and potential to attract tourists from all over the world.
