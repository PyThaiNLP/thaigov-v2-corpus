PM observes preparation of COVID-19 field hospital at Royal Thai Army Club
July 23, 2021, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha took a field visit to the Royal Thai Army Club to follow up on the setup of a field hospital for patients of all levels of risks.
The Prime Minister thanked and gave his moral support to all personnel and officials, and affirmed the Government’s commitment to work together with all sectors to supply beds and treatment to all patients.
Royal Thai Army Club Field Hospital is providing 400 beds to COVID-19 patients, 300 of which will be available by July 30, while the other 100 will be used in the hospital’s isolation center for COVID-19 patients waiting for hospital admission.
