​PM to attend 28th APEC Summit and handover of APEC chairmanship
​PM to attend 28th APEC Summit and handover of APEC chairmanship
November 10, 2021, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha is scheduled to attend the APEC Economic Leaders’ Week (AELW) via a teleconference during November 11-12, 2021. Detail of his schedule is as follows:
November 11, 2021
- 19:00: Prime Minister attends ABAC – Leaders’ Dialogue
November 12, 2021
- Prime Minister delivers a remark at the APEC CEO Summit
- 18:00: Prime Minister attends the 28th APEC Summit and New Zealand (Prime Minister Right Honourable Jacinda Ardern)’s handover of APEC chairmanship to Thailand (Prime Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha)
The theme for APEC 2021, hosted by New Zealand, is “Join, Work, Grow Together”, which focuses on three key deliveries: 1) Economic and Trade Policies that Strengthen Recovery; 2) Increasing Inclusion and Sustainability for Recovery; and 3) Pursuing Innovation and a Digitally-Enabled Recovery.
The Government Spokesperson affirmed the Prime Minister’s readiness to present Thailand’s role, and put forward such priorities as: 1) regional connectivity, especially commuting and tourism; 2) trade and investment; and 3) economic transformation through adoption of BCG Economy Model. He also invited the Thai people to join hands in hosting the APEC 2022, and expressed confidence on Thailand’s capability to successfully host the APEC 2022 to reach tangible outcomes.
