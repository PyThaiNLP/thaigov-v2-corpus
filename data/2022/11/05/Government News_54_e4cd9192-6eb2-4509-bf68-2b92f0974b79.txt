​PM receives royal donation for setup of field hospitals and isolation centers
August 4, 2021, at the Ivory Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House, ACM. Sathitpong Sukwimol, Lord Chamberlain of the Royal Household, delivered the royal donation, graciously granted by His Majesties the King and Queen, to the Thai Government to commemorate the auspicious occasions of His Majesty the King’s birthday anniversary on July 28, 2021, and Her Majesty Queen Sirikit the Queen Mother’s birthday anniversary on August 12, 2021. Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, on behalf of the Government and people, expressed profound appreciation toward the royal benevolence.
The Royal donation in the amount of 99,900,000 Baht will be used for the setup of field hospitals and isolation centers for COVID-19 patients at schools, vocational schools, universities, and military compounds nationwide.
