​PM and spouse chairs floral offering ceremony to bless Her Majesty the Queen
June 3, 2022, at 19.19 hrs, at Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, Bangkok, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, together with spouse Assoc. Prof. Naraporn Chan-o-cha, presided over the ceremony of homage offering and glory praising in celebration of Her Majesty the Queen’s birthday anniversary on June 3, 2022. Attending the ceremony were the House Speaker, President of the Supreme Court, the Senate, and constitutional organizations and spouses, cabinet members and spouses, high-level government officials, military, police, civilians, private and people sectors.
The Prime Minister and all the participants took their position, paid homage to Her Majesty’s portrait, and presented traditional cone-shaped floral and homage offerings before delivering a commemorative speech to praise Her Majesty the Queen. He, then, led all the participants to bow and pay homage to Her Majesty’s portrait again, followed by singing the Royal Anthems to complete the ceremony.
