Cabinet approves purchase of additional 9,998,820 doses of Pfizer vaccines
August 30, 2021, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting on August 30, 2021, approved the budget of 4.745 billion Baht for the procurement of 9,998,820 doses of COVID-19 Pfizer vaccines to be given to 6 target groups, namely, children (12-17 years of age), pregnant women (12 weeks and over), medical personnel and frontline public health workers (for booster doses), the elderlies and those with 7 underlying non-communicable diseases, foreign residents in Thailand (especially the elderlies and those with 7 underlying non-communicable diseases), and people who need Pfizer vaccination before going abroad, such as students, athletes, diplomats, etc. However, the target groups may be adjusted according to the emerging situation.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the cabinet has earlier approved the budget of 22.990 billion Baht foracquisition of 80 million doses of COVID-19 vaccines, comprising AstraZeneca (61 million doses), Sinovac (19 million doses), and Pfizer (19 million doses). With the procurement of the additional 9.9 million doses of Pfizer, the Government’s target to acquire at least 100 million doses of COVID-19 vaccine within 2021 would be accomplished.
