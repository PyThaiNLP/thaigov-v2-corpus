PM’s field trip to Nakhon Si Thammarat to observe water management and flood prevention plan
October 7, 2021, at 1410hrs, in Pho Sadej subdistrict, Muang district, Nakhon Si Thammarat province, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha observed the province’s water management and flood prevention plan. Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed gist as follows:
After listening to the briefing, the Prime Minister revealed his intention of the field trip, which is to ensure preparedness of the province against impending flood volume. The Government has prepared a number of response plans, but the local people are also urged to follow up on official announcement and warning, and plan on moving things up above ground or evacuating to a prepared evacuation site.
The Prime Minister also instructed concerned agencies to rush in surveying the damages and giving financial relief to flood-affected victims. Local administrations and MPs are also called on to provide immediate help where possible. He affirmed the Government’s commitment to take the best possible care of the people.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister expressed concern over the ongoing COVID-19 situation, especially in the South, and called on everyone to observe hygienic etiquettes. Solidarity is also very important. He also urged the Southern people to uphold the value of nation, religion, and monarchy.
