​Government House’s Songkran celebration to be toned down
April 6, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister has made an order to Office of Prime Minister’s Secretary General to reduce protocol and number of the participants in the Government House’s “2022 Songkran Celebration”, and make it an internal activity. All the protocols must be carried out according to the advices of Ministry of Public Health and Ministry of Culture, under strict COVID-19 preventive measures and the requirements of Center for COVID-19 Situation Administration.
The Prime Minister also expressed appreciation toward senators, MPs, representatives of political parties, and chiefs of various public agencies for their intent to visit, seek his blessing, and pour water on occasion of Songkran festival. However, they are, instead, urged to organize activities within their own agency under the COVID-free setting to set a good example to the public.
Public agencies and organizations are also advised by the Prime Minister to hold Songkran activities online, with which, not only the country’s culture and tradition be carried over, everyone’s commitment to prevent the spread of COVID-19 is demonstrated and appreciated.
