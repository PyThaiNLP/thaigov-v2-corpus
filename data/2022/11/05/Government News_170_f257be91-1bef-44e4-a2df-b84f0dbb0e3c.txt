Cabinet approved additional 76,080.95 million Baht for farmers’ income insurance scheme
Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting on November 30, 2021, approved additional budget of 76,080.95 million Baht for the implementation of 2021/2022 farmers’ income insurancescheme. Of the total budget approved, 74,569.31 million Baht will be allocated for farmer compensation, while the other 1,511,64 million will be used for related expenses of the Bank for Agriculture and Agricultural Cooperatives (BAAC).
The Government Spokesperson added that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, as chair of National Rice Policy Committee, wanted to ensure that the farmers’ earning from paddy sales are reasonable and enough to sustain their living during the COVID-19 situation. He also wants to boost confidence of the farmers, as committed in the Government’s policy statement declared to the House of Representatives.
