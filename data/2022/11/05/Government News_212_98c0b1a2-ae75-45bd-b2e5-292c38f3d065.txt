PM hands down policy on human resource development and educational reform
January 16, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha commended an establishment of the “Higher Education Development Fund” which will systematically mobilize Thai tertiary institutions with the aim to prepare them for global disruptive change, and provide up-to-date knowledge to human resources. The Higher Education Development Fund also promotes cross-institutional study to enhance students’ knowledge and experiences, and to integrate information and knowledge between different education institutions. The Prime Minister is confident that the establishment of “Higher Education Development Fund” would greatly contribute to the development of Thai educational system on par with those in other countries.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister has placed great importance on the development of youths and children, and handed down a policy for concerned agencies to enhance children’s capacity, promote educational development and opportunity and their access to suitable occupations. They are also instructed to take care of children’s mental health and seek proper measures to minimize their financial burden incurred from the online study.
