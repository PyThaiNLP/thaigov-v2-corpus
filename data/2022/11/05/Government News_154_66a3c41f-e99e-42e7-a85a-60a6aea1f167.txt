PM attends exhibitions held by local agencies before chairing 1/2021 cabinet retreat in Krabi
November 16, 2021, at 0800hrs, at Sofitel Krabi Phokeethra Golf & Spa Resort, Krabi, prior to the meeting on economic and social development of the Andaman Southern provincial cluster (Krabi, Trang, Phang-gna, Phuket, Ranong, and Satun) and the 1/2021 cabinet retreat, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha visited the exhibition of Andaman Southern provincial cluster presented by local agencies under various ministries. Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed gist as follows:
The exhibition was held by Ministry of Interior’s Community Development Department (Krabi province’s OTOP mobile presenting local products, such as basketry, civet coffee); Ministry of Education’s Office of Non-Formal and Informal Education (monitoring platform for the education of special target groups); Ministry of Higher Education, Science, Research and Innovation’s National Science and Technology Development Agency (water/UV-resistant and antibacterial patek fabrics); Ministry of Digital Economy and Society (replacement of overhead line by underground cable system in Krabi); Ministry of Culture (New Normal Loy Kratong Festival). The Prime Minister attentively visited all the booths displayed at the exhibition.
The Prime Minister also chatted with local students and told them that he loved all the Thai children and would like them to work hard at school and consider learning the 2nd language. He, then, chaired the meeting on economic and social development of the Andaman Southern provincial cluster and the 1/2021 cabinet retreat.
