PM plants tree at Thai Embassy and meets with Thai people and students in Saudi Arabia
January 25, 2022, at 17.45 hrs (Riyadh local time), Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha paid a visit to Al-Turaif District, located in Al-Dir'iyah north-west of Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, which has been enlisted as UNESCO World Heritage.
Later in the evening, the Prime Minister and delegation visited to the Royal Thai Embassy in Riyadh. He planted a Burma padauk tree in front of the Embassy, and attended dinner reception hosted by Charge D'affaires of the Royal Thai Embassy in Riyadh, Mr. Sathana Kasemsan na Ayutthaya. He also met with Thai students and citizens residing in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
After the dinner reception, the Prime Minister and delegation left Riyadh for Bangkok to arrive in Thailand on January 26, 2022, at 08.40hrs (Thailand time).
