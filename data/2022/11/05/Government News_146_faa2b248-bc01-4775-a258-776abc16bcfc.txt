THAI’s restructuring plan sees satisfactory progress
THAI’s restructuring plan sees satisfactory progress
Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that on November 8, 2021, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha met with concerned ministers and public agencies to discuss the policy on long-term residency in Thailand for foreign tourists and investors following the country reopening since November 1, 2021, on which expected revenues should also be taken into account. The Prime Minister also listened to the briefing on the progress of Thai Airways International Plc's (THAI) debt-rehabilitation plan. According to the briefing, the restructuring has seen a satisfactory progress, especially through cost reduction and organizational restructuring. The Prime Minister affirmed that THAI remains the country’s national airline.
