The cabinet met on November 30, 2021. Some of the resolutions are as follows:
Title: Royal Decree issued under the Revenue Code Regarding Exemption from Revenue Taxes (No. …) B.E. … (Tax measure to support public healthcare donation)
The cabinet approved in principle the Royal Decree issued under the Revenue Code Regarding Exemption from Revenue Taxes (No. …) B.E. … (Tax measure to support public healthcare donation), as proposed by Ministry of Finance.
Gist
In a bid to continue with the measure to promote public donation to Siriraj Foundation, and Chulabhorn Foundation, and reduce the Government’s budget spent on public healthcare, Ministry of Finance proposed the Royal Decree issued under the Revenue Code Regarding Exemption from Revenue Taxes (No. …) B.E. … (Tax measure to support public healthcare donation). Under the Royal Decree, donation money to the two Foundations will be calculated by double deduction of actual donated amount but not exceeding 10% of income after standard deductions and allowances. This applies for both individuals, corporates, and juristic partnerships.
Title: Report on the Industrial Economics Status for September 2021
The cabinet acknowledged Industrial Economic Report for September 2021 as proposed by Ministry of Industry.
Gist
Based on the report, manufacturing production index (MPI) contracted by 1.3% YOY due to the latest phase of the COVID-19 pandemic and continual decrease of vehicle manufacturing as a result of parts shortage.
Key industries that contributed to the contraction were Hard Disk Drive manufacturing (-18.9%), refined petroleum production (-5.56%), and automotive and parts (-5.43%).
Key industries that have expanded well in September 2021 yoy were electronics parts (+13.12%), and iron and steel (+13.61%).
Title: Report on removal of assets off the legal execution system for 4th quarter of FY 2021
The cabinet acknowledged the report made by Ministryof Justic on the removal of assets off the legal execution system for 4th quarter of FY 2021. According to the report, the assets removed worth for the total of 48,051,746,688 Baht, comprising 13,143,855,168 Baht from the public auctions, 14,847,883,863 Baht from suspension of legal execution, and 20,060,007,657 Baht from withdrawal of legal execution.
