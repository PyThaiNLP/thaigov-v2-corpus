Businesses and services urged to strictly comply with ‘Covid Free Setting’ requirements
December 2, 2021, at 1100hrs, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha gave an interview about Omicron, the new variant of COVID-19, which has already been found in some countries in Asia that the Thai Government has not any plan to take the harsh measure of country lockdown, as strict preventive measure has been put in place, that is, the RT-PCR screening process which requires quarantine of travelers until the result comes out as negative.
According to the Prime Minister, the Government strives to drive forward economic recovery in parallel with people’s safety. The measures implemented have been scrutinized by medical personnel and concerned agencies including the private sector before proposing to the Prime Minister. It is important that all businesses and services strictly comply with the ‘Covid Free Setting’ requirements. They may put up an announcement to certify that their place is COVID-free.
