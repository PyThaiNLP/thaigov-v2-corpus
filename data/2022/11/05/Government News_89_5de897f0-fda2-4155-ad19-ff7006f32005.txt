PM instructs concerned agencies to provide assistance to people in flood-risk areas
Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha ordered the military, local administrations, and Ministry of Interior to rush in providing assistance and relief to the people in flood and mudslide risk areas, and to have Bangkok’s flood drainage system ready to prevent inundation. The public is also urged to exercise caution when commuting and traveling.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister also called on the public to strictly follow the universal prevention guideline and adhere to the D-M-H-T-T principle. Although some of the COVID-19 measures have been eased, the effort to control the spread of COVID-19 is still ongoing, and public cooperation is required to prevent further spread of the disease. All concerned staffs and officials have been instructed to take the best possible care of the people. The Prime Minister also thanked medical staffs for working hard to help out the people.
