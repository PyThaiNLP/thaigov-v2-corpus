PM hands down policy to promote Thailand as world sports destination
December 7, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha handed down a policy to promote “Soft power” through sports to build upon tourism promotion. Focus will be made to promote Thailand as world sports destination.
Thailand, through Ministry of Tourism and Sports, is scheduled to host Water Jet World Grand Prix (WGP)#1 during January 12-16, 2022 in Pattaya, Chonburi. The jet ski competition will be participated by the highest number of athletes since 2018, and is part of the WGP#1 World Series 2021-2022. The event will be broadcasted in 90 countries across the globe.
According to the Government Spokesperson, promotion of soft power and sports industry is the Government’s important policyto increase economic value. The aim is to push forward Thailand to become the world sports destination under the strictest COVID-19 preventive measure.
