Cabinet approves Thai Industrial Standard for retreaded pneumatic tyres for commercial vehicles and trailers
June 22, 2021, at the Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Deputy Government Spokesperson Ratchada Thanadirek disclosed that the cabinet has made approval to the draft Ministerial Regulation on Thai Industrial Standard for retreaded pneumatic tyres for commercial vehicles and trailers, B.E. …, as proposed by Ministry of Industry, to be in line with UN Regulation No. 109.
The draft Ministerial Regulation on Thai Industrial Standard for retreaded pneumatic tyres for commercial vehicles and trailers, B.E. …, prescribes Thai Industrial Standard for retreaded pneumatic tyres for commercial vehicles and trailers under TIS 2979-2562 (2019), in accordance with the Ministerial Announcement No. 5471 (B.E. 2562) issued under the Industrial Product Standards Act B.E. 2511. The draft Regulation will take effect 180 days after being published in the Royal Gazette.
Before proposing the draft Regulation to the cabinet, Ministry of Industry, through the Thai Industrial Standards Institute, has conducted a public hearing via its website: www.tisi.go.th, and notify 140 stakeholders, most of whom agreed with the draft Regulation. According to the Deputy Government Spokesperson, the Ministerial Regulation on Thai Industrial Standard for retreaded pneumatic tyres for commercial vehicles and trailers would help enhance competitiveness of Thai retreaded pneumatic tyre entrepreneurs, prevent the import of sub-standard retreaded pneumatic tyres into the country, and ensure public confidence and safety.
