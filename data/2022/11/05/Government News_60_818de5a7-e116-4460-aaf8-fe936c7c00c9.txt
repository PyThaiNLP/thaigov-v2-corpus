PM chairs floral offering ceremony to bless Her Majesty Queen Sirikit the Queen Mother
August 12, 2021, at 0830hrs, at Bhakdi Bodin Building, Government House, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, together with spouse Assoc. Prof. Naraporn Chan-o-cha, presided over the ceremony of homage offering and glory praising in celebration of Her Majesty Queen Sirikit the Queen Mother’s birthday anniversary on August 12, 2021. Attending the ceremony were Deputy Prime Ministers, Minister Attached to the Prime Minister’s Office, Prime Minister’s Secretary General, Permanent Secretary to the Prime Minister’s Office, and Cabinet’s Secretary General.
The Prime Minister and all the participants took their position, paid homage to Her Majesty’s portrait, and presented traditional cone-shaped floral and homage offerings before delivering a commemorative speech to praise Her Majesty Queen Sirikit the Queen Mother. He, then, led all the participants to bow and pay homage to Her Majesty’s portrait again, followed by singing the Royal Anthems to complete the ceremony.
