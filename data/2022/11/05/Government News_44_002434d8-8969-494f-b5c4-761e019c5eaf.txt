​PM leads cabinet members and government officials to take oath of allegiance to H.M. the King
The Prime Minister presided over an oath taking ceremony on the auspicious occasion of His Majesty the King’s birthday on July 28, 2021. Government officials took an oath to uphold decency and be the power of the nation.
July 28, 2021, at 0830hrs, at Bhakdi Bodin Building, Government House, Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, Prime Minister and Defense Minister, presided over an oath taking ceremony and floral offerings on the auspicious occasion of His Majesty the King’s birthday on July 28, 2021.  Participating in the ceremony were Deputy Prime Ministers, Minister Attached to the Prime Minister’s Office, Permanent Secretary to the Prime Minister’s Office, Secretary General of the Office of the Civil Service Commission, and representatives of high-level government officials.
The Prime Minister and participants of the ceremony paid homage to His Majesty the King’s portrait, and offered traditional cone-shaped floral receptacle. He, then, led the cabinet members and government officials in taking an oath to uphold decency and be the power of the nation, and sang the Royal Anthem in unison to complete the ceremony.
