The PM visited Sustainable Community Projects to solve drug problems comprehensively in Saraphi District, Chiang Mai Province.
The Prime Minister visited Sustainable Community Projects to address drug problems comprehensively under the National Strategy in Saraphi District, Chiang Mai Province, as a successful example of preventing and resolving drug problems at the local level.
June 29, 2022, at 1645 hrs, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Minister of Defense, Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, has visited the Sustainable Community Project to solve drug problems comprehensively according to the National Strategy of Saraphi District, which is a collaboration between the Royal Thai Police by Saraphi Police Station, Saraphi District, Chiang Mai Province, and the local community to contribute to firmly implementing a Sustainable Community Project in the area of Ban Chiang Khang, Village No. 7, Chaiyasathan Sub-district, Saraphi District. However, the community is also the centre for prevention, and solving drug problems, especially the introduction of voluntary drug addiction into the treatment procedure and work in various businesses. This aims to offer patients the energy and motivation to live, as well as the ability to actively live within the community and society in peace.
