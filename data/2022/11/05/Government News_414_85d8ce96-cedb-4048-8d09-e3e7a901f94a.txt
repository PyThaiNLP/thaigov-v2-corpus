​MFA compiles e-book “A Legacy for All” to glorify royal honor on occasion of His Majesty the King’s birthday anniversary on July 28, 2022
July 25, 2022, Deputy Government Spokesperson Ratchada Thanadirek disclosed that on an auspicious occasion of His Majesty the King’s birthday anniversary on July 28, 2022, the Government, through Ministry of Foreign Affairs, has compiled an e-book on “A Legacy for All” to glorify His Majesty’s royal honor. The e-book elaborates the legacy and role of the Thai monarchy in 6 areas of development, namely, public health, sustainable development, water management, humanitarianism, foreign relations, and multiculturalism.
The e-book is available for download at https://info.mfa.go.th/static/pdf-flipbook-master/index.html?file=https://image.mfa.go.th/mfa/0/jgapjwQOLy/E-book/eBook_A_Legacy_for_All_6July2022.pdf
