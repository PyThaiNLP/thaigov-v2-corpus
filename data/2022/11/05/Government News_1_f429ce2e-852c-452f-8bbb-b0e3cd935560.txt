Govt Spokesperson: Vaccination plan on target
Govt Spokesperson: Vaccination plan on target
Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri gave an update that the Government’s plan for COVID-19 vaccination is on target. The number of vaccines given to people across the country during the first week of mass vaccination (June 7-12, 2021) totals 1,865,190 doses. Cumulative number of vaccines given between February 28- June 12, 2021 is 6,081,242 doses (first shot: 4,456,786 persons, and second shot: 1,624,456 persons). Currently, Thailand ranks 3rd on the ranking of ASEAN countries with the highest cumulative number of COVID-19 vaccination.
From June 7 onward, Ministry of Labor’s Social Security Officehas given out over 200,000 doses of COVID-19 vaccines. There have been 6,037,497 insured people who have registered for vaccination, out of which 3.6 million are residents of Bangkok and vicinity. Cumulative number of vaccinations in Bangkok and vicinity accounts for 1,678,948 doses (first shot: 944,552 persons, and second shot: 367,198 persons). Vaccine distribution to other provinces has also been undertaken according to the plan.
