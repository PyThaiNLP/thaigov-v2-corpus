PM gives guidance to Thai youths who are tasked to man “Thai Pavilion” at World Expo 2020 Dubai
September 14, 2021, at 1500hrs, at Bhakdi Bodin Building, Government House, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha was paid a courtesy call by and gave guidance to 25 Thai youths who have been selected as “Thailand Pavilion Ambassadors” to man Thai Pavilion at the World Expo 2020 Dubai.
According to the Prime Minister, World Expo 2020 Dubai will be an important opportunity to increase global awareness on various dimensions of Thailand, and to stimulate the country’s economic mobility, both in terms of trade, investment, and tourism. Thailand’s presence at the World Expo 2020 Dubai would also showcase and boost global confidence on the country’s tangible adoption of digital technology in tourism promotion endeavor, aside from the presentation of its unique identities.
The Prime Minister also commended the project that gave an opportunity for Thai youths to take part in and represent Thailand in the global event. They will be able to engage in the cultural exchange and presentation of Thai identities, i.e., Siamese smile, Thai food, beautiful nature, and humble characteristics of the Thai people. The Government has strived to “reinvent Thailand” toward security, prosperity, and sustainability both for the presence and into the future.
“Thailand Pavilion” at the World Expo 2020 Dubai will be set up under the theme “Mobility for the Future”. The Expo will be held during October 1, 2021- March 31, 2022.
