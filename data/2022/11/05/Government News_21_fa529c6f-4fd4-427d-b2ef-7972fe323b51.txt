PM welcomes foreign tourists to Phuket under Phuket Sandbox
PM welcomes foreign tourists to Phuket under Phuket Sandbox
Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that on July 1, 2021, at 1730hrs, ath the Arrival Hall of Phuket International Airport, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, together with Deputy Prime Minister and Public Health Minister Anutin Charnvirakul, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Energy Supattanapong Punmeechaow, Minister of Interior Gen. Anupong Paojinda, Minister of Tourism and Sports Phiphat Ratchakitprakarn, Minister of Transport Saksayam Chidchob, Minister of Natural Resources and Environment Varawut Silpa-archa, and Minister of Labor Suchart Chomklin, welcomed 51 Asian and European tourists on arrival from Singapore to Phuket via SQ763.
The Prime Minister’s greetings have impressed the tourists, some of whom asked to take a picture with the Prime Minister.
