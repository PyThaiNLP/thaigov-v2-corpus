Thailand to host 28th APEC SME Ministerial Meeting and 54th APEC SME Working Group Meeting
Thailand to host 28th APEC SME Ministerial Meeting and 54th
APEC SME Working Group Meeting
August 2, 2022, at 0845hrs, at Santi Maitri Building, Government House, prior to the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha attended the publicity campaign for Thailand’s hosting of the 28th APEC SME Ministerial Meeting and 54th APEC SME Working Group Meeting, as well as other related meetings, during September 5-10, 2022. Joining the Prime Minister at the event were Prime Minister’s Secretary General Distat Hotrakitya, Director-General of Office of SMEs Promotion (OSMEP) Veerapong Malai, and OSMEP executives. Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed gist as follows:
The 28th APEC SME Ministerial Meeting and 54th APEC SME Working Group Meeting will be held under the theme: “Inclusive Recovery of APEC MSMEs through BCG & High Impact Ecosystem”, with 4 sub-themes, namely, 1) Accelerating BCG Adoption 2) Inclusive Digital Transformation 3) The Next Normal MSME Financing and Debt Restructuring, and 4) Coping with evolving market landscape: high impact policy. The aim is to showcase capacity of Thai SMEs, which is the nation’s key driver, and the country’s readiness to welcome foreign trade and investment, and tourists from all over the world.
The Prime Minister was reported on the progress and readiness for hosting of the events, and visited the exhibition of various SMEs products that are beautifully designed according to customers’ preferences. The 28th APEC SME Ministerial Meeting and 54th APEC SME Working Group Meeting, as well as other related meetings, are held in accordance with the Government’s policy to drive forward the BCG economy and promote post-COVID-19 revival of SMEs situation.
