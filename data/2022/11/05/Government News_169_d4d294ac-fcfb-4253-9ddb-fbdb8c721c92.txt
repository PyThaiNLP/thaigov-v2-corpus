RT-PCR testing remains a requirement for incoming Thais and foreigners
November 30, 2021, at 1320hrs, at the Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha affirmed that Thailand will continue to use RT-PCR testing method for both Thais and foreigners who enter the country from 63 countries/territory that are exempted from quarantine requirement, and tourists under the Test & Go scheme. This is as proposed by Ministry of Public Health and concerned agencies and agreed upon at the cabinet meeting to prevent and control the spread of COVID-19’s Omicron variant.
The Prime Minister added that he would lead a delegation to Udon Thani province today (December 1). He has recently traveled to provinces in several regions to meet with and listen directly to the people in a bid to identify and promptly solve their problems. In Udon Thani, the Prime Minister would also visit and pay homage to local temple and sacred place for auspiciousness.
