​PM signs book of blessing for H.M. the King and Queen on New Year Day 2022
January 1, 2022, at 0900hrs, at the Red Room, the Grand Palace, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen Prayut Chan-o-cha, and Mrs. Naraporn Chan-o-cha, spouse, offered flower vases and signed a book of blessing for His Majesty the King and Her Majesty the Queen on occasion of the New Year 2022. Participating in the ceremony were Deputy Prime Ministers and spouses.
The Prime Minister and spouse paid respect to, and offered a flower vase in front of His Majesty’s and Her Majesty’s portraits, and 2 other flower vases on behalf of members of the cabinet and spouses respectively. He and the spouse, then, signed the book of blessings, and paid respect in front of His Majesty’s and Her Majesty’s portraits once again to complete the ceremony.
