Cabinet approves 3,995 million for procurement of COVID-19 medicines
July 18, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting today, approved procurement of COVID-19 medicines under the budget framework of 3,995.2708 million Baht. The implementation period is for 3 months (July- September 2022). New medicines and medical supplies to be procured are 1) approx. 27 million pills per month of Favipiravia/Molnupiravia (1,296.00 million Baht); and 2) 57,000 vials per month of Remdesivir (21.96 million Baht). The rest of the budget will be used to pay up for 165 pills of Favipiravia/Molnupiravia (2,653.81 million Baht), and 1 million sets of ATK (23.5 million Baht), which are the quota of March-June 2022.
The procurement of COVID-19 medicines and medical supplies is part of the Government’s response to the pandemic, and to support the work of medical personnel at all levels of healthcare facilities.
