​PM instructs government agencies to seek measures against energy price hike that have the least impact on people
March 11, 2022, at 13.30 hrs, at the Green Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha chaired the meeting with the group of Prime Minister’s advisors to discuss S-Curve industry investment plan.
Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed gist of the meeting as follows:
The Prime Minister acknowledged the progress of S-Curve investment in various areas, including the electric vehicle manufacturing, which saw tangible development and is well responded by the investors. He was also reported on the implementation of long-term resident visas to accommodate foreign investors and travelers, and promotion progress of digital, electronics, and medical industries.
The meeting also reported to the Prime Minister on the implementation of trade and investment promotion between Thailand and Saudi Arabia, such as downstream partnership on refinery and petrochemical industry, EV hub, biofuels and biochemicals, and video games/entertainment industry, among others, of which tangible progress should be seen by this month.
The Prime Minister also handed down a policy for his advisors to work proactively, progressively, and sustainably, and carefully adjust trade and investment plan and operation in a bid to keep up with changes of situation for the best interest of the nation. He also discussed current energy price hike, and instructed all agencies to seek proper countermeasures that have the least impact on the people.
