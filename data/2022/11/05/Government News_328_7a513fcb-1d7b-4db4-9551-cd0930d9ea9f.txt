Thailand and Saudi Arabia to advance energy cooperation
Thailand and Saudi Arabia to advance energy cooperation
May 15, 2022, at 06.30 hrs, at the Air force Base 6 (DMK), Mr. Yasir bin Othman Al-Rumayyan, Governor of the Public Investment Fund (PIF), Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, paid a courtesy call on Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha. Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed gist of the meeting as follows:
The Prime Minister expressed pleasure to meet with the PIF Governor, and congratulated PIF for being one of the world's largest sovereign wealth funds.  He also stated that Thailand is planning to lift its entry measures in accordance with the development of the COVID-19 situation administration, which would better facilitate Thailand-Saudi Arabia cooperation. Thailand is pleased with cordial relations between the two countries, and stands ready to further advance relations and cooperation with Saudi Arabia.
The PIF Governor expressed pleasure to meet with the Prime Minister and informed him that he was enjoined by His Royal Highness Prince Mohammad bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, the Crown Prince of Saudi Arabia, First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense, to discuss with the Prime Minister on reinforcement of cooperation between the two countries, especially in trade and investment, and energy (i.e., gas exploration).
Both parties took the opportunity to exchange views on mutual relations and cooperation in various areas, such as tourism, sports, e-sports, and soft power. Saudi Arabia hopes to have further business dialogues with Thailand to enhance trade and investment opportunity, and to use Thailand as its production base in the region.
