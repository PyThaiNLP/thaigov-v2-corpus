​Cabinet approves 150.69 million Baht to procure COVID-19 supplies for correctional facilities
February 22, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that the cabinet, in its meeting today, approved the budget of 150.69 million Baht for the Department of Corrections to procure 19 items of COVID-19 essential supplies as part of the Department’s effort to curb the spread of COVID-19 pandemic in correctional facilities across the country.
The list of essential supplies to be procured include 162,000 PPEs, 162,000 N95 masks, 540,000 rubber gloves, 454,674 ATK test kits, liquid alcohols, fingertip pulse oximeters, PCR liquid, among others. These supplies will be distributed to prisons and correctional facilities that are facing the shortage of COVID-19 supplies.
