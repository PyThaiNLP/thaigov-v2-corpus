PM pleased with Thailand’s signing of MOU on Cooperation for Suppressing Call Center Gang and Hybrid Scam with Cambodia
July 12, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha was pleased with the effort of concerned agencies to address the issue of call center gang and hybrid scam, which has been plaguing the Thai people.
According to the Government Spokesperson, Representatives of Ministry of Digital Economy and Society has travelled to Cambodia to meet with the Cambodian counterpart, and to sign the Memorandum of Understanding between the Ministry of Digital Economy and Society of the Kingdom of Thailand and the Ministry of Post and Telecommunications of the Kingdom of Cambodia on Cooperation for Suppressing Call Center Gang and Hybrid Scam.
The two countries have continued to work closely together to investigate and suppress call center gangs and hybrid scammers that have been lurking in Cambodia. Recently, 4 large call center gangs and 74 gang members have been arrested, while other 15 accomplices are interrogated.
The Prime Minister thanked concerned agencies for the success in suppressing call center gangs, which have caused damages to the Thai people and Government agencies. He also called on concerned agencies to rush in creating public awareness to ensure that they do not fall for the scams.
