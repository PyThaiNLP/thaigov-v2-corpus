PM to take field trip to Nakhon Si Thammarat Thursday Oct 7
Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha is scheduled for a field trip to Nakhon Si Thammarat province on October 7, 2021 (Thursday).
Upon arrival, the Prime Minister will observe and listen to the briefing on the province’s water management missions, and visit a community digital center at Mamuang Song Ton subdistrict, where he will chair the opening ceremony of the center, and handover hardware and equipment. The Prime Minister will also be presented with the exhibition on the Government’s key policies and performances, such as Pracharat internet network and digital services by National Telecom Public Company Limited, prompt awareness and action against natural disasters by the Thai Meteorological Department, and promotion of online sales and marketing by Thailand Post, followed by meeting with the local people.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister has genuine concern over the flood situation and flood-affected people in all areas, and would like to take field trips to as many flood-affected areas as possible to find out first hand the actual situation in order to promptly solve the problem and give moral support to the affected people.
