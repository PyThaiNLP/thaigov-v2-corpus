​PM signs condolence book on the passing of former Japanese PM
July 12, 2022 at 1530hrs, at the Embassy of Japan in Thailand, Wireless Road, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana revealed that Prime Minister and Minister of Defense General Prayuth Chan-o-cha  signed a book of condolences on the passing of former Prime Minister of Japan Shinzo Abe, and presented a flower vase on behalf of the country and the Thai people.
On this occasion, the Prime Minister had a talk with H.E. Mr. Nashida Kazuya, Japanese Ambassador to the Kingdom of Thailand, and conveyed his condolences over the passing of the former Prime Minister of Japan.
