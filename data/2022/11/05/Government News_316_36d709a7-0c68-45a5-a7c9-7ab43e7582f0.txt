​PM pleased with private sector’s donation of ATK sets and surgical masks
Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that on May 3, 2022, at 08.45 hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, prior to the cabinet meeting, Mr. Tony Yang, president of HIP Global Co., Ltd. and Security Services Association of Thailand president, paid a courtesy call on Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha to hand over 100,000 sets of ATK (Antigen Test Kit) and 100,000 surgical masks (KN95), worth 10 million Baht, to Office of the Prime Minister for official use. Witnessing the presentation was Prime Minister’s Secretary General Distat Hotrakitya.
The Prime Minister took the opportunity to thank the private sector for providing support and joining force with the Government in fighting against the spread of COVID-19 in Thailand. He also emphasized the importance of masks with good quality and affordable prices to ensure effective prevention and easier access of the people.
