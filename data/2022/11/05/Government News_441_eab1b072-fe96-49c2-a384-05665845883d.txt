​Thailand to host “APEC Health Week” during Aug 22-26
Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister and Acting Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha expressed confidence on Thailand’s capability to host various meetings and events under the APEC framework, including the “APEC Health Week”, to be held during August 22-26, 2022.
According to the Acting Government Spokesperson, Thailand will host the 12th High Level Meeting on Health and the Economy (HLMHE) under the theme: “Open to Partnership. Connect with the World. Balance Health and the Economy”, which will be the first public health conference (“APEC Health Week”) in the country. The meeting will be organized both onsite and online, and Thailand is now ready to welcome the attendance of public health ministers of the APEC member economies, heads of international organizations, i.e., ASEAN Secretary-General and APEC Secretariat Executive Director, as well as 150 high-level officials and representatives from various sectors.
The event will be an opportunity for Thailand to showcase its medical and public health capacity on par with the international standard, and to become world's medical hub, through the exhibition of medical service magnets, medical tourism activities, and high products hub, such as Thai traditional massage, spa treatment.
The Prime Minister thanked all concerned agencies for supporting and organizing various meetings under the APEC framework which fitted the country’s honor as APEC host. Thailand’s hosting of the APEC Health Week 2022 in the midst of world’s various challenges will also be an opportunity for all sides to join forces in attaining public health and economic balances in a bid to move toward sustainable and inclusive economic recovery.
