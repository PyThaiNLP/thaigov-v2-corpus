PM and spouse present floral offerings on occasion of Father’s Day on December 5, 2021
December 5, 2021, at 08.30 hrs, at Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha and Assoc. Prof. Naraporn Chan-o-cha, spouse, presided over the ceremony to present floral offerings and pay homage to His Majesty late King Bhumibol Adulyadej on occasion of Father’s Day on December 5, 2019. Participating in the ceremony were heads of the House of Representatives, Senate, and the Supreme Court, heads of constitutional organizations, cabinet members, Prime Minister’s Secretary-General, BMA Governor, Commanders-in Chief, Chief of the Royal Thai Police, permanent secretaries and officials of equivalent positions, and representatives of public agencies, state enterprises, and the private sector.
Upon arrival at the Sanam Luang Royal Plaza, the Prime Minister paid homage in front of His Majesty late King Bhumibol Adulyadej’s portrait, and offered traditional cone-shaped floral receptacles as the Prime Minister and on behalf of members of the cabinet, while the Prime Minister’s spouse presented the other floral receptacle on behalf of spouses of cabinet members.
He, then, made a statement in remembrance of His Majesty to express reverence and profound gratitude over his immeasurable benevolence on occasion of His Majesty’s birthday anniversary and Father’s Day on December 5, 2021. The Prime Minister stressed that all the Thai people commit to perform good deeds for the Royal Father, and would remember His teachings, forever be humble subjects of the late King, and pledge to live up to His aspirations.
After the laudatory statement, he led the participants to bow 3 times to complete the ceremony.
