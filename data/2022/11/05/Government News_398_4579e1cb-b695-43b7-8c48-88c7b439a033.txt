​​Thailand’s 3 provinces ranked among top 10 in survey on South-Eastern Asia: Health Care Index by City 2022 Mid-Year
Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha was pleased that Thailand's 3 provinces are ranked among top 10 Southeast Asian cities with the highest quality healthcare system for 2022 mid-year, according to the website Numbeo's survey on South-Eastern Asia: Health Care Index by City 2022 Mid-Year.
Top the ranking is Chiang Mai, followed by Bangkok, and Pattaya, at 3rd, and 4th respectively. Visitors of Numbeo, the website with largest database on cost of living and healthcare system, have been surveyed on overall quality of healthcare system, medical personnel, equipment, staffs, and medical expenses.
According to the survey, Chiang Mai is a popular city among foreigners thanks to its low cost of living, convenience, and cultural identity. There are large foreign communities in the province, as well as quality healthcare services, which are affordable comparing to similar treatment in hospitals abroad.
The Government Spokesperson added that the Prime Minister was pleased that Thai healthcare system has been acknowledged by the international community. The Government has set a policy to upgrade the country’s medical industry to becoming an inclusive medical hub. The Prime Minister also thanked all concerned sectors and agencies, be it, the public and private sectors, and medical R&D centers and facilities, as well as medical personnel, for their integrative effort to drive investment and development in medical industry, and promote Thailand’s healthcare reputation at an international level.
