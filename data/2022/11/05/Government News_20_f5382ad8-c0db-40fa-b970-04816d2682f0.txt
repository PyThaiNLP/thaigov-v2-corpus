PM visits Phuket’s Sirinat National Park
Government Spokesperson Anucha Burapachaisri disclosed that on July 1, 2021, at 1530hrs, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, together with Deputy Prime Minister and Public Health Minister Anutin Charnvirakul, Minister of Interior Gen. Anupong Paojinda, Minister of Tourism and Sports Phiphat Ratchakitprakarn, Minister of Transport Saksayam Chidchob, Minister of Natural Resources and Environment Varawut Silpa-archa, and PM’s Secretary General Distat Hotrakitya, took a field visit to Sirinat National Park in Thalang district, Phuket province to observe the Park’s preparation for the reopening of province under Phuket Sandbox scheme.
The Prime Minister took the opportunity to plant Sea Poison trees, and release 9 green turtles back to the nature. He, then, commended and gave his moral support to staffs of Sirinat National Park for their performing duties in sustainably conserving and protecting natural resources, and urged them to maintain cleanliness of the park, and provide facilitations to the incoming tourists.
