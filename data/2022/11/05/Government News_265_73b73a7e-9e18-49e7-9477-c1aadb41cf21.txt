PM orders Operation Center for Security Emergency Situation to support operation of 1330 NHSO Hotline
March 9, 2022, Government Spokesperson Thanakorn Wangboonkongchana disclosed that Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha, in his capacity as head of the Center for COVID-19 Situation Administration (CCSA), has ordered Operation Center for Security Emergency Situation to deploy its troop to support the National Health Security Office for the latter’s operation of 1330 COVID-19 Hotline in a bid to clear the backlog of COVID-19 case registration.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Operation Center for Security Emergency Situation has set up 200 lines to support 1330 Hotline. Officers to be deployed in the operation has undergone intensive training on March 7, 2022 about the NHSO protocol and related guideline, including reference of urgent and complicated cases to National Institute for Emergency Medicine’s 1669 Hotline. Each of the armed forces will prepare space and equipment for the operation.
The troop deployed by Operation Center for Security Emergency Situation will work from 0800-1630hrs every day. Supervisory system will also be set up to monitor and ensure operation efficiency.
