​PM leads government officials in blessing ceremony on occasion of His Majesty King Rama X’s Coronation Day anniversary on May 4, 2022
May 4, 2022, at 19.00 hrs, at the Outer Santi Maitri Building, Government House, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen Prayut Chan-o-cha, together with Assoc. Prof. Naraporn Chan-o-cha, spouse, presided over a blessing ceremony on occasion of His Majesty King Rama X’s Coronation Day anniversary on May 4, 2022.
At an opportune timing, the Prime Minister paid homage to His Majesty’s portrait by offering traditional cone-shaped floral receptacle and homage offering, and delivered a felicitation to praise His Majesty’s glory and honor. The royal anthem was, then, played by a traditional band to end the ceremony.
The blessing ceremony on occasion of His Majesty King Rama X’s Coronation Day anniversary on May 4, 2022 was participated by prominent figures and representatives of various agencies, namely, cabinet members and spouses, President of National Legislative Assembly, President of the Senate, President of the Supreme Court, President of the Constitutional Court, President of the Supreme Administrative Court, Chairman of the Election Commission of Thailand, Chairman of the National Human Rights Commission, Chairman of the National Anti-Corruption Commission, Chairman of the State Audit Commission, Ombudsman, Attorney General, presidents of standing committees for the House of Representatives and the Senate, Prime Minister’s Secretary-General, advisors to the Prime Minister, Assistant to Minister attached to the Prime Minister’s Office, Deputy Secretary-General to the Prime Minister for Political Affairs, Government Spokesperson, permanent secretaries and chiefs of ministry-level agencies and equivalent officials, executives of Prime Minister’s Secretary General Office, Commanders in Chief of the Royal Thai Armed Forces and the three armed forces, Commissioner-General of the Royal Thai Police, Dean of the Diplomatic Corps, senior ambassadors to various regions, President of the Board of Trade of Thailand, President of the Federation of Thai Industries, President of the Thai Bankers' Association, and President of Foreign Correspondents' Club of Thailand, and spouses.
