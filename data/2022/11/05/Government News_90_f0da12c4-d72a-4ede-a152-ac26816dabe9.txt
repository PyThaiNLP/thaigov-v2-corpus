PM chairs 2/2021 meeting of National Land Policy Committee
September 13, 2021, at 1505hrs, at Bhakdi Bodin Building, Government House, Prime Minister and Defense Minister Gen. Prayut Chan-o-cha gave an interview, following the 2/2021 meeting of the National Land Policy Committee, that land policy is the Government’s key policy to promote proper productive land tenure and GAP certification of crops to facilitate exportation. The National Land Policy Committee is the Government’s mechanism to legalize productive land tenure in line with agro product demands. Legalization of productive land tenure will be expedited in all provinces to enhance efficiency and competitiveness of agricultural business and the country’s economic sector.
According to the Prime Minister, the National Land Policy Committee is considered the hardware, which should be operated in parallel with the software, that is, various digital platforms to enhance capacity and facilitate online trading. Ministry of Digital Economy and Society will be the implementing agency to develop Big Data that serves the purposes and promote use of digital technology in driving forward the country. The Prime Minister also emphasized that an important thing to beware of is data hacking.
