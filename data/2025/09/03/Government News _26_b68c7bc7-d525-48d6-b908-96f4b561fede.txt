Govt to host Grand Reception in honor of H.M. the King on occasion of His Majesty’s birthday anniversary on July 28, 2025
Govt to host Grand Reception in honor of H.M. the King on occasion of His Majesty’s birthday anniversary on July 28, 2025
July 22, 2025, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that the Cabinet Secretariat announced the postponement of the 30/2025 cabinet meeting on July 29, 2025to 1300hrs on the same day.
In the morning, at 1000hrs, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior, acting Prime Minister Phumtham Wechayachai, is scheduled to have an audience with Their Majesties the King and Queen at the Buddhist rituals to be held at the AmarindraWinitchai Hall in the Grand Palace.
Later at 1900hrs, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior, as acting Prime Minister, will have an audience with Her Royal Highness Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn at the Grand Reception in honor of H.M. the King on occasion of His Majesty’s birthday anniversary on July 28, 2025, to be hosted by the Government.
On this occasion, the Government would like to invite all the Thai people to watch live telecast of the Grand Reception in honor of H.M. the King on occasion of His Majesty’s birthday anniversary which will be aired via TV Pool of Thailand on July 29, 2025 from 1900hrs onward. They are also encouraged to wear yellow during July 28- August 1, 2025 in honor of His Majesty.
