Thailand – Cambodia situation remains stable as of morning of Aug 5
August 5, 2025, at 0700hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub, as member of Ad Hoc Center for Thailand-Cambodia Border Situation, updated the overall situation along the Thai -Cambodian border in 7 provinces that the situation remains stable with no report of any clash nor violence. Security authorities, however, continue to closely monitor the situation and stay vigilant at all times.
Thailand and Cambodia are now holding a General Border Committee (GBC) meeting during August 4-7, 2025. The Secretariat meetings are taking place on August 4-6, 2025, with the main meeting scheduled for August 7, 2025.
