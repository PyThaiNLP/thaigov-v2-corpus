The cabinet met on July 3, 2025.
Title: Request for exemption from compliance with Cabinet Resolutions for the use of mangrove forest areas for construction of Highway No. 3702, Ban Bang Kwai - Ban Khao Din section, (Bang Pakong River Bridge) in Tha Sao and Khao Din Subdistricts, Bang Pakong District, Chachoengsao Province
The cabinet approved the proposal of Ministry of Transport to exempt compliance with Cabinet Resolutions, dated July 23, 1991, August 22, 2000, and October 17, 2000, for the use of mangrove forest, with the total area of 1 rai 1 ngan 23.71 square wah, for the construction of Highway No. 3702, Ban Bang Kwai - Ban Khao Din section, (Bang Pakong River Bridge) in Tha Sao and Khao Din Subdistricts, Bang Pakong District, Chachoengsao Province.
Gist
Highway No. 3702, Ban Bang Kwai - Ban Khao Din section, (Bang Pakong River Bridge) in Tha Sao and Khao Din Subdistricts, Bang Pakong District, Chachoengsao Province will be connected with Highway No. 314 to accommodate traffic volume as a result of community expansion, and to reduce accidents on Highway No. 3701 and 3702.
However, a part of the construction site covers a 1 rai 1 ngan 23.71 square-wah mangrove forest area, so there is a need to request for the cabinet’s approval for using the area.
Title: Delegation of Authority to Deputy Prime Ministers as Acting Prime Minister
The cabinet approved the proposal of the Prime Minister’s Secretariat on the delegation of authority of the deputy prime ministers as acting prime minister.
1. Delegate deputy prime ministers in the following order as acting prime minister:
1) Mr. Phumtham Wechayachai
2) Mr. Suriya Jungrungreangkit
3) Mr. Pirapan Salirathavibhaga
4) Mr. Pichai Chunhavajira
5) Mr. Prasert Jantararuangtong
2. As acting prime minister, the person in 1. shall hold similar authorities and duties as the Prime Minister and has the power and duty as chairman of any committee or organization
3. In the event that the acting Prime Minister under 1. (2) – (5) shall give an order regarding personnel administration and budget approval that is under the authority of the Prime Minister, approval from the Cabinet must be obtained first.
