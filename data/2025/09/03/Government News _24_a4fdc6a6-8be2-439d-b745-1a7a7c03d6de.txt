Govt denies approving projects under 157-billion-Baht economic stimulus plan to single bidder
July 21, 2025, at 1200hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that the Department of Comptroller General has dismissed the news about public procurements under the 157-billion-Baht economic stimulus plan, which alleged that some projects have only one bidder and have eventually been awarded to the single bidder.
According to the Department of Comptroller General, all public procurements must be carried out in accordance with relevant laws to ensure utmost benefit to respective government agencies. Each bidding will align with the principles of value for money, transparency, efficiency, effectiveness, and auditability, as stipulated in Section 8 of the Public Procurement and Supplies Administration. Act, B.E. 2560 (2017). All government agencies are required to strictly adhere to all procurement-related regulations within the budgetary framework.
