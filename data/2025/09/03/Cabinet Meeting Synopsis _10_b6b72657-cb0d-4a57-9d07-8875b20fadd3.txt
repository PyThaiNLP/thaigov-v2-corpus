The cabinet met on August 26, 2025
Some of the resolutions are as follows:
Title: Request for withdrawal of draft ministerial regulation prescribing characteristics or provision of equipment, facilities, or services in buildings, venues, vehicles, and transport services to promote accessibility and utilization of people with disabilities, B.E. ....
The cabinet approved withdrawal of the draft ministerial regulation prescribing characteristics or provision of equipment, facilities, or services in buildings, venues, vehicles, and transport services to promote accessibility and utilization of people with disabilities, B.E. ...., as proposed by Ministry of Finance.
Title: Draft Ministerial Regulations prescribing procurement of supplies through specific methods
The cabinet approved in principle the draft Ministerial Regulations prescribing procurement of supplies through specific methods, as proposed by Ministry of Finance.
Gist
The draft Ministerial Regulations is a revision to Ministerial Regulations prescribing procurement of supplies through specific methods, B.E. 2561. The revision is made to include procurement of supplies for anti-cybersecurity threats and military-related threats which affect or may affect stability of a government agency or the country. Such supplies include cloud/data center leasing for the storage of confidential or critical data related to national security, and armories, among others. These procurements must be carried out with urgency and high flexibility to prevent and resolve the situation as quickly as possible or within a limited timeframe for national security’s sake.
Title: Nomination of Thailand’s 3-year hosting of World Capital Marathon Series (Amazing Thailand Marathon Bangkok), 2025-2027
The cabinet approved in principle on the nomination of Thailand’s 3-year hosting of World Capital Marathon Series (Amazing Thailand Marathon Bangkok), 2025-2027, under the budget framework of 264,352,941.15 Baht, as proposed by Ministry of Tourism and Sports.
Gist
World Athletics has granted Thailand the rights to host the World Capital Marathon Series. Total budget for event preparation, including copyright fees and related taxes, is 264,352,941.15 Baht. It is expected that hosting the event over the 3-year period will generate economic value of no less than 4.377 billion Baht, and over 114,000 runners and 75,000 spectators will participate in the event.
The event will be a one-day event, and expected event dates are: Year 1: November 30, 2025; Year 2: A date between November 26-29, 2026; and Year 3: A date between November 25-28, 2027.
Title: Draft Joint Ministerial Statement for 15th APEC Energy Ministers' Meeting 2025
The cabinet approved the draft Joint Ministerial Statement for 15th APEC Energy Ministers' Meeting 2025. Should there be an amendment in parts that are not gist nor against national interest, Minister of Energy may proceed and later inform the cabinet of the amendment. Minister of Energy or a representative is authorized to adopt the draft Joint Statement. This is as proposed by Ministry of Energy.
Gist
Korea will host the 15th Energy Ministers' Meeting on August 27-28 in Busan under the theme: “Building a Sustainable Tomorrow”. The Joint Ministerial Statement will be adopted, which will serve as a guideline for driving APEC Energy Goals, that is, to double use of renewable energy in the region by 2030 and to reduce energy consumption by 45% by 2035 through: 1) promoting strong economic growth; 2) strengthening cooperation among APEC member economies on exchange of knowledge for energy security and regional economic resilience; 3) promoting the development of sustainable energy system; 4) increasing capacity of power transmission lines and modernize power grid systems; 5) promoting integrated development of AI technology; 6) fostering a comprehensive approach to recovery from crises; and 7) enhancing stability of sustainable energy sourcing.
