Thailand – Cambodia situation remains stable as of morning of Aug 19
August 19, 2025, at 0700hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub, as member of Ad Hoc Center for Thailand-Cambodia Border Situation, updated the overall situation along the Thai -Cambodian border in 7 provinces that the situation remains stable with no report of any clash nor violence. Thai military troops remain on guard at all 11 fortified locations, and continue to install barbed wire fences to protect Thailand's sovereignty and prevent any unauthorized entry.
Thailand also reaffirmed its commitment to upholding the unconditional ceasefire agreement, which has taken effect since midnight of July 28, and to pursuing a peaceful resolution to the border problem.
At the same time, the Thai Government remains adamant to protecting Thailand's sovereignty and will not allow any violation of its territorial integrity. Barbed wire fences will continue to be installed and will not be removed in any case to protect the Thai sovereignty and ensure safety of people along the border area.
