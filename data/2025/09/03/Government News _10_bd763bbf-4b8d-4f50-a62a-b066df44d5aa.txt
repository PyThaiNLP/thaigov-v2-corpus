Govt Spokesperson: Special cabinet meeting to be held on July 3
July 2, 2025, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that the Cabinet Secretariat has made announcement about a special cabinet meeting, which will be held on July 3, 2025 at 1400hrs at the Command Building I, Government House. The special cabinet meeting will be chaired by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Transport Suriya Juangroongruangkit (Acting Prime Minister). Main agenda of the meeting is to delegate tasks and responsibilities to deputy prime ministers and ministers.
In the morning of the same day, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Transport Suriya Juangroongruangkit (Acting Prime Minister) will lead new members of the cabinet to take an oath of allegiance in front of His Majesty the King at Amphorn Sathan Residential Hall, Bangkok.
