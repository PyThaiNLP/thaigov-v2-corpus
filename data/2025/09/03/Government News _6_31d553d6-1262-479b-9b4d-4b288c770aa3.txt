Thailand named Top Global Spa & Wellness Destination Award
June 26, 2025, at 0830hrs, Deputy Government Spokesperson Sasikarn Watthanachan disclosed that Thailand is recognized as Top Global Spa & Wellness Destination at the Hospitality Horizon Spa & Wellness Summit & Awards 2025, held on June 14 in Mumbai BKC, India.
A panel of experts in spa and wellness, including famous celebrities from the Republic of India, selected Thailand for its strength in holistic wellness that perfectly incorporates traditional wisdoms, nature, and world-class hospitality. This award cements the country’s image as a world-class health and wellness destination, and will help create value-added to Thai tourism products and services.
To further underscore the country's health and wellness service potential, “Thailand Wellness and Healthcare Expo 2025” will be held during June 26-29, 2025, at BITEC convention center. The event will gather entrepreneurs, businessmen, investors, and international experts who will exchange knowledge, create networks, and share the latest trends in the health and wellness industry. It is expected that the event will generate over 120 million baht.
According to the Deputy Government Spokesperson, the Government strives to promote Thailand’s healthcare and wellness tourism to become a world-class health & wellness destination, focusing on adding values with comprehensive health treatments and rehabilitations. Entrepreneurs are, therefore, encouraged to design various activities in line with global wellness tourism trends.
