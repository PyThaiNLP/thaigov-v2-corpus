Jirayu discloses Deputy Prime Minister Phumtham holds telephone discussion with Malaysian Prime Minister
to follow up on Thai-Cambodian border situation, reaffirms IOT operation under GBC framework, thanks Malaysia for supporting Thailand’s peaceful approach
August 19, 2025, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub, as member of Ad Hoc Center for Thailand-Cambodia Border Situation, disclosed that at 12.30 hrs, at the office of the Deputy Prime Minister, 4th Floor, Command Building 1, Government House, Mr. Phumtham Wechayachai, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior, Acting Prime Minister, held a telephone discussion with Prime Minister Dato’ Seri Anwar Ibrahim of Malaysia regarding the Thai-Cambodian border situation, particularly on the operations of the Interim Observer Team (IOT).
This meeting was also joined by the Minister of Foreign Affairs, the Deputy Minister of Defence, and the Secretary-General to the Prime Minister.Deputy Prime Minister reaffirmed that Thailand wishes for the IOT, which has just commenced working, to carry out its work in accordance with the principles and framework agreed upon at the previous Extraordinary Meeting of the General Border Committee (GBC) on 7 August 2025. Deputy Prime Minister further proposed that the next GBC meeting scheduled to take place next month may review the operations of the IOT if necessary.
Government Spokesperson said that Deputy Prime Minister also expressed gratitude to Prime Minister Anwar for his role in helping the situation move toward a more positive direction, and for Malaysia’s readiness to support Thailand’s stance in seeking a peaceful resolution based on the Thai-Cambodian bilateral consultations.
