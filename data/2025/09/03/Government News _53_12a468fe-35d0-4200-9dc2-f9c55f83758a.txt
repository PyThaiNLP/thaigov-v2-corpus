​Thailand – Cambodia situation remains stable as of morning of Aug 6
August 6, 2025, at 0700hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub, as member of Ad Hoc Center for Thailand-Cambodia Border Situation, updated the overall situation along the Thai -Cambodian border in 7 provinces from late at night of August 5 until this morning that the situation remains stable with no report of any clash nor violence. The EOD unit continues to inspect risk-prone areas to destroy any remaining unexploded ordinances. Government agencies, the private sector, and the public are also working closely together to provide support and assistance in the civilian assembly areas.
General Border Committee (GBC) meeting between Thailand and Cambodia are now underway with an attempt to reach sustainable ceasefire agreement.
