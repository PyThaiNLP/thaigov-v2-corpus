Thailand – Cambodia situation remains stable as of morning of Aug 4
August 4, 2025, at 0700hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub, as member of Ad Hoc Center for Thailand-Cambodia Border Situation, updated the overall situation along the Thai -Cambodian border in 7 provinces that the situation remains stable with no report of any clash nor violence.
Nonetheless, security authorities continue to closely monitor the situation and stay vigilant at all times. Military forces have still been deployed according to the operational plan to safeguard Thai sovereignty and people’s safety.
