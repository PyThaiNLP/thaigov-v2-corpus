Chiang Mai named best Asian city in 2025
July 16, 2025, at 0845hrs, Deputy Government Spokesperson Sasikarn Watthanachan disclosed that Chiang Mai has been named the No. 1 City in Asia in the Travel + Leisure World’s Best Awards 2025 with 91.94 points, whereas Bangkok ranks 3rd, after Tokyo (91.39 points), with 91.34 points.
According to Travel + Leisure, a renowned tourism and lifestyle online platform, Chiang Mai is praiseworthy not only for its superiority in culture, ways of life, local food, and hospitality, but also wide-ranging tourist attractions, e.g., Wat Phrathat Doi Suthep, green and mountainous nature, beautiful national parks, etc. It also boasts various environmental activities, such as elephant feeding and washing, which have attracted a lot of tourists.
Top 15 Asian cities ranked by Travel + Leisure are:
1. Chiang Mai, Thailand
2. Tokyo, Japan
3. Bangkok, Thailand
4. Jaipur, India
5. Hoi An, Vietnam
6. Kyoto, Japan
7. Ubud (Bali), Indonesia
8. Siem Reap, Cambodia
9. Mumbai, India
10. Agra, India
11. Osaka, Japan
12. Hanoi, Vietnam
13. Taipei, Taiwan
14. Singapore
15. Seoul, South Korea
The Deputy Government Spokesperson added that the Government appreciates the Thai people of all sectors for joining hands in welcoming and ensuring safety of the tourists. The ranking is another testimony to Thailand’s quality tourism and its potential to become regional tourism hub.
