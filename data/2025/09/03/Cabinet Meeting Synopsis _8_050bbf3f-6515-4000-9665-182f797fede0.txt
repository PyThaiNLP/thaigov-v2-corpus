The cabinet met on August 5, 2025
Some of the resolutions are as follows:
Title: Draft Ministerial Regulation on Thai Industrial Standard for cooking paper, B.E. …
The cabinet approved in principle the draft Ministerial Regulation on Thai Industrial Standard for cooking paper, B.E. …, as proposed by Ministry of Industry.
Gist
The draft Ministerial Regulation on Thai Industrial Standard for cooking paper, B.E. …, prescribes Thai Industrial Standard for cooking paper under TIS 2948-2562, to be in accordance with the Ministerial Announcement No. 5417 (B.E. 2562), issued under the Industrial Product Standards Act, B.E. 2511, on Thai Industrial Standard for cooking paper.
The draft Ministerial Regulation shall take effect 180 days after being announced in the Royal Gazette.

Title: Royal Decree on Re-Election of Member of House of Representatives for Chiang Rai Province, Constituency 7, B.E. ....
The cabinet approved the draft Royal Decree on Re-Election of Member of House of Representatives for for Chiang Rai Province, Constituency 7, B.E. .... to fill in vacancy, following the membership termination of former MP Pichet Chuamuangpan in accordance with Article 144 (3) of the Constitution of Thailand.
Gist
The re-election shall be held within 45 days after the vacancy (by September 14, 2025). It is expected that the reelection will take place on September 14, 2025.
Title: Review of cabinet resolution, dated December 18, 2001, on revision of organizations for prevention and suppression of petroleum-related offenses
The cabinet approved the review of cabinet resolution, dated December 18, 2001, on revision of organizations for prevention and suppression of petroleum-related offenses, as proposed by Ministry of Energy. The revision shall take effect from FY2027 onward.
Gist
A revision prescribes the change of responsible agency for requesting budget allocation for the prevention and suppression of petroleum-related offenses from “Ministry of Finance” to "agency that wishes to receive the budget allocation for the prevention and suppression of petroleum-related offenses”, i.e., Energy Policy and Planning Office, Department of Energy Business, Excise Department, Customs Department, Royal Thai Police, and Royal Thai Navy.
The revision is aimed at reflecting actual budget disbursement and expenditure of each agency, which will lead to better budget management efficiency. This also enables these agencies to monitor and expedite disbursements in line with their designated plans.
Title: Extension of timeframe and increase of budget framework for construction of Nam Pi Royal Initiative Reservoir Project in Phayao Province
The cabinet approved the following proposals of Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives:
Approved extension of implementation period for the construction of Nam Pi Royal Initiative Reservoir Project in Phayao Province from 10 years (FY2016 – 2025) to 13 years (FY2016 – 2028)
Approved increase of the project’s budget framework from 3,981 million Baht to 5,000 million Baht
Gist
The Nam Pi Royal Initiative Reservoir Project was approved by the cabinet on November 3, 2015 with an aim to increase water budget of 96 million cubic metres, and expand irrigational area in Phayao’s Chiang Muan distict to 35,000 rai. However, the project is facing delays because original construction contractor has not been able to complete the work according to the contract. Additionally, the project's budget was estimated based on the preliminary design, and prices of construction materials and fuel from 2015. Land value was also higher, and some residents whose land was to be expropriated did not accept the compensation offers. As a result, the land compensation value had to be increased. In light of the above, budget framework for the project must be increased.
Title: Political Declaration for Third UN Conference on Landlocked Developing Countries (LLDC3)
The cabinet approved the draft Political Declaration for Third UN Conference on Landlocked Developing Countries (LLDC3). Should there be an amendment in parts that are not gist nor against national interest, Ministry of Foreign Affairs may proceed without having to resubmit to the cabinet for approval. Deputy Permanent Secretary to Ministry of Foreign Affairs is assigned as head of the Thai delegation at the LLDC3 to adopt the Political Declaration. This is as proposed by Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
Gist
The Third UN Conference on Landlocked Developing Countries (LLDC3) will be hosted by Turkmenistan on August 5-8, 2025. The Political Declaration stipulates the call for an increase of global cooperation to promote sustainable development in Landlocked Developing Countries (LLDCs), be it, developing accessible physical and digital trade infrastructure, and calling for international development banks to increase investment in LLDC in physical infrastructure, energy and transport connectivity infrastructure, and digital infrastructure.
Title: 2025 APEC Women and the Economy Forum Ministerial Statement
The cabinet approved the 2025 APEC Women and the Economy Forum Ministerial Statement, as proposed by Ministry of Social Development and Human Security.
Gist
The 2025 APEC Women and the Economy Forum Ministerial Statement prescribes the need to empower women and increase their economic engagement, as well as to create a safe society through eliminating and preventing violence against women.
The 2025 APEC Women and the Economy Forum Ministerial Meeting will be held on August 12, 2025 in Incheon, Republic of Korea.
