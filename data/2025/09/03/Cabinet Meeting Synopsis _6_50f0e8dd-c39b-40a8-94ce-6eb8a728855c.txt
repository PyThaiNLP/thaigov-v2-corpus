Title: Draft 2nd Master Plan for Agricultural Development (2025- 2032)
The cabinet approved the draft 2nd Master Plan for Agricultural Development (2025- 2032), and assigned National Farmers Council.
Gist
The National Agricultural Council has developed the 2nd Master Plan for Agricultural Development (2025- 2032), which is in line with the 20-Year National Strategy and the 13th National Economic and Social Development Plan. Objectives of this Master Plan are to enhance farmers’ capacity, promote green agriculture that generates income for farmers and is environmentally friendly, and to increase economic value of the agricultural sector. Key targets of the Master plan are: (1) 75% of agricultural households (5.78 million households) are empowered and ready to create opportunities and adapt to change. (2) Agricultural and agro-industrial export value increases by 5% annually. (3) Agricultural Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is no less than 4.5% annually. (4) Net cash income for agricultural households is no less than 537,000 Baht per household by 2570 B.E. (2027 A.D.).
Title: Request for approval of Government’s FY2025 central fund for emergency and contingency for land acquisition, compensation, and relocation expenses for implementation of Lam Nam Chee Reservoir Project Under the Royal Initiatives in Chaiyaphum Province
The cabinet approved the proposal of Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives (the Royal Irrigation Department) to allocate the Government’s FY2025 central fund for emergency and contingency, at the budget framework of 602.74 million Baht, for land acquisition, compensation, and relocation expenses for the implementation of Lam Nam Chee Reservoir Project Under the Royal Initiatives in Chaiyaphum Province.
Gist
The Royal Irrigation Department is instructed to urgently proceed according to all relevant laws, regulations, rules, and the cabinet resolutions taking into account utmost benefit for both the Government and the public. The approval will support the construction project's operations, and alleviate hardship of the landowners affected by the implementation of this project.
Title: Outcome of 3/2025 meeting of Emergency Situation Administration Committee
The cabinet approved the extension of state of emergency in the areas of Narathiwat Province, excluding Yi-ngo District, Su-ngai Kolok District, Waeng District, and Sukhirin District; Pattani Province, excluding Yaring District, Panare District, Mayo District, Mai Kaen District, Thung Yang Daeng District, Kapho District, and Mae Lan District; and Yala Province, excluding Betong District, Yaha District, Raman District, Kabang District, and Krong Pinang District. The state of emergency will be extended for another 3 months, from July 20, 2025 to October 19, 2025, making it the 81st extension. This is as proposed by the National Security Council.
Gist
The extension is approved to ensure the authorities’ operation continuity in preventing, suppressing, and quickly de-escalating incidents in the area, and to be able to continue to hold suspects of violence in the Southern border area for interrogation.

Title: Request for budget to host FIVB Women’s World Championships 2025
The cabinet approved the Government’s FY2025 central fund for emergency and contingency, at the amount of 424,177,200 Baht, to pay for the license to host a volleyball tournament “FIVB Women’s World Championships 2025” during August 22 – September 7, 2025 in Bangkok, Nakhon Ratchasima, Phuket, and Chiang Mai. The license fee will have to be paid by July 15, 2025. This is as proposed by Ministry of Tourism and Sports.
Gist
Through hosting of the host FIVB Women’s World Championships 2025, economic value that Thailand is expected to gain is approximately 8,435,700,000 Baht. Additionally, the tournament is expected to enhance Thailand’s positive image in the eyes of the global community through live broadcast of the competitions, and to showcase the country's readiness and potential in all aspects, including tourism, the economy, and society, creating confidence among international tourists and investors.
