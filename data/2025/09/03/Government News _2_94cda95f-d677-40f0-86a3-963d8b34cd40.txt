PM bestows Supreme Patriarch with offerings on occasion of His Holiness’s birthday anniversary
June 24, 2025, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that prior to the cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and spouse visited Wat Ratchabophit Sathitmahasimaram Ratchaworawihan Temple to bestow the Supreme Patriarch with the offerings on occasion of His Holiness’s birthday anniversary on June 26, 2025.
Upon arrival at Wat Ratchabophit Sathitmahasimaram Ratchaworawihan, the Prime Minister and spouse offered a garland to pay homage to the statue of King Rama V, and later paid a courtesy call on Somdej Phra Mahawirawong, Secretary to the Supreme Patriarch, member of the Sangha Supreme Council, and assistant abbot of Wat Ratchabophit Sathit Maha Simaram Ratchaworawihan.
She, then, bestowed the Supreme Patriarch with the offerings (incense sticks and candles, lotus pedestal tray, monk robes, and articles) before the Supreme Patriarch sprinkled lustral water upon the Prime Minister for her good fortunes.
