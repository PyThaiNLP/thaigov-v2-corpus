Minister of Foreign Affairs to clarify to UN member states on Cambodia’s initial attack on Thailand
July 24, 2025, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub, as member of the Ad Hoc Center for Thailand-Cambodia Border Situation, disclosed that Minister of Foreign Affairs Maris Sangiampongsa, together with his delegation, has been at the UN Headquarters in New York since July 21 to attend the High-Level Political Forum on Sustainable Development 2025 (HLPF2025). This is a timely opportunity for Thailand to clarify to the global community on the ongoing tension between Thailand and Cambodia.
At 1800hrs (Thailand time) today, Minister of Foreign Affairs will have a meeting via the videoconference with Thai relevant agencies to review preparation and process for the evacuation of Thai nationals in Cambodia if necessary. He will also talk with representatives of UN member states about the initial attack of Cambodia on Thailand, and the evidences that prove Cambodia’s violation against different international laws and convention, as well as against the international principle, for its strikes on civilian targets including hospitals and public areas.
