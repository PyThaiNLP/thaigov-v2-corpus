รัฐบาลไทย-ข่าวทำเนียบรัฐบาล-Trang ready to host 22nd Chief Ministers and Governors Forum (CMGF)

วันอาทิตย์ที่ 31 สิงหาคม 2568
Trang ready to host 22nd Chief Ministers and Governors Forum (CMGF)
and 31st IMT-GT Ministerial Meeting
August 29, 2025, Deputy Government Spokesperson Sasikarn Watthanachan disclosed the readiness of the National Economic and Social Development Council, in collaboration with, Trang provincial administrations, to host the 22nd Chief Ministers and Governors Forum (CMGF) on September 3, 2025 and the 31stIndonesia-Malaysia-Thailand Growth Triangle (IMT-GT) Ministerial Meeting on September 4, 2025.
The meetings are aimed at reviewing and advancing trilateral economic cooperation plan under the 5-Year Action Plan (2022–2026) and the IMT-GT Vision 2036, which covers cooperation in various fields, i.e., infrastructure, tourism, agriculture and agricultural products, trade and investment, and human resource development. Projects to be deliberated at the meetings are for example, construction of road connecting New Sadao customs checkpoint with Bukit Kayu Hitam checkpoint; Hat Yai–Padang Besar dual-track railway project; development of airports and seaports in the southern provinces; Srivijaya beef cattle farmingproject; and restoration of cultural tourism attractions, among others.
Hosting the meetings not only enhance potential of Trang province and the Southern region as a whole, but also demonstrate Thailand’s capacity as regional hub for economic cooperation. It is also expected to bring about new opportunities, especially in trade and investment, and tourism, and better quality of life of the people in all areas.
