PM delivers statement on AH 1446 Idul Fitri
March 30, 2025, at 2000hrs, at the Purple Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed the statement of Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra, delivered on occasion of AH 1446 Idul Fitri to convey well wishes and love to the Muslim Thais in the country and abroad.
Idul Fitri marks the end of the Holy Month of Ramadan and accomplishment of the Muslims in performing their religious duty with pure mind, sacrifice, and devout faith in Allāh. The Prime Minister respected their strong will and forbearance to perform good deeds and treat others with kindness, forgiveness, and love. May their strong spirit to adhere to faith and beautiful practices in Islam lead to happiness, prosperity and forever peace of mind.
