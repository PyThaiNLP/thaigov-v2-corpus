Prime Minister’s Statement on Holy Month of Ramadan (AH 1446)
February 28, 2025, at 1950hrs, at the Purple Room, Thai Khu Fah Building, Government House, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra delivered a statement on occasion of the Holy Month of Ramadan for AH 1446. Gist of her statement is as follows:
The Prime Minister conveyed well wishes and love to the Muslims throughout the country and the world on occasion of the Holy Month of Ramadan for AH 1446.
The Holy Month of Ramadan is a good opportunity for the Muslims to exercise mindfulness and perseverance, and to enhance mental strength and purity with strong faith. The Prime Minister congratulated them for the chance to perform their religious ritual with strong will and forbearance in fasting, and exercise kindness and non-violence, all of which are key fundamentals for pureness to the body and mind. Studying the Noble Qur’an is truly a means to express their respect and faith in Allāh.
On the auspicious occasion of the Holy Month of Ramadan AH1446, the Prime Minister asked for the blessing of Allāh for the happiness and joy of all Muslims in Thailand. May they perform their religious duties during the month of Ramadan and have their Duʿā fulfilled. She also wished for prosperity, and success in all endeavors.
