Cabinet approved allocation of central budget to promote sustainable management of marine resources and tackle PM 2.5 particulates
March 3, 2025, at 1010hrs, at the Command Building, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that the cabinet approved the budget proposal of Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives to remove fishing vessels from the system for sustainable management of marine resources. The budget of 1,622.60 million Baht will be allocated from the FY2025 central budget to compensate for the remove of 923 vessels of which the owners expressed willingness to give up their fishing career and vessels.
The cabinet also approved the budget framework of 200 million Baht from the FY2025 central budget for the procurement of cloud seeding substance generators (formula 3: dry ice) to be used for rainmaking operations in Tak, Phitsanulok, Buriram, and Khon Kaen provinces. These generators will increase efficiency of royal rainmaking operations and weather modification to tackle drought and PM 2.5 particulate.
