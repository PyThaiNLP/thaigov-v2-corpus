Thailand-India relations to be elevated to Strategic Partnership
March 28, 2025, at 1100hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed about the official visit of the Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi as guest of the Thai Government during April 3-4, 2025.
On April 3, 2025, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and members of the cabinet will host an official welcoming ceremony for the Indian Prime Minister at the Government House, followed by a plenary meeting, the signing ceremony of agreements and MoUs, a joint press conference, and a luncheon hosted by the Thai Prime Minister in honor of the Indian counterpart at 1300hrs.
This will be the first official visit of an Indian Prime Minister in 12 years, and the first for Prime Minister Modi. During the visit, the two leaders will discuss promotion of relations and cooperation, both at the bilateral, multilateral, and regional levels. An elevation of relations to strategic partnership will also be announced during the official visit of the Indian Prime Minister to strengthen closer ties between Thailand and India in all dimensions.
