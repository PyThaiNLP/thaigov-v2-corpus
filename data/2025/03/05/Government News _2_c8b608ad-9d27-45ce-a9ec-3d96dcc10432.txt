PM meets with DKSH CEO in Zurich
March 4, 2025, at 1000hrs (Zurich local time), at the Mandarin Oriental Savoy Zurich, Swiss Confederation, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra met with Mr. Stefan P. Butz, CEO of DKSH Holding Ltd., who expressed appreciation for the Thai Government’s support over its investment in the country. The Prime Minister thanked DKSH for the investment in Thailand, whereas DKSH CEO expressed confidence in the Prime Minister’s vision and policies which are relevant with the current global development trends.
The Prime Minister affirmed Thailand’s commitment to promote ease of doing business, and consider reducing complicated procedure and limitation in applying for importation of drugs and medical equipment for sale. They also discussed promotion of green businesses through leveraging EVs and the Landbridge project for goods transport and logistics. DKSH also focuses on the manufacturing of semiconductor parts, in which the company has expertise, and has set up a factory in Thailand. DKSH CEO expressed appreciation over the Thai Government’s support in the investment in future industries.
The Prime Minister called on the company to support Thai farmers through purchasing Thai agro products. The two parties also discussed promotion of sustainable environment, which is a global agenda and an issue of mutual concern. The Prime Minister reaffirmed the Thai Government’s commitment to work together with DKSH on the issues of mutual interest.
