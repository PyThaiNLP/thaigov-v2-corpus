PM instructs Ministry of Tourism and Sports to look into extension of MotoGP World Championship contract
March 3, 2025, at 1010hrs, at the Command Building, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed about the MotoGP World Championship contract which will expire next year that she has instructed Ministry of Tourism and Sports to conduct a detailed feasibility study on the contract extension taking into account cost effectiveness.
According to the Prime Minister, projects that are beneficial to the wider public and the country will definitely be continued. However, projects that are not publicly beneficial and are shortfalls will have to be scrutinized and decided on what to do next. The Prime Minister also denied an alleged rumor that politics is behind the decision to not renewing the contract, and reiterated that national interest and benefit is of utmost priority
