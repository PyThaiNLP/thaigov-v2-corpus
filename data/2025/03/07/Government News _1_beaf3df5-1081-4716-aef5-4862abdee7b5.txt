PM visits Thai Pavilion at ITB Berlin 2025
March 6, 2025, at 1000hrs (Berlin local time), at Messe Berlin Exhibition Ground, Berlin, Federal Republic of Germany, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra attended the ITB Berlin 2025, the world’s largest travel trade show where tourism business operators and agencies across the globe gather to present, sell, and buy tourism products and services. Each year, representatives from more than 180 countries take part in the event to promote tourism of their own countries.
The Prime Minister visited the Thai Pavilion, located at Stand no.26 of Hall 26B, and met with the Thai private sector. Representing Thailand is 160 tourism business operators and entrepreneurs from 5 regions across the country who have contributed to promoting good image of Thai tourism through the brand “Amazing Thailand”. The year 2025 has also been announced as “Amazing Thailand Grand Tourism & Sports Year 2025” under the theme “Sustainable Thailand Soft Power”.
The Prime Minister visited “Amazing Thailand” booth, and chatted with European tourism business operators to exchange view on Thai tourism promotion. She also stopped by the “Muay Thai” booth and the Thai Soft Power booth to watch traditional boxing performance, and join a workshop on the making of bamboo garland respectively.
The Thai Pavilion features five zones: Business Meeting and Provincial Areas connect Thai sellers with international buyers, Partner Organization Areas promote sustainable tourism, and the Saneh Thai (Soft Power) Area highlights Thai culture through Food, Fashion, Fight (Muay Thai), Festivals, and Films. The Tourist City Area showcases hidden gem provinces, while the Sports Tourism Area focuses on Muay Thai and adventure tourism.
