PM expresses condolences to families of deceased and affected victims of Sai Buri bombing
March 9, 2025, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinwatra expressed condolences to families of the deceased and affected victims of the bombing and shooting incident in Pattani’s Sai Buri district on the night of March 8. They will be fully compensated and remedied according to related regulations.
The Prime Minister also ordered relevant agencies to conduct thorough investigation into the incident, and instructed security authorities to beef up security measures and night patrol as this is the month of Ramadan when Muslims observe fasting and have activities at night. She also wanted the number of security officers increased to enhance public safety and boost morale of the locals during this time of losses.
