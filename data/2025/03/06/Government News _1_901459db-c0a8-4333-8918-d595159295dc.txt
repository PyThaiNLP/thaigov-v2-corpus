PM meets with Bayer AG’s executives
March 5, 2025, at 1530hrs (Berlin local time), Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra visited Bayer AG, a global enterprise with core competencies in the Life Science fields of health care and agriculture.
The visit followed an earlier meeting between the Prime Minister and Mr. Stefan Oelrich, Board of Management and Head of the Pharmaceuticals Division of Bayer AG in Davos on January 21, 2025. The company has expertise in the fields of medicine and medical treatment, e.g., lung cancer, prostate cancer, heart disease, cell and gene therapy, Parkinson’s disease, and certain types of blindness, and has planned to introduce these medical innovations and technologies in Thailand. Mr. Oelrich also emphasized the importance of streamlining drug approval process to enable faster introduction of new treatments.
The Prime Minister has also met with Dr. Christian Rommel, Executive Vice. President and Global Head of Research &. Development who expressed interest in increasing cooperation with Thailand on agriculture. She also mentioned R&D cooperation and exchange which would be truly beneficial to the Thai people. They also constructively exchanged views on the company's research endeavors, especially in agriculture and life sciences, and on future cooperation between Thailand and Bayer, as the Prime Minister places importance on education and research, and advanced agriculture to uplift farmers' lives, and increase production and earnings in a sustainable manner. Thailand is ready to work together with Bayer in implementing the “Better Life Farmer” project.
