PM chairs Preparatory Committee for state visit of their Majesties to Bhutan
Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that on March 12, 2025, at 1400hrs, at Bhakdi Bodin Building, Government House, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra presided over the meeting of the Preparatory Committee for His Majesty King Rama X and Her Majesty the Queen's State Visit to the Kingdom of Bhutan. This will be the first state visit of His and Her Majesties upon an invitation of His Majesty Jigme Khesar Namgyel Wangchuck, King of Bhutan.
Participating in the meeting were representatives from the Royal Household Bureau and related agencies. The meeting’s main agenda is preparation for the state visit in various aspects to ensure orderliness befitting to the royal honor.
A committee has been appointed to prepare and facilitate the state visit of their Majesties, which is chaired by the Prime Minister. The members comprises Deputy Prime Ministers, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and representatives from relevant Government agencies.
The Prime Minister assigned Deputy Prime Minister Anutin Charnvirakul to represent the Thai Government as Minister-in-Attendance in the suite of honor for their Majesties’ state visit to Bhutan. Preparation will be in accordance with the protocol for state visit of the royal head of state and will be made in a royally befitting manner.
