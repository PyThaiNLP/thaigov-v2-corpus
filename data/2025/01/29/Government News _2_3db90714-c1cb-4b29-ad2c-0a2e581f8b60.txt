​Cabinet approves Equitable Education Fund’s 2026 budget expenditure plan
January 28, 2025, at 1230hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that the cabinet has made approval to the 2026 budget expenditure plan of the Equitable Education Fund (EEF), at the total of 7.987 billion Baht. The budget will be for the implementation of education and learning plans, teacher and student capacity building, and promotion of cooperation with network partners. The Prime Minister also affirmed that the Government would continue to support education sector, especially through the implementation of its policy on the “Zero Dropout”, which will subsidize and bring approximately 1.35 children and youths back into the formal educational system.
