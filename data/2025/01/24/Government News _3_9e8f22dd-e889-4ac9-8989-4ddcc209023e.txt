PM witnesses signing of Thailand-EFTA FTA
January 23, 2025, at 0900hrs (Davos local time) at the House of Switzerland, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra witnessed the signing of the Thailand-EFTA (European Free Trade Association) FTA. Signatories of the TH-EFTA FTA are:
(1) Mr. Guy Parmelin, Vice President and head of the Federal Department of Economic Affairs, Education and Research of the Swiss Federation, as host and spokesperson of the Free Trade Agreement negotiations between Thailand and the European Free Trade Association
(2) Mr. Pichai Naripthaphan, Minister of Commerce, Thailand
(3) Ms. Cecilie Myrseth, Minister of Trade and Industry, Kingdom of Norway, as chair of the European Free Trade Association (EFTA) in 2025
(4) Mr. Martin Eyjolfsson, Permanent Secretary of State, Iceland
(5) Ms. Dominique Hasler, Minister for Foreign Affairs, Education and Sport of Liechtenstein
(6) Mr. Kurt Jager, EFTA Secretary-General
The TH-EFTA FTA is the first FTA signed during this Government. In addition to strengthening economic relations between Thailand and EFTA member countries, the FTA will also promote closer cooperation in such areas as advanced technology and innovation, human capital development, SME promotion and sustainable development.
