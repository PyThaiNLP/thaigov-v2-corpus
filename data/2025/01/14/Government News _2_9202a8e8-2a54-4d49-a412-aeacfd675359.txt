Cabinet approves draft Entertainment Complex Business Act, B.E. …
January 13, 2025, at 1140hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that the cabinet approved in principle the draft Entertainment Complex Business Act, B.E. …, as proposed by Ministry of Finance. The draft bill will be forwarded to Office of Council of State for scrutiny.
Essence of the draft Entertainment Complex Business Act, B.E. … is to establish legal frameworks for the operation of integrated entertainment businesses, i.e., set up of the policy committee and Office of the Entertainment Complex Business regulatory commission; and criteria related to granting permission for entertainment complex businesses, among others.
The draft bill is aimed to further promote domestic tourism and investment, and to legalize underground gambling industry, which would create positive effects on the future society as a whole. The cabinet’s approval of the draft Entertainment Complex Business Act, B.E. … is part of the Government’s effort to promote sustainable tourism through man-made destinations. Ministry of Finance will later reveal relevant details of the matter.
The cabinet also approved the draft FTA between the Kingdom of Thailand and the European Free Trade Association (EFTA), which comprises Switzerland, Norway, Ireland, and Liechtenstein. The signing of the FTA will be carried out during the World Economic Forum (WEF) held in Davos, Switzerland next week.
