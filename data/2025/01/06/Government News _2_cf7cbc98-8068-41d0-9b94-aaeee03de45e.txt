Govt House opened for kids on Children’s Day, Jan 11, 2025
January 6, 2025, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that Office of Prime Minister’s Secretary General prepares to celebrate the 2025 Children’s Day on January 11, 2025. The Government House will be opened to welcome children from 0800hrs - 1500hrs. Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra will chair the opening of Children’s Day at the Government House and will join in various activities to celebrate the event with the participants.
The children will be given a chance to sit on the Prime Minister's chair and visit Thai Khu Fah Building, the Prime Minister's office, and participate in activities honoring His Majesty the King and the Thai monarchs at Bhakdi Bodin Building. They will also get to try a role of a news anchor in both Thai and English with the activity “Government Spokesperson...News Jiw (Mini News)" at Naree Smosorn Building. The Prime Minister will also have an online meeting via Zoom with youth representatives from regions across the country at the Inner Santi Maitri Building.
According to the Government Spokesperson, the Prime Minister has given the motto for 2025 Children’s Day as: “Every Opportunity is a Learning Experience, Be Ready to Adapt to the Future of Your Own Choice”.
Additionally, the Prime Minister’s Secretariat has launched an activity to give a chance for the children to send messages, e-cards, or clips to the Prime Minister via https://forms.gle/dY8RzbMrPexSsv8j9 from now until January 9, 2025. All messages sent will be forwarded to the Prime Minister and publicly posted.
