PM pays homage to Brahma Shrine and Govt House’s sacred shrines
for good fortune on occasion of Songkran Festival 2025
April 10, 2025, at 0830hrs, at the Government House, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra offered flower garlands to pay homage to Brahma Shrine on the Thai Khu Fah Building, and Govt House’s sacred shrines (the spirit house and the grandparents shrine) for good fortune as part of the Thai New Year (Songkran Festival 2025).
Participating in the ceremony were Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Phumtham Wechayachai, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior Anutin Charnvirakul, Minister Attached to the Prime Minister’s Office Chousak Sirinil, Minister Attached to the Prime Minister’s Office Jiraporn Sindhuprai, Prime Minister’s Secretary General Prommin Lertsuridej, President of Thailand Trade Representatives Nalinee Taveesin, and Government Spokesperson and the deputies.
