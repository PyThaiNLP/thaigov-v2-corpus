PM to pay official visit to Cambodia during April 23-24
April 22, 2025, at 1300hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra is scheduled to pay an official visit to the Kingdom of Cambodia during April 23-24, 2025.
The Prime Minister will leave the Royal Thai Air Force Airport (Wing 6) at approx. 0830hrs on April 23 to arrive in Phnom Penh at 0950hrs. She will, then, head to the Peace Palace to attend an official welcoming ceremony.
At 1050hrs, the Prime Minister will attend a plenary meeting with the Cambodian counterpart, Prime Minister Samdech Moha Borvor Thipadei Hun Manet, before witnessing the signing of important documents, and cochairing the launch of logo for the 75th Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between Thailand and Cambodia. The joint press conference and the luncheon hosted by the Cambodian Prime Minister in honor of the Thai Prime Minister will, then, follow.
In the afternoon of April 23, the Prime Minister is scheduled to travel to the Independence Monument to lay the wreath at the Statue of King Father Norodom Sihanouk before paying a courtesy call on Samdech Rathsaphea Thipadei Khuon Sudary, President of the National Assembly of Cambodia at 1500hrs, and on President of the Senate and head of the Supreme Privy Council of Cambodia His Excellency Samdech Akka Moha Sena Padei Techo Hun Sen at 1600hrs.
On April 24, 2025, the Prime Minister will be granted an audience with His Majesty King Narodom Sihamoni before leaving Cambodia to arrive back in Thailand at approx. 1620hrs.
