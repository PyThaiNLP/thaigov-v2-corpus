Malaysian PM to pay working visit to Thailand on April 17
April 16, 2025, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra will welcome the working visit of His Excellency Dato' Seri Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister of Malaysia, on April 17, 2025 at the Government House.
This visit aims to further enhance bilateral cooperation between Thailand and Malaysia, following the Prime Minister of Thailand's Official Visit to Malaysia on December 16, 2024 and the recent telephone conversation between the two Prime Ministers on April 5, 2025.
Both Prime Ministers will focus on key areas of bilateral cooperation, including infrastructure development, enhancing connectivity, trade, tourism, security, advancing peace and development in border areas. Both leaders are also expected to discuss cooperation between Thailand and Malaysia, as current ASEAN Chair, to further deepen regional integration.
