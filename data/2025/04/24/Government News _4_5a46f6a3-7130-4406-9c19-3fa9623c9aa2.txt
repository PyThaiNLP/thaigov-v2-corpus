PM granted audience with H.M. King Norodom Sihamoni of Cambodia
Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that on April 24, 2025, at 1100hrs, at the Royal Palace, Kingdom of Cambodia, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra was granted an audience with His Majesty Preah Bat Samdech Preah Boromneath Norodom Sihamoni on occasion of her official visit to the country.
Relations and cooperation, especially people-to-people relations, are the foundation of long-standing friendship between Thailand and Cambodia. The Thai Government attaches importance to promoting closer interactions between people of the two countries through the implementation of various development cooperation projects in Cambodia, i.e., projects under the Royal Initiative of Her Royal Highness Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn, which serve as the bridges to strengthen relations between people of the two countries.
