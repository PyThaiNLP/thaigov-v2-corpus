PM: Govt continues to implement both short-term, medium-term, and long-term economic stimulus policies
April 29, 2025, at 1130hrs, at Mekong Subregion Conference Hall, Nakhon Phanom University, following the cabinet retreat for the Upper Northeastern Provincial Cluster 2 (Nakhon Phanom, Sakon Nakhon, and Mukdaharn), Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed about the World Bank’s adjustment of Thailand’s GDP growth forecast from 2.9% to 1.6%.
According to the Prime Minister, the Government has continually implemented both short-term, medium-term, and long-term policies in attempt to boost the nation’s economy. The digital wallet scheme has been put in place as short-term economic stimulus project, whereas the Budget Bureau is instructed to accelerate budget allocation for public sector investment. The Prime Minister added that inward foreign investments have been promoted, and that with the Government’s continuous effort, Thailand records the highest foreign direct investment (FDI) in history. Amidst the global economic recession, the Government strives to do its best to push forward plans and projects that would benefit the country into the future, such as the establishment of an entertainment complex
