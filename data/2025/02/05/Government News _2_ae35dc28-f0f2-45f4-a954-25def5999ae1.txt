PM to discuss anti-online fraud and scam with China
February 4, 2025, at 1200hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed about her official visit to People’s Republic of China on February 5-8, 2025, that the issue of online fraud and scam will be focused in her discussion with the Chinese counterpart. As the problem has become global, not only with China, but Thailand will seek cooperation with other countries and opt for adoption of various technologies to counter online scam. China's Assistant Minister of Public Security Liu Zhongyi, who has recently visited Thailand, has also met with Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Phumtham Wechayachai to discuss the matter.
The Prime Minister affirmed that tangible progress regarding the fight against online fraud and scam will be made during her official visit to China as both the countries have mutually affected by the problem.
