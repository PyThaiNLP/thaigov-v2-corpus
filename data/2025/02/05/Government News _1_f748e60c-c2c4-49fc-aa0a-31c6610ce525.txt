PM to pay official visit to China during Feb 5-8, 2025
February 4, 2025, at 1200hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed about her official visit to Beijing, People’s Republic of China, which is scheduled on February 5-8, 2025, that she will also attend the opening ceremony of the 9th Asian Winter Games Harbin (2025), as invited by Chinese Prime Minister Li Qiang. Her official visit will mark the beginning of a celebration for the Golden Jubilee for Thailand - China Friendship on occasion of the 50th anniversary of diplomatic relations between the two countries this year.
During her official visit to China, the Prime Minister will meet with Thai and Chinese private sector to discuss and exchange views on various issues, including cultural exchanges, endorsement of the “One country, two systems” principle, promotion of semiconductor industry supply chain, manufacturing and development of electric vehicle batteries, and accelerating construction of the Thai-Chinese high-speed railway (both Phase 1 and Phase 2) to promote connectivity between Thailand, Laos and China.
Another issue to be discussed during the Prime Minister’s official visit to China is education and R&D cooperation, especially hi-tech cooperation to promote Thailand’s human resource development. Several MoUs will also be signed to enhance relations and cooperation in all dimensions.
