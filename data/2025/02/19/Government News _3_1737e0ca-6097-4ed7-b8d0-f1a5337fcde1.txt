PM orders acceleration of road alignment project linking CIQ Sadao and Malaysia’s CIQS Bukit Kayu Hitam
February 18, 2025, at 1400hrs, at Sadao Customs Checkpoint, Sadao district, Songkhla, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and relevant ministers followed up on the progress of road construction linking new Sadao Customs, Immigration and Quarantine (CIQ) and Bukit Kayu Hitam Immigration, Customs, Quarantine and Security (CIQS) in Malaysia.
According to the report by Deputy Director-General of the Customs Department Yuthana Poolpipat, the construction was completed in 2019, but it is yet to be open for use because of some technical problems and infrastructural differences between the two countries. The Prime Minister has ordered concerned agencies to expedite construction of the 300-meter connecting section under the budget framework of 28 million Baht to complete by the first quarter of this year.
On November 6, 2024, Songkhla Provincial Administrative Office submitted a budget proposal to the Department of Local Administration for the construction of 2 land routes for the total amount of 98 million Baht. Details are as follows:
2nd Phase of road construction project to accommodate the development of Songkhla’s Sadao Special Economic Zone (road alignment project linking new Sadao Customs, Immigration and Quarantine (CIQ) and Bukit Kayu Hitam Immigration, Customs, Quarantine and Security (CIQS) in Malaysia): 6 lanes, 600 meters long, 28 million Baht in budget, and construction period of 6 months
Road construction project connecting Highway No. 4 with Phru Tiew Highway – 2nd CIQ Sadao: 1,150 meters long, 70 million Baht in budget, and construction period of 12 months
Total trade value through CIQ Sadao in fiscal year 2024 was at 450,189 million Baht (+17,192 million Baht or +8%). For the first quarter of fiscal year 2025 (October - December 2024), total trade value amounted to 114,086 million Baht, comprising the import value of 51,288 million Baht and the export value of 62,799 million Baht, +2,044 million Baht (1.8%)QoQ.
The Prime Minister commended the accomplishment of the Customs Department, and reemphasized acceleration of the road alignment project in order to maximize services of the new CIQ Sadao. She, then, inspected the construction site before returning to Bangkok.
