PM: Govt ready to listen to public opinion on Landbridge project
February 18, 20205, at 1140hrs, at the 60th Anniversary of His Majesty the King’s Accession to the Throne International Convention Center, Prince of Songkla University, Hat Yai, Songkhla, following the cabinet retreat, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra stated about opposition of some against the Landbridge project that relevant ministers have been assigned to clarify the project’s goal and what the country would benefit from implementing the project. Workshops will also be held to ensure better public understanding on the project. The Prime Minister understood that changes require adaptation and understanding, and emphasized that public voices are very important.     The Government is ready to listen to opinions and suggestions of all sides and stakeholders.
