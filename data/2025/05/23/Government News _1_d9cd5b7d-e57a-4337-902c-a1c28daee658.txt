Number of tourists increases during campaign “Amazing Thailand 2025”
Number of tourists increases during campaign “Amazing Thailand 2025”
May 22, 2025, Deputy Government Spokesperson Sasikarn Watthanachan disclosed the cumulative number of incoming tourists in 2025, during the campaign “Amazing Thailand 2025”, which amounted to over 13 million, generating over 630 billion Baht.
From January 1 to May 18, 2025, the number of South Korean tourists traveling to Thailand increased by 10%, now ranking 4th greatest number of nationals visiting the country (up from 8th). This week alone saw 464,531 foreign tourists entering Thailand, with the top 5 being tourists from Malaysia (68,738), China (62,569), India (50,571), South Korea (18,213), and Laos (17,034). In addition to the increase of South Korean tourists, those from China and India also increased by 2.18% and 1.34%, respectively.
With regard to the measures to attract Chinese tourists throughout the year, Tourism Authority of Thailand is preparing to organize an event “Sawasdee Ni Hao” to celebrate the 50th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Thailand and China from May 28 to June 1, 2025, which will bring 300 Chinese tour operators and 200 KOLs/Influencers from potential areas in China’s 25-30 provinces to travel to Thailand for business matching with 500 Thai operators.
The Deputy Government Spokesperson reaffirmed Thailand’s readiness to become the world’s popular tourist destination. The Thai Government also strives to leverage tourism as the sustainable economic driving force to generate and spread income to communities across the country.
