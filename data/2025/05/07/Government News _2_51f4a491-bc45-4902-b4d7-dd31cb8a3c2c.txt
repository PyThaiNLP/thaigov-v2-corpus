​PM: Negotiations with US on trade tariffs to take place within 90-day timeframe
​PM: Negotiations with US on trade tariffs to take place within 90-day timeframe
May 6, 2025, at 1135hrs, at the Command Building I, Government House, following the weekly cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed that the negotiations with the US on trade tariffs would take place within the 90-day timeframe. As of now, concerned authorities, led by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Finance Pichai Chunhavajira, are preparing and working on related details for the negotiations.
With regard to the secret deal in the US tariff talks, the Prime Minister stated that some elements are disclosable, while others, for the best interest of the country, will later be shared with the public in a timely manner. Concerned authorities have liaised with the US counterpart on certain details where Thailand is required to proceed in order to ensure inclusivity of the negotiations.
