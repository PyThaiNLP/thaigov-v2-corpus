PM’s statement on the 2025 Farmers’ Day
May 8, 2025, at 0800hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub revealed Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra’s statement on occasion of the 2025 Farmers’ Day, gist of which is as follows:
The Prime Minister conveyed her well wishes to all the Thai farmers on Farmers' Day, May 8, 2025. The farmers have been playing an essential role in advancing agricultural sector, which is the country’s key manufacturing sector that interconnects both economic, social, and environmental developments.
The Government strives to promote modern agriculture through the "market-driven, innovation-enhanced, income-boosting" approach to empower the farmers and enhance their capacity. Agricultural technologies have been adopted for career development in agriculture, fisheries, livestock, and other related professions. Strengths and distinctiveness of Thai agro products are also highlighted through the “Thai Kitchen to the World” scheme to demonstrate the country’s food security and stabilize Thailand’s agricultural sector as world’s agricultural hub.
The Government has also placed importance on leveraging agriculture to promote environmental conservation through implementing the BCG and Carbon Credit programs for safe production of agro products; recycling agricultural waste to address PM2.5 particulates; and empowering the Thai farmers to elevate their quality of life and improve value added of agro products.
The Prime Minister thanked Thai farmers for being a vital component in the development and sustainability promotion of Thailand’s agricultural sector. On occasion of the 2025 Farmers’ Day, she wished the Thai farmers happiness, prosperity, and strong physical and mental health. May they succeed in all the endeavors.
