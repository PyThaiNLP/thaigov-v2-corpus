PM commences official visit in Vietnam
May 15, 2025, at 1200hrs (local time), Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra and the delegation, comprising Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Transport Suriya Jungrungreangkit, Minister o Foreign Affairs Maris Sangiampongsa, Minister of Tourism and Sports Sorawong Thienthong, Deputy Minister of Finance Julapun Amornvivat, Prime Minister’s Secretary General Prommin Lertsuridej, and Thai Trade Representative Chai Wacharonke, arrived at Noi Bai International Airport, Hanoi, Socialist Republic of Vietnam for his official visit to the country. She was welcomed by Vietnamese Minister of Culture, Sports, and Tourism Nguyen Van Hung as Minister-in-Attendance, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Nguyen Manh Coung, and Vietnamese Ambassador to Thailand Pham Vietnam Hung. Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed detail of the Prime Minister’s official visit to Vietnam as follows:
Upon arrival, the Prime Minister met with representatives of the Thai Chamber of Commerce and Industry in Vietnam (ThaiCham) at Melia Hanoi Hotel. She also met with executives of two Vietnam’s leading firms, i.e., SOVICO Group (Vietjet Air), and FPT Corporation, to discuss their business and investment expansion in Thailand.
In the afternoon, the Prime Minister is scheduled to pay a courtesy call on H.E. Mr. Tran Thanh Man, Chairman of the National Assembly of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, before attending dinner reception in the evening at the Government House, to be hosted by Prime Minister Pham Minh Chinh in honor of the Prime Minister.
