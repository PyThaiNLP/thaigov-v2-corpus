​PM talks with Cambodian PM on border tension in Ubon Ratchathani
May 28, 2025, at 1740hrs, at the Parliament House, Bangkok, following her attendance at the 1st Session of the 26th House of Representatives, the 2nd Year (Extraordinary Session) for the deliberation of the draft Expenditure Budget Act for fiscal 2026 (1st day), Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed about border tension and a gunfight between Thai and Cambodian soldiers at the area of Chong Bok border pass in Ubon Ratchathani province that she was reported and had a talk with the Cambodian Prime Minister on the situation. Ministry of Defense, through Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Thai Army, will also hold talk with the Cambodian counterpart to settle the matter the soonest. She affirmed that her relations with the Cambodian Prime Minister remain cordial.
