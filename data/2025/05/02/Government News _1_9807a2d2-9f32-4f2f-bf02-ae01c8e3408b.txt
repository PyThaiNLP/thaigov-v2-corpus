​Cabinet approves issuance of charity lottery tickets in support of ODOS programs
April 29, 2025, at 1130hrs, at Mekong Subregion Conference Hall, Nakhon Phanom University, following the cabinet retreat for the Upper Northeastern Provincial Cluster 2 (Nakhon Phanom, Sakon Nakhon, and Mukdaharn), Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra disclosed about a cabinet’s resolution on issuance of charity lottery tickets in support of the 1st phase of the Outstanding Development Opportunity Scholarship (ODOS) initiative, which consists of 7 programs, worth 5,308.14 million Baht, as follows:
Short-term scholarship and technology training program abroad for youths, each representing 878 districts nationwide and 50 districts of Bangkok
Scholarships for studying abroad: 60 for vocational-level study and 140 for bachelor's degree programs, totaling 200 scholarships
60 scholarships for undergraduate degrees in the United States in science and technology programs
50 scholarships for undergraduate degrees in the United Kingdom in science and technology (STEM) programs: 2 batches, 25 scholarships per batch
60 scholarships for vocational study and 30 bachelor's degree programs in Australia
2,200 scholarships for undergraduate degrees in science and technology (STEM) programs in Thailand: 6 batches (700 recipients to be subsidized by the charity lottery program and 1,100 scholarship recipients from the Government’s lottery program, and 400 to be funded by educational institutions)
4,800 scholarships for high school and vocational study in the country in science and technology (STEM): 4 batches, 1,200 people per batch
