Grand Reception hosted by Govt in honor of H.M. King on Coronation Day
May 4, 2025, at 1900hrs, Government Spokesperson Jirayu Houngsub disclosed that Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra presided over the Grand Reception hosted by the Government in honor of His Majesty the King on occasion of the Coronation Day.
Upon arrival at the Outer Santi Maitri Building, the Prime Minister and spouse bowed in front of His Majesty’s portrait, presented candle and incense stick offerings, and bowed again before extending her well wishes to His Majesty. After the laudatory wishes, the Prime Minister led the participants to offer the toast, followed by the royal anthem “Sansoen Phra Barami” played by the concert band. All the participants, then, chanted “Long Live the King” three times. The royal anthem “Sadudee Chom Racha (Hymn to the King) was played to end the ceremony.
On this occasion, the Government invited the Privy Councilors, former prime ministers, heads of organizations under the Constitution, members of the Parliament and the Senate, ministry permanent secretaries and equivalent levels of high-ranking officials from the civil services, military, police, civil servants, political officers attached to Office of the Prime Minister, diplomatic corps, honorary consuls, representatives of international organizations, presidents of the Thai Chamber of Commerce, Federation of Thai Industries, and the Thai Bankers Association, and president of the Foreign Correspondents' Association of Thailand, along with their spouses, to attend the Grand Reception.
