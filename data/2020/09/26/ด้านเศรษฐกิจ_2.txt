EXIM Thailand Launches “EXIM Amazing M Credit” Enhancing Liquidity of Medium Entrepreneurs
Export-Import Bank of Thailand (EXIM Thailand) recently launched EXIM Amazing M Credit, a revolving credit facility to enhance liquidity of medium entrepreneurs with sales income ranging from 300-500 million baht with a maximum credit line of 80 million baht per entrepreneur and a minimum interest rate of 3.75% per annum (prime rate -2.00% per annum), in conjunction with a foreign exchange forward contract facility of up to 3 times the credit facility. Approval period from today until July 31, 2021.
For further information, please contact Sustainable Development and Corporate Communication Department
